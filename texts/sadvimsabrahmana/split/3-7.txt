etayaivābhicarant stuvīta || 1 || 
vajro vai trivṛt | vajraḥ pañcadaśaḥ | vajraḥ triṇavaḥ | yat trivṛtpañcadaśatriṇavaiḥ saptadaśaṃ vidadhāti vajram eva tat samyak saṃdadhāti || 2 || 
evam iva vai vajraḥ sādhuḥ yad ārambhaṇato 'ṇīyān praharaṇataḥ sthavīyān || 3 || 
tena pāpmānaṃ bhrātṛvyaṃ stṛṇute | vasīyān ātmanā bhavati | ya etayā stute || 4 ||
