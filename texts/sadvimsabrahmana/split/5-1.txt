prajāpatis tapo 'tapyata | tasya ha vai tapyamānasya manaḥ prājāyata devān sṛjeyam iti | ta ime devā asṛjyanta | divā devān asṛjata | naktam asurān | yad divā devān asṛjata tad devānāṃ devatvam | yad asūryaṃ tad asurāṇām asuratvam | yat pītatvaṃ tat pitṝṇāṃ pitṛtvam || 1 || 
devā vai svargakāmās tapo 'tapyanta | teṣāṃ tapyamānānāṃ raso 'jāyata | pṛthivy antarikṣaṃ dyaur iti | te 'bhyatapan | teṣāṃ tapyamānānāṃ raso 'jāyata | ṛgvedaḥ pṛthivyā yajurvedo 'ntarikṣāt sāmavedo 'muṣmāt | te 'bhyatapan | teṣāṃ tapyamānānāṃ raso 'jāyata | ṛgvedād gārhapatyo yajurvedād dakṣiṇāgniḥ sāmavedād āhavanīyaḥ | te 'bhyatapan | teṣāṃ tapyamānānāṃ puruṣo 'jāyata | sahasraśīrṣaḥ sahasrākṣaḥ sahasrapāt || 2 || 
te devāḥ prajāpatim upādhāvan vedaśarīrair vā idam amṛtaśarīraṃ na ha vā idaṃ mṛtyoḥ samāpsyata iti te 'bruvan ko nāmāsī (se?) ti || 3 || 
sa hovāca yajño nāmeti || 4 || 
teṣāṃ prajāpatiḥ sadyoyajñasaṃsthām upaiti | sadyo ha vā eṣa yajñaḥ saṃsthām upaiti || 5 || 
yad gārhapatyaṃ prāduṣkaroti sā dīkṣaṇīyā | yad dakṣiṇāgniṃ cāhavanīyaṃ ca sā prāyaṇīyā | yat samidho 'bhyādadhāti tā upasadaḥ || 6 || 
atha yasyājyam utpūtaṃ skandati | sā skannā nāma āhutiḥ | tato vai yajamānaḥ pramāyur bhavati | varo deyaḥ | saiva tasya prāyaścittiḥ || 7 || 
atha yasyājyam anutpūtaṃ skandaty asau vā askannā nāma āhutiḥ | tato vai yajamānasya cittaṃ pramāyur bhavati | citraṃ deyaṃ saiva tasya prāyaścittiḥ || 8 || 
yad gārhapatye juhoti tat prātaḥsavanam | yad dakṣiṇāgnau juhoti tan mādhyaṃdinaṃ savanam | yad āhavanīye juhoti tat tṛtīyasavanam || 9 || 
yan mārjayate so 'syāvabhṛthaḥ || 10 || 
yad annā dadāti tena udayanīyasyodavasānīyasya samāptyai || 11 || 
atha yasyāgnir mathyamāno na jāyetānyam āhṛtyāsminn avakāśe juhuyād brāhmaṇasya vā haste 'jasya vā karṇe kuśastambe vāpsu vā juhuyāt || 12 || 
anyaiḥ śatahutān homān ekaḥ śiṣyahuto varam | śiṣyaiḥ śatahutān homān ekaḥ putrahuto varam || putraiḥ śatahutān homān eko hy ātmahuto varam || 13 || 
svayaṃ hotā svayaṃ dohī svayam evopatiṣṭhetāgnihotram || 14 || 
haumyaśeṣaṃ dakṣiṇā || 15 || 
sarvair ha vā etasya yajñakratubhir iṣṭaṃ bhavati | ya evaṃ vidvān agnihotraṃ juhoti || 16 ||
