sa tṛtīyasavane savanamukhīyeṣv āhṛteṣūpahavam icchate || 1 || 
prāṇo me hotā sa mopahvayatāṃ hotar upa māyahvayasvety uccaiḥ | apāno me 'dhvaryuḥ sa mopahvayatām adhvarya upa māhvayasvety uccaiḥ | vyāno me brahmā sa mopahvayatāṃ brahmann upa māhvayasvety uccaiḥ | samāno ma udgātā sa mopahvayatām udgātar upa māhvayasvety uccaiḥ || 2 || 
yo 'yam antaḥpuruṣa ākāśaḥ sa me sadasyaḥ sa mopahvayatāṃ sadasyopa māhvayasvety uccaiḥ | yā imā antaḥpuruṣa āpas te me hotrāśaṃsinas te mopahvayantāṃ hotrāśaṃsina upa māhvayadhvam ity uccaiḥ | lomāni me camasādhvaryavas te mopahvayantāṃ camasādhvaryava upa māhvayadhvam ity uccaiḥ || 3 || 
tā vā etā devatā ṛtvijām eva vāgbhir upahvayante || 4 || 
sa upahūto bhakṣayati || 5 || 
sa udāno yajamāno 'tho yatraitāsāṃ devatānāṃ lokas tad upahūto bhavati || 6 || 
sarveṣāṃ vai vaṣaṭkartā camasaṃ bhakṣayet || 7 || 
devānāṃ vā etad yajñasya mukhaṃ yad udgātṛcamasam tasmād udgātṛcamasaṃ nānyo bhakṣayet || 8 || 
evaṃ viduṣo ha vai yajño na vyathate | evaṃ viduṣo ha vai yajamānasya svargyaṃ putryaṃ paśavyam iṣṭaṃ bhavati | evaṃ vidvān ha ve yajamāno dviṣantaṃ bhrātṛvyam abhibhavati | atha vā ato yajñe hīnaṃ kuryāt || 9 ||
