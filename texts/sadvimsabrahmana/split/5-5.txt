brahmavādino vadanti | kasmād brāhmaṇaḥ sāyam āsīnaḥ saṃdhyām upāste kasmāt prātas tiṣṭhan kā ca saṃdhyā kaś ca saṃdhyāyāḥ kālaḥ kiṃ ca saṃdhyāyāḥ saṃdhyātvam || 1 || 
devāś cāsurāś cāspardhanta | te 'surā ādityam abhidravan | sa ādityo bibhet | tasya hṛdayaṃ kūrmarūpeṇātiṣṭhat || 2 || 
sa prajāpatim upādhāvat | tasya prajāpatir etad bheṣajam apaśyat | ṛtaṃ ca satyaṃ ca brahma coṃkāraṃ ca tripadāṃ ca gāyatrī brahmaṇo mukham apaśyat || 3 || 
tasmād brāhmaṇo 'horātrasya saṃyoge saṃdhyām upāste | sajyotiṣyā jyotiṣo darśanāt | so 'syāḥ kālaḥ | sā saṃdhyā | tat saṃdhyāyāḥ saṃdhyātvam | yat sāyam āsīnaḥ saṃdhyām upāste tayā vīrasthānaṃ jayati | atha yad apaḥ prayuṅkte tā vipruṣo vajrībhavanti | tā vipruṣo vajrībhūtvāsurān apāghnanti | tato devā abhavan | parāsurā bhavanti | ātmanā parāsya bhrātṛvyo bhavati | ya evaṃ veda | yata sāyaṃ prātaś ca saṃdhyām upāste tayā vīrasthānaṃ sthānaṃ ca saṃtatam avicchinnaṃ bhavati | ya evaṃ veda || 4 ||
