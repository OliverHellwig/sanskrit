mādhyaṃdine savane savanamukhīyeṣv āhṛteṣūpahavam icchate || 1 || 
vāṅ me hotā sa mopahvayatāṃ hotar upa mā hvayasvety uccaiḥ | cakṣur me 'dhvaryuḥ sa mopahvayatām adhvarya upa mā hvayasvety uccaiḥ | mano me brahmā sa mopahvayatāṃ brahmann upa mā hvayasvety uccaiḥ | śrotraṃ ma udgātā sa mopahvayatām udgātar upa mā hvayasvety uccaiḥ || 2 || 
yo 'yam antaś cakṣuṣyākāśaḥ sa me sadasyaḥ | sa mopahvayatāṃ sadasyopa mā hvayasvety uccair yā imā antaś cakṣuṣy āpas te me hotrāśaṃsinas te mopahvayantāṃ hotrāśaṃsina upa mā hvayadhvam ity uccaiḥ aṅgāni me camasādhvaryavas te mopahvayantāṃ camasādhvaryava upa mā hvayadhvam ity uccaiḥ || 3 || 
tā vā etā devatā ṛtvijām eva vāgbhir upahvayante || 4 || 
sa upahūto bhakṣayati || 5 || 
apāno yajamāno 'tho yatraitāsāṃ devatānāṃ lokas tad upahūto bhavati || 6 ||
