mādhyaṃdine savane savanamukhīyeṣv āhṛteṣūpahavam icchate || 1 || 
vāṅ me hotā sa mopahvayatāṃ hotar upa māhvayasvety uccaiḥ | cakṣur me 'dhvaryuḥ sa mopahvayatām adhvarya upa māhayasvety uccaiḥ | mano me brahmā sa mopahvayatāṃ brahmann upa māhvayasvety uccaiḥ | śrotraṃ ma udgātā sa mopahvayatām udgātar upa māhvayasvety uccaiḥ || 2 || 
yo 'yam antaś cakṣuṣyākāśaḥ sa me sadasyaḥ | sa mopahvayatāṃ sadasyo māhvayasvety uccair yā imā antaś cakṣuṣy āpas te me hotrāśaṃsinas te mopahvayantāṃ hotrāśaṃsina upa māhvayadhyam ity uccaiḥ aṅgāni me camasādhvaryavas te mopahvayantāṃ camasādhvaryava upa māhvayadhvam ity uccaiḥ || 3 || 
tā vā etā devatā ṛtvijām eva vāgbhir upayante || 4 || 
sa upahūto bhakṣayati || 5 || 
apāno yajamāno 'tho yatraitāsāṃ devatānāṃ lokas tad upahūto bhavati || 6 ||
