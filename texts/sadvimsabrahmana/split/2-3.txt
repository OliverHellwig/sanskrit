devāś ca vā asurāś caiṣu lokeṣv aspardhanta | te devāḥ prajāpatim upādhāvan || 1 || 
tebhya etān dhuraḥ prāṇān prāyacchat | manaḥ prathamam atha prāṇam atha cakṣur atha śrotram atha vācam || 2 || 
tābhyaḥ pañcabhyo dhūrbhyaḥ puruṣaṃ ca paśuṃś ca niramimīta || 3 || 
tena puruṣeṇāsurān adhūrvan | yad adhūrvaṃs tad dhurāṃ dhūstvam | dhūrvati pāpmānaṃ bhrātṛvyaṃ ya evaṃ veda || 4 || 
yo vai dhurāṃ dhūstvaṃ veda dhurā dhurā bhrātṛvyād vasīyān bhavati | etad vai dhurāṃ dhūstvaṃ yan nānāvīryā nānārūpā nānāchandasyā nānādevatyāḥ samānaṃ hiṃkāram abhisaṃpadyante | etad vai dhurāṃ dhūstvam | dhurā dhurā bhrātṛvyād vasīyān bhavati | ya evaṃ veda || 5 || 
yo vai dhūrṣu mahāvrataṃ veda sarvā asmin puṇyā vāco vadanti || 6 || 
śiro gāyatry uras triṣṭup madhyaṃ jagatī pādāv anuṣṭup sarvā asmin puṇyā vāco vadanti ya evaṃ veda || 7 || 
yo vā evaṃ dhuro vidvān athāsāṃ vrataṃ caraty āgamiṣyato 'sya pūrvedyuḥ puṇyā kīrtir āgacchati || 8 || 
surabhir eva gandho gāyatryā vratam | darśanīyaṃ triṣṭubhaḥ | śravaṇīyaṃ jagatyāḥ | yad eva vācā puṇyaṃ vadati tad anuṣṭubhaḥ | tad u sarvāsāṃ vratam || 9 || 
tad u vidvāṃsam āhur ati no 'vādīr iti tad anādṛtyam || 10 || 
yasya vai dhuro vigītās tasya saṃgītāḥ || 11 || 
yasya vā etā bahiṣpavamāne vigīyāntarājyeṣu saṃgāyanti tasya vai dhuro vigītās tasya saṃgītāḥ || 12 || 
yaḥ kāmayetaikadhā yajamānaṃ yaśa ṛcched yathādiṣṭaṃ prajāḥ syur iti hotur ājye gāyet | ekadhā yajamānaṃ yaśa ṛcchati yathādiṣṭaṃ prajā bhavanti || 13 || 
yaḥ kāmayeta kalperan prajā yathādiṣṭaṃ yajamānaḥ syād iti yathājyaṃ gāyet | kalpante prajā yathādiṣṭaṃ yajamāno bhavati || 14 || 
yo vā evaṃ dhuro vedānapajayyam ātmane ca yajamānāya ca lokaṃ jayaty ati yajamānam ātmānaṃ mṛtyuṃ paraṃ svarga lokaṃ harati || 15 ||
