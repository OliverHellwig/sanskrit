sa pratīcīṃ diśam anvāvartate || 1 ||
atha yadāsya kṣetragṛhasaṃstheṣu dhānyeṣv ītayaḥ prādurbhavanti | ītayo vānekavidhāḥ ākhupataṅgapipīlikāmadhvakabhaumakaśukaśarabhakasaukṣmaka ity evamādīni | tāny etāni sarvāṇi varuṇadevatyāny adbhutāni prāyaścittāni bhavanti || 2 || 
ghṛtavatī iti sthālīpākaṃ hutvā pañcabhir ājyāhutibhir abhijuhoti varuṇāya svāhā apāṃ pataye svāhā pāśapāṇaye svāhā īśvarāya svāhā sarvapāpaśamanāya svāhā iti vyāhṛtibhir hutvātha sāma gāyet || 3 ||
