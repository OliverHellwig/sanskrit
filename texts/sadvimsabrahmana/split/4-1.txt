svarāntaḥ prathamas trirātro gāyatrī || 1 || 
prāṇo vai gāyatrī | prajāpatiḥ svaraḥ | prajāpatim evāpnoti || 2 || 
nidhanānto dvitīyas triṣṭup || 3 || 
vīryaṃ vai triṣṭup | traiṣṭubhaḥ puruṣaḥ [nidhanam] | puruṣam evāpnoti || 4 || 
iḍāntas tṛtīyo jagatī || 5 || 
paśavo vā iḍā | paśavo jagatī | jāgatāḥ paśavaḥ | paśūn evāpnoti || 6 || 
prasṛtacchandāḥ prathamas trirātraḥ | tena so 'yātayāmā || 7 || 
adhottarasya chandāṃsi vyūhanti | triṣṭubhaḥ pratipado bhavanti | triṣṭubhāṃ loke jagatyo jagatīnāṃ gāyatryas tena so 'yātayāmā || 8 || 
athottamasya chandāṃsi vy evohanti | jagatyaḥ pratipado bhavanti | jagatīnāṃ loke triṣṭubhaḥ | triṣṭubhāṃ gāyatryaḥ | teno eva so 'yātayāmā || 9 || 
api vā etā anyonyasyai lokam abhyadhyāyan | kāmam evainā gamayati | āpnoti taṃ kāmaṃ yasmai kāmāyaiṣa āhriyate || 10 || 
tasmād yujau na sadṛg vahatas tasmād vipariṇītau vahīyāṃsau bhavatas tasmād ubhayatodantaḥ syadante || 11 || 
tṛce vā anya ṛṣayo virājam apaśyan | vasiṣṭhaś ca catuṛce || 12 || 
na tena sa caturṛcaḥ | yat tṛce 'nyat sāma bhavati ekarce 'nyad yad vāva | catasṛṣv ekaṃ sāma sa eva catuṛcaḥ || 13 || 
sāma bhūyāṃsaṃ poṣaṃ puṣyati | tam anu poṣaṃ poṣuko bhavati || 14 || 
etaddha smāha muñjaḥ sāmaśravasaḥ etaṃ ha vai vayaṃ sāmnaḥ poṣaṃ vidmeti tasmād vayam apratigṛhṇanto na sahasrapoṣāc cyavāmahā iti || 15 || 
tad u ha smāha vāsiṣṭhaś caikitāneyaḥ | sāma ha vai nūnaṃ bhūyāṃsaṃ poṣaṃ puṣyati | tam anu poṣaṃ poṣako bhavati | vidhavāyā iva tve(ivetye?)vaitajjanma yad asāmnī caturthī | evaṃ ha vā ete vidur iti | tasmād eteṣāṃ yuvānaḥ śrotriyāḥ purāyuṣaḥ pramāyukā bhavanti | yuvatayo jāyā vidhavā bhavanti iti | yat tṛce 'nyat sāma bhavaty ekarce 'nyad yad vāva catasṛṣv ekaṃ sāma sa eva catuṛca iti || 16 || 
paśur atiricyate sa aindrāgna ālambhyaḥ || 17 || 
indrāgnī vai devānām ojiṣṭhau tā u ayātayāmānau tāv eva tad yunakti svargasya lokasya samaṣṭayai || 18 || 
sauryaṃ brahmavarcasakāma ālabheta || 19 || 
etasyāṃ vā ekādaśinyām ādityāḥ sūryam ajanayan | sa tejo brahmavarcasam avarundhate | ojasvī brahmavarcasī bhavati | ya evaṃ veda || 20 || 
atho khalv āhur āgneya evālambhya iti | agnir vai sarvā devatās tena na devatānāṃ kāṃcanāntareti || 21 ||
