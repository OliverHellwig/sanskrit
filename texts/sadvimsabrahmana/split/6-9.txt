sa divam anvāvartate || 1 || 
atha yadāsya tārāvarṣāṇi colkāḥ patanti, nipatanti, dhūmāyanti diśo dahyanti ketavaś cottiṣṭhanti, gavāṃ śṛṅgeṣu dhūmo jāyate, gavāṃ staneṣu rudhiraṃ sravati, atyarthaṃ himaṃ nipatati ity evamādīni tāny etāni sarvāṇi somadevatyāny adbhutāni prāyaścittāni bhavanti || 2 || 
somaṃ rājānaṃ varuṇam iti sthālīpākaṃ hutvā pañcabhir ājyāhutibhir abhijuhoti | somāya svāhā, nakṣatrādhipataye svāhā, śītapāṇaye svāhā, īśvarāya svāhā, sarvapāpaśamanāya svāhā iti vyāhṛtibhir hutvā atha sāma gāyet || 3 ||
