ghnantīva vā etat somaṃ rājānaṃ preva mīyate | yad enam abhiṣuṇvanti tasyaitām anustaraṇīṃ kurvanti yat saumyaṃ carum | tasmāt puruṣāya puruṣāyānustaraṇī kriyate || 1 || 
sādhyānāṃ vai devānāṃ sattram āsīnānāṃ śarkarā akṣasu jajñire | te hendram upaniṣeduḥ | kathaṃ nu teṣāṃ śarkarā akṣasu jāyeran yās tvaṃ vidyā iti | tebhya etat saumye carau śyāvam ājyaṃ prāyacchat | tad avekṣanta | te prāpaśyan | prapaśyaty anandho bhavati ya evaṃ vidvānt saumyaṃ carum avekṣate || 2 || 
yo 'lam annādyāya sann athānnaṃ nādyād dakṣiṇārdhaṃ sadaso gatvaitaṃ saumyātiśeṣaṃ prāśnīyāt | janaṃ vā etasmād annādyaṃ krāmati | yo 'lam annādyāya sann athānnaṃ nātti jano 'smāt pitaro janyenaivānnenānnam atti | annādo bhavati || 3 ||
