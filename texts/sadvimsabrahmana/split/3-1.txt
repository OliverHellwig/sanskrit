yaddhotā jahāti vāggha tad yajamānaṃ jahāti | sa yat tat karoti svāṃ vācaṃ yajamāne dadhāti | sa viṣvaṅ vācāmuṣmiṃlloke saṃbhavati || 1 || 
yad adhvaryur jahāti cakṣur ha tad yajamānaṃ jahāti | sa yat tat karoti svaṃ cakṣur yajamāne dadhāti | sa viṣvaṅ cakṣuṣāmuṣmiṃlloke saṃbhavati || 2 || 
yad brahmā jahāti mano ha tad yajamānaṃ jahāti | sa yat tat karoti svaṃ mano yajamāne dadhāti | sa viṣvaṅ manasāmuṣmiṃlloke saṃbhavati || 3 || 
yad udgātā jahāti śrotraṃ ha tad yajamānaṃ jahāti | sa yat tat karoti svaṃ śrotraṃ yajamāne dadhāti | sa viṣvaṅ śrotreṇamuṣmiṃlloke saṃbhavati || 4 || 
yat sadasyo jahāti ātmā ha tad yajamānaṃ jahāti | sa yat tat karoti svātmānaṃ yajamāne dadhāti | sa viṣvaṅṅ ātmanāmuṣmiṃlloke saṃbhavati || 5 || 
yaddhotrāśaṃsino jahaty aṅgāni ha tad yajamānaṃ jahati | te yat tat kurvanti svāny aṅgāni yajamāne dadhati | te viṣvañco ṅgair amuṣmiṃlloke saṃbhavanti || 6 || 
yac camasādhvaryavo jahati lomāni ha tad yajamānaṃ jahati | te yat tat kurvanti svāni lomāni yajamāne dadhati | te viṣvañca lomabhir amuṣmiṃlloke saṃbhavanti || 7 || 
tasmād evaṃvid yajñe hīnaṃ na kuryāt || 8 ||
atha vā ata ṛtvijām eva vijñānam || 9 || 
paśavo hādhvaryum anu kīrtir hotāraṃ yogakṣemo brahmāṇam ātmā ca prajā codgātāram || 10 ||
