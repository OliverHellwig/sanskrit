sa prātaḥsavane savanamukhīyeṣv āhṛteṣūpahavam icchate || 1 || 
agnir me hotā sa mopahvayatāṃ hotar upa mā hvayasvety uccaiḥ || 2 || 
ādityo me 'dhvaryuḥ sa mopahvayatām adhvarya upa mā hvayasvety uccaiḥ || 3 || 
candramā me brahmā sa mopahvayatāṃ brahmann upa mā hvayasvety uccaiḥ || 4 || 
parjanyo ma udgātā sa mopahvayatām udgātar upa mā hvayasvety uccaiḥ || 5 || 
ākāśo me sadasyaḥ sa mopahvayatāṃ sadasyopa mā hvayasvety uccaiḥ || 6 || 
āpo me hotrāśaṃsinas te mopahvayantāṃ hotrāśaṃsina upa mā hvayadhvam ity uccaiḥ || 7 || 
raśmayo me camasādhvaryavas te mopahvayantāṃ camasādhvaryava upa mā hvayadhvam ity uccaiḥ || 8 || 
tā vā etā devatā ṛtvijām eva vāgbhir upahvayante || 9 || 
sa upahūto bhakṣayati || 10 || 
prāṇo yajamāno 'tho yatraitāsāṃ devatānāṃ lokas tad upahūto bhavati || 11 ||
