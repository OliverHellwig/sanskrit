sa dakṣiṇāṃ diśam anvāvartate || 1 || 
atha yadāsya prajāyāṃ paśuṣu śarīre vāriṣṭāni prādurbhavanti | vyādhayo vā anekavidhā atisvapnam asvapnam atibhojanam abhojanam ālasyaṃ vraṇam ajīrṇanidrā[jāgarā]ṇīty evamādīni | tāny etāni sarvāṇi yamadevatyāny adbhutāni prāyaścittāni bhavanti || 2 || 
nāke suparṇam iti sthālīpākaṃ hutvā pañcabhir ājyāhutibhir abhijuhoti | yamāya svāhā pretādhipataye svāhā daṇḍapāṇaye svāhā īśvarāya svāhā sarvapāpaśamanāya svāhā iti vyāhṛtibhir hutvātha sāma gāyet || 3 ||
