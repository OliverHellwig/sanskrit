tā vā etā devalokāya yujyante yat parācyaḥ || 1 ||
pratīcyo manuṣyalokāya || 2 || 
eṣa vāva jāta eṣo 'valuptajarāyur eṣa ārtvijīno yasya dhuro gīyante yaś caivaṃ vidvān dhuro gāyati | jātam evainam annādyāya parivṛṇakti | ubhāv annam atta udgātā ca yajamānaś ca || 3 ||
yā prathamā tām annādyaṃ dhyāyan gāyet || 4 || 
retasa eva tat siktāyānnādyaṃ pratidadhāti || 5 || 
na hiṃkuryāt || 6 || 
yaddhiṃkuryād vajreṇa hiṃkāreṇa retas siktaṃ vicchindyāt || 7 || 
retasyā chando yujyate | mano dhīyate || 8 || 
yā dvitīyā tāṃ gāyatrīm āgāṃ gāyaṃs tasyā dve akṣare saśayanī vyatiṣajati | madhyamasya ca padasyottamam uttamasya ca prathamam | vyatiṣaktau prāṇāpānau prajā dadhato gāyatrī chando yujyate | prāṇāpānau dhīyete || 9 || 
yā tṛtīyā tāṃ triṣṭubham āgāṃ gāyaṃs tasyā dve uttamārdhe 'kṣare dyotayati | cakṣur eva tad yunakti | tasmād virūpaṃ cakṣuḥ kṛṣṇam anyacchuklam anyat triṣṭup chando yujyate | cakṣuṣī dhīyete || 10 || 
yā caturthī tāṃ jagatīm āgāṃ gāyaṃs tasyāś catvāry uttamārdhe 'kṣarāṇi dyotayati | śrotram eva tad yunakti | tasmād yuktaṃ śrotram | śrotre dve pratiśravaṇe dve | tasmād api parāṅ yan pratyaṅ śṛṇoti | jagatī chando yujyate | śrotre dhīyete || 11 || 
yā pañcamī tām anuṣṭubham āgāṃ gāyaṃś caturdhā vyāvṛjya gāyet | caturdhā vā idaṃ puruṣo vīryāya vikṛto jāyate | vīryāyevaināṃ tad vyāvṛjya gāyati | uccāvacām iva gāyet | uccāvacaiva hi vāk | saṃkṣṇutyeva gāyet | saṃkṣṇutyeva hi vācaṃ puruṣo vadati | anuṣṭup chando yujyate | vāg dhīyate || 12 || 
yā ṣaṣṭhī tāṃ paṅktim āgāṃ gāyaṃs tasyā dve dve akṣare udāsaṃ gāyaty ā ṣaḍbhyo 'kṣarebhyaḥ | ṣaḍ ṛtavaḥ | ṛtuṣv eva pratitiṣṭhati | paṅktiś chando yujyate | samānodānau dhīyete || 13 || 
sad iti prathamāyā dhuro nidhanam | retaso hy adhi saj jāyate || 14 || 
sam iti dvitīyāyāḥ | retaso hy adhi saṃbhavaḥ || 15 || 
svar iti tṛtīyāyāḥ | pra svargaṃ lokaṃ jānāti || 16 || 
iḍeti caturthyāḥ | paśavo vā iḍā | paśuṣv eva pratitiṣṭhati || 17 || 
vāg iti pañcamyāḥ | sarvā asmin puṇyā vāco vadanti || 18 || 
ya evaṃ veda || 19 || 
yā prathamā tām āyacchann iva gāyet | āyata iva hy ayam avāṅ prāṇaḥ || 20 || 
yā dvitīyā tāṃ ghoṣiṇīm iva gāyet | ghoṣīva hy ayam apānaḥ || 21 || 
yā tṛtīyā tām udyacchann iva gāyet | udyata iva hy ayaṃ prāṇaḥ || 22 || 
yā caturthī tāṃ nikrīḍayann iva gāyet | nikrīḍita iva hy ayaṃ vyānaḥ || 23 || 
yā pañcamī tāṃ niruktām aniruktām iva gāyet | nirukto 'nirukta iva hy ayaṃ samānaḥ || 24 || 
yā ṣaṣṭhī tām udāsam iva gāyet | udasta iva hy ayam udāno yacchṛṅgam || 25 || 
rathantaravarṇām uttamāṃ gāyet | iyaṃ vai rathantaram asyām eva pratitiṣṭhati || 26 ||
