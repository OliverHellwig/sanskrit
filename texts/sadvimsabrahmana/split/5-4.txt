yūpaṃ karoti sakṣīraṃ sthūlaṃ mūle bālāgram anurūpam || 1 || 
vajro vai yūpo vajreṇaivāsmai bhrātṛvyaṃ praharati || 2 || 
aṣṭāśrīḥ karoti || 3 || 
daśa diśaḥ parigṛhṇāti | ubhayataḥ pāṅkte dve yūpe karoti || 4 || 
pañcadaśasaptadaśaikaviṃśatyaratniṃ vā || 5 || 
pālāśaṃ puṣṭikāmasya | bailvaṃ brahmavarcasakāmasya | audumbaram annādyakāmasya | khādiraṃ balakāmasya | vaibhītakarājavṛkṣau bhrātṛvyavataḥ | kramukāśvatthapāṣāṇāḥ yaśaskāmasya | yat kiṃca yajñiyaṃ paśukāmasya || 6 || 
tatra varjanīyā bhavanti | gaḍulo vraṇilo vyāvṛttaḥ kuṭhiḥ kubjaḥ śūlo dagdhaḥ śuṣkaḥ suṣiro ghuṇajagdha ity apraśastāḥ || 7 || 
atha praśastāḥ | śuddhāvartā anupūrvasamāḥ praśastāḥ || 8 || 
yūpasyāśridaivatāni | agniḥ pūrvāyām | yamo dakṣiṇāyām | varuṇaḥ paścimāyām | soma uttarāyām | yā vidiśas tāsv ādityarudramarudvasavaḥ | aparājitāḥ pitaraś cādharāyām | sādhyāś cordhvāyām || 9 || 
sarvadevatyo vai yūpaḥ | yūpo vai bahurūpaḥ vajrībhūtvā devān upatiṣṭhate || 10 || 
te devāḥ prajāpatim upādhāvan | yūpena praharanty āropayanty āyodhayanti ca | tad yūpasya yūpatvam || 11 || 
mūlam aratniṃ satvacaṃ nikhanet || 12 || 
tasya yan naikhānyaṃ tat pitṝṇām | yad ūrdhvaṃ tan manuṣyāṇām yat tadadho raśanāyā oṣadhivanaspatīnām | yad ūrdhvaṃ raśanāyās tad viśveṣāṃ devānām || 13 || 
āplāvayanty alaṃkurvanty ahatena vasanenācchādayanti ca || 14 || 
tad gandharvāpsarasām || 15 || 
indrasya caṣālam | yad ūrdhvaṃ caṣālasyāṅgulamātraṃ kāryaṃ tat sādhyānāṃ devānām || 16 || 
prācīṃ saṃnamaty etad vai viṣṇoḥ paramaṃ padam || 17 || 
tasyartavaḥ śarīram | śiraḥ saṃvatsaraḥ | vedā rūpāṇi | saṃvatsara eva pratitiṣṭhati | ya evaṃ veda || 18 ||
