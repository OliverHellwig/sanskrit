prajāpatir akāmayata bahuḥ syāṃ prajāyeyeti | sa etāṃ retasyām ṛcaṃ sāmnā pracchannām agāyat | yad ṛcam asāmnīm agāsyad asthy amāṃsam ajaniṣyata | yat sāmānṛcaṃ māṃsam anasthikam ajaniṣyata ṛcaṃ sāmnā pracchannāṃ gāyati | tasmāt puruṣaḥ pracchanno māṃsena tvacā lomnā jāyate || 1 || 
trir udgṛhṇāti || 2 || 
traya ime lokā eṣāṃ lokānām avaruddhyai tribhyaś ca retaḥ sicyate || 3 || 
na hiṃkuryāt || 4 || 
vajro vai hiṃkāro balam iva reto yaddhiṃkuryād vajreṇa hiṃkāreṇa retaḥ siktaṃ vicchindyāt || 5 || 
retasyā [gāyatrī] chandasā | prājāpatyā devatayā | sarvam etayā dhyāyan gāyet | sarvaṃ hīdaṃ retaḥ || 6 || 
dvitīyāṃ gāyati || 7 || 
tasyā dve akṣare saśayanī vyatiṣajati | madhyamasya ca pādasyottamam uttamasya ca prathamam || 8 || 
vyatiṣaktau prāṇāpānau prajā dadhataḥ || 9 || 
gāyatrī chandasā | āgneyī devatayā | pṛthivīm etayā dhyāyan gāyet || 10 || 
tṛtīyāṃ gāyati || 11 || 
tāṃ balavad ivoraseva gāyati || 12 || 
tasyā dve uttamārdhe 'kṣare dyotayati || 13 || 
cakṣur eva tad yunakti tasmād yuktaṃ cakṣuḥ || 14 || 
triṣṭup chandasā | aindrī devatayā | antarikṣam etayā dhyāyan gāyet || 15 || 
caturthīṃ gāyati || 16 || 
tasyāś catvāri catvāry akṣarāṇi nikrīḍayann iva gāyaty ā dvādaśebhyo 'kṣarebhyaḥ || 17 || 
dvādaśākṣarapadā jagatī | paśavo vai jagatī | paśuṣv eva pratitiṣṭhati || 18 || 
tasyāś catvāry uttamārdhe 'kṣarāṇi dyotayati || 19 || 
śrotram eva tad yunakti | tasmād yuktaṃ śrotram | śrotre dve pratiśravaṇe dve | tasmāt puruṣaḥ sarvā diśaḥ śṛṇoti | api parāṅ yan pratyaṅ śṛṇoti || 20 || 
jagatī chandasā | saurī devatayā | divam etayā dhyāyan gāyet || 21 || 
pañcamīṃ gāyati || 22 || 
tāṃ ninardann iva gāyati || 23 || 
āha bahutamāt puruṣād annam atti | annādo bhavati | ya evaṃ veda || 24 || 
niruktāṃ cāniruktāṃ gāyati || 25 || 
niruktena vai vāco bhuñjate 'niruktam asyā upajīvanti | bhuṅkte vācam upaināṃ jīvati | ya evaṃ veda || 26 || 
anuṣṭup chandasā | prājāpatyā devatayā | sarvam etayā dhyāyan gāyet | sarvaṃ hīdaṃ prājāpatyam || 27 || 
ṣaṣṭhīṃ gāyati || 28 || 
tasyā dve dve akṣare udāsaṃ gāyaty ā ṣaḍbhyo 'kṣarebhyaḥ || 29 || 
ṣaḍ ṛtava ṛtuṣv eva pratitiṣṭhati || 30 || 
paṅktiś chandasā | saumī devatayā | diśa etayā dhyāyan gāyet || 31 || 
iheva ca vā eṣa iheva ca manasā gacchati | yo gāyatre prātaḥsavane triṣṭubhaṃ gāyati jagatīṃ gāyaty anuṣṭubhaṃ gāyati paṅktiṃ gāyati | āste || 32 || 
gāyatre dve gāyati || 33 || 
prāṇam eva tad abhyeti | prāṇo hi gāyatram | panthānam eva tad abhyeti | panthā hi gāyatram || 34 || 
rathantaravarṇām uttamāṃ gāyati || 35 || 
iyaṃ vai rathantaram asyām eva pratitiṣṭhati || 36 ||
