udagayanapūrvapakṣapuṇyāhasaṃnipāte yajñakālo 'nādeśe || 8.1.1 ||
iha trikāṇḍo yajñaḥ ekāhāhīnamatrāṇīti teṣāṃ sarveṣāṃ sarvakratva-
dhikāra ityata trārabhya sāmānyo vidhiruktaḥ sāmprataṃ pratikāṇḍaṃ vidhi-
vivacitaḥ taṃ vivatuḥ sāmānyāṃ tāvatparibhāṣāmārabhate udagayana-
pūrvapakṣapuṇyāisannipāte yajñakālo 'nādeśa iti udagayanaṃ pūrvapakṣa
iti siddhaṃ kālajñāne tacca na pūrva prakṛtaṃ pūrvapakṣudagayanamiti
kālanirdeśaḥ śrādityasya saṃvatsare do gatī udīcī dakṣiṇa ca tadyadā
udīcīndiśamāśritya gatirbhavati tadudagayanamucyate tacca na pūrva-
prakṛtaṃ pūrvapakṣo māsasya dvau pakṣau pūrvancottaraca pūrvapakṣo jyotsna
ityarthaḥ tena māsa ārabhyate puṇyamahaḥ puṇyācaṃ tadyasyānukūlena nakṣa-
treṇa yuktaṃ tattasya puṇyam udagayanañca pūrvapakṣaśca puṇyāhañca udagayana-
pūrvapakṣapuṇyāhāni teṣāṃ mannipātaḥ udagayanapūrvapakṣapuṇyāisanni-
pātaḥ ekatra nipātaḥ sannipātaḥ tasmin udagayanapūrvapakṣapuṇyāhamantri-
pāte yajñakālaḥ tasmin kāle yajñaḥ pravarttate anādeśe ādeśādanyatra
ādeśagrahaṇasya prayojanamuktamādivatra eva ādeśaḥ ṣaṣṭhyāṃ śaradi
kārttike māsi yajeta trayodagyāmaparapakṣasya dīneta jāmadagnyāya
prathamāyāṃ pūrvapakṣasya dījete kā hebhyaḥ evamādaya ādeśāḥ śratha kāla-

<8.6.1 uktāḥ sādyaḥkrāḥ sāmprataṃ vrātyastomā ucyante tān vivakṣuridaṃ
