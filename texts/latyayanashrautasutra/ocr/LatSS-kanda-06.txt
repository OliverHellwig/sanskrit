<<0431>> ṣaṣṭhaḥ prapāṭhakaḥ |
atha prathamā kaṇḍikā |

saṃtani codyamānam ekaikasyā sarvaṃ stomasaṃbhavāt || 6.1.1 ||
ukta brahmatvaṃ sāmprataṃ mantanino vidhānamucyate kaḥ punarbrahmatvāna-
ntaraṃ mantanino 'vasara iti ucyate udgācaṃ prakṛtaṃ tatra brahmatvaṃ prāsa-
ṅgikamukta sāmpratamayamācārya: sāmavidhānaṃ vakṣyati tacaikacaklaptiñca
tatra tasmin mantanini ṭcaikarcakriyāṃ prati saṃśayaḥ tatparīkṣārthaṃ santa-
nina eva tāvadvidhānamārabhate etat santanyāmnāye ṭace gītaṃ tatprayo-
gakāle ṭaceva manyate tadetaccinyate kimetadāmnāyagānaṃ pratyṛcaṃ sarvaṃ
kriyatāṃ atha yathāmnāyameveti kutaḥ saṃśayaḥ uccate pragīteṣu, darśitaṃ
santaninaskhayaḥ prastāvāḥ pradarbhyante yadyekasyāmmṛti sarvaṃ gīyate tata-
statprastāvanā sambhavati atha punaryathāmnāyaṃ tato 'sya vākyasyānartha kya
bhavati cayaḥ prastāvasadṛśā iti śrata ārabhyate mantani codyamāna-
mekaikasyāṃ sarva stomasammavāditi santanīti māmnaḥ saṃjñā codyamānaṃ
ādiśyamānam ekaikasyāmmṛci sarvaṃ karttavyaṃ yadetat dvipadottare tayoreva
etaddvāname kaikasyāmmṛci sarva karttavyaṃ kiṃ kāraṇaṃ stomasambhavādeva evaṃ hi
kṛte slomaḥ sambhavati pañcabhaktiyuktena sānā stomaḥ saṃkhyāyate uktañca
* drāhmāyaṇenaitena sūtradvayaṃ kṛtam |

prastāvodgotha pratihāropadravanidhanāni iti tadetā bhaktayaḥ kriyante
tataḥ stomaḥ sambhavati atha punarna kriyante tata utsṛṣṭaikadeśaṃ sāma kṛtaṃ
bhavati nacāyaṃ nyāyaḥ sāmaikadeśaiḥ stomasaṃkhyānamiti tasmādekaika
svāmmṛci sarvaṃ karttavyaṃ bhavati || 1

tathā cānyāni svavicchandaḥsu || 6.1.2 ||
tathā cānyāni sāmāni svacchandaḥsu vicchandaḥsu ca bhavanti
tathā tena prakāreṇetyarthaḥ vāravantīyayajñāyajñīyavāmadevyapañcanidhanavā-
madevyavat yathā vāravantīyamekasmin pāde kṛtsnaṃ paṭhitaṃ tathā yajñā-
yajñīyaṃ jagatīṣu gāyatrīṣu ca tathā vāmadevyaṃ gāyatrīṣu triṣṭapsu ca
gotaṃ tathā pañcanidhanaṃ vāmadevyam atiindaḥsu ca gītaṃ tathā kavatīṣu
gotaṃ rathantaram evametadgānamuttarābhyāṃ luptabhaktikaṃ dṛṣṭam || 2 ||

saṃtanīti yad vāhiṣṭhoyavat || 6.1.3 ||
nanu yadyetatpratyṛcaṃ sarvaṃ gīyate tataḥ mantanitvannopapadyate yadi
bhaktāpāyaḥ kriyate tataḥ mantanilaṃ bhavati anyathā mantanitva na
bhavati anrocyate mantanīti yadāhiṣṭhīyavat yathā yadvāhiṣṭhīyaṃ
sṛte 'pi yadāhiṣṭhaśabdāt yadvāhiṣṭhīyatvaṃ naiva jahāti evametatsantani
ṛte 'pi bhaktilopāt santanitvaṃ na jahāti || 3 ||

triṣu vā tṛceṣv ekarcavat tṛce darśanāt || 6.1.4 ||

triṣu vā ḍhaceṣvetatsantani kriyatām ekavattace darśanāt yathā-
bāya ekarce dṛṣṭāni prayoge ṭace kriyante evamidamapi santaniṭace
dṛṣṭauṃ prayoge triṣu ṭaceṣu, kriyate evaṃ parvaṇaṃ nāya kāyauṃ kṛtamiti

sāmnā ca stomasaṃkhyānāt || 6.1.5 ||
kṛtsnena ca sāmnā stomaḥ saṃkhyāyate nāṅgahonena || 5 ||

tisṛṣv ācāryaḥ smṛteḥ || 6.1.6 ||
tisṛṣu ṛtu ācāyā gānaṃ manyante prayoge kasmāt smṛteḥ
evaṃ hi ācāryaiḥ gotaṃ smṛtiḥ ūhagītiḥ smṛtitastuḥ paraṃ pramāṇaṃ
yadyevamācāvyā manyante atha pūrvavidhānaṃ kasya uccate sthavirasya gauta-
masyedaṃ mataṃ mantanicodyamānamekaikasyāṃ sarvamityevaṃ sthaviro gautamo
manyata iti evameva yaṇvādīnāñcodyamānagrahaṇat || 6 ||

yathānyāni sāmāni || 6.1.7 ||
triṣu bhavanti smṛtereva yathānyāni sāmānoti bhūyastvaṃ gṛhītvā
bravīti bhṛyasvamiti niyāmakaṃ vidhirbhavati ubhayorapi loka-
vedayoḥ bhūyastvaṃ pramāṇobhavati yathā loke matadvaidhe tayorbajatvaṃ tato
gṛhyate tathāmaśakaḥ bhūmāsya vṛhadrathantarapṛṣṭhānyahāni bhavanti pratha-
matra dyaha uttarañcaturahobhūmnovaśannevyāmityetasmāddhetoriti || 7 ||

saṃtānāc ca saṃtani || 6.1.8 ||
yadukta santanīti yadāhiṣṭhīyavaditi tana mantānādeva mantani
yadyuttare stotrīye luptavibhaktike kriyate tataḥ santanitvamupapadyate ||8||

saṃtataṃ gāyatīti cāha || 6.1.9 ||

evaṃ ca brāhmaṇam āha saṃtataṃ gāyatīti yajñasya saṃtatyā iti || 9 ||

ekarceṣu coddhṛtaṃ kalpe || 6.1.10 ||
ekarceṣu, kriyamāṇeṣu, pavamāne maśakenodbhutaṃ yata ekasyāmṭaci
na sambhavati yadi hyabhaviṣyat maśako 'pi noḍūriṣyat pavamānāt
vyūheṣvagniṣṭomeṣu pavamānāduddhṛtya māmakṛtam || 10 ||

pramāṇārthaṃ tṛce darśanaṃ stotrīyāyāṃ sāmasamāptiḥ || 6.1.11 ||
atha yaduktaṃ triṣu vā ṭaceṣvekarttavattṛce darśanāditi tadatra brūmaḥ
pramāṇārthantace darśanam evaṃ kuyāt ḍhaceṣu gānaṃ sthāditi stotrīyā-
yāmeva santani samāptam || 11 ||

tāsu sarvāsu prastāvāḥ syur nyāyapragāthadharmābhyām || 6.1.12 ||
yad etaduktaṃ tisṛvvācāyāḥ smṛteriti etasmin kalpe tāsu sarvāsu
stotrīyāsu prastāvāḥ syuḥ ye uttare stotrīye luptaprastāve āmnā-
yete tayorapi prastāvaḥ karttavyaḥ kasmāt nyāyāt eṣa stotrīyāṇāṃ

dharmaḥ pañcabhaktitva yāni ca pragātheṣu gotāni teṣāmeṣadharma uttama-
pādābhyāsena kriyā || 12 ||

nottarayor ācāryāḥ smṛteḥ || 6.1.13 ||

uttarayoḥ stotrīyayorācāyāḥ prastāvaṃ necchanti kasmāt smṛteḥ
evaṃ hi smaryyate santanyūhagītā || 13 ||

saṃtataṃ gāyatīti ca bhaktilopadarśanāt || 6.1.14 ||
kiñca santataṃ gāyatīti ca yadetadbrāhmaṇaṃ etakilopamevaṃ
dṛṣṭavat || 14 ||

tasya stotrādāv āvartiṣu prastāvaḥ paryāyādiṣu vā || 6.1.15 ||
tasya santaninaḥ āvarttigatasya vidhānamucyate stotrasyādau prastāvaḥ
syāt itarāḥ sarvā luptaprastāvāḥ evaṃ santanitvaṃ sutarāmupapadyate
athavā payāyasya payāyamyādī prastāvaḥ syāt || 15 ||

sarvatra prathamāyām iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 6.1.16 ||
yāvatkṛtvaḥ prathamā prayujyate tāvatkṛtvaḥ prastāvaḥ syāditi evaṃ
dhānañjaya trācāryo manyate || 16 ||

nidhanabhūtāḥ pratihāravatyo 'prastāvyāḥ || 6.1.17 ||
ukraṃ stotrādāvāvarttiṣu prastāvyāḥ prastāvaḥ payāyādiṣu ceti

tadyāsāṃ prastāvo nāsti tāmāṃ kaḥ prayogadharma iti ucyate nidhana-
bhṛtāḥ syuḥ uttarā nidhanatulyā ityarthaḥ yathā piḍhabhūto māṭabhūta
iti atha kiṃ nidhanamiva sahavacanaṃ sarvāsāmeva nidhanabhūtā ityukte
pratihārasyāprāptiḥ śrata āha pratihāravatya iti pratihāra pratiharttā
brūyāt aprastāvyānāmeṣa dharmaḥ || 17 ||

dhānañjapyena prathamāyāṃ śāṇḍilya uttare tu stotrīye viṣṭāvamukheṣv evāprastāvye viṣṭāveṣu caikiṣu trivṛtstome ca parivartino vidhānam || 6.1.18 ||
dhānañjapyena matena prathamāyāṃ sarvatra prastāvo bhavati ityetasmi-
nevaṃ śāṇḍilya āha evamastu sarvatra prathamāyāṃ prastāvo 'stu uttarayoḥ
stotrīyayorviṣṭāvamukheṣveva prastāvo mābhṛt anyatra bhavatviti viṣṭā-
veṣu ca ekiṣu aprastāvye syātāṃ trivṛtstome tu yat parivarttinyāvi-
dhānaṃ tat syāt || 18 ||

uttarayoḥ stotrīyayoś chandasto vipratipattiḥ || 6.1.19 ||
uttarayoḥ stotrīyayoḥ mantaninaḥ chandasi vipratipattiḥ ūha-
goto anekadhā pratipattiḥ vipratipattiḥ bṛhatyākāreṇāpi gītaṃ
kakuvuttarākāreṇapi gotam dvipadottarākāreṇāpi gītam atrāyaṃ
saṃśayaḥ kiṃ vikalpena kriyatām atha viniveśeneti uccate || 18 ||

dvipade syātām aprastāvye kakubhau prastāvye || 6.1.20 ||

yatrottarayoḥ prastāveḥ lupyate tasmin dvipade syātāmuttare
stotrīye nottarayorācāyāḥ smṛterityetasmin prastāvye kalpe kaku-
bhāvuttare stotroye syātāṃ tāsu sarvāsu prastāvāḥ khurityetasmin
kalpe || 20 ||

bṛhatyau śāṇḍilyaḥ kakubhāv aprastāvye || 6.1.21 ||
bṛhatyau śāṇḍilya uttare stotrīye manyate 'prastāvye kalpe kaku-
bhāvuttare stotrīye manyate || 21 ||

tathā saty ubhe kakubhau sthaviro gautamaḥ || 6.1.22 ||
tathā mati śāṇḍilyakatye kriyamāṇe bṛhatyā śāṇḍilyaḥ kakubhāva-
prastāvye ityetasmin ye ca prastāvayitavya stotrīye ye ca na prastāvaya-
tavye ubhī kakubhau syātāmityevaṃ sthaviro gautama śrācāryo ma
nyate || 22 ||

bṛhatyau dhānañjapyaḥ || 6.1.23 ||
uttare stotrīye bṛhatyā dhānañjaya ācāryo manyate śāṇḍilya
kalpe || 23 ||

atha dvitīyā kaṇḍikā |

prathamābhir viṣṭutibhiḥ stomavidhānam anādeśe || 6.2.1 ||
iha trivṛdādiṣu stomeṣu ekaikasmin baho viṣṭutayaḥ paṭhitāḥ
tatprayoge tāsāṃ viṭutonāṃ vikalpo bhavatu atha niyama iti eta-
smin saṃśaye trācāryyaḥ sūtramārabhate prathamābhirviṣṭutibhiḥ soma-
vidhānamanādeśa iti prathamābhiḥ śrādyābhirviṣṭutibhiḥ viṣṭutaya uktā
brāhmaṇena udyatīciḍatorviṣṭutiḥ ityata ārabhya stomavidhānaṃ sto-
mānāṃ klṛptiḥ prayoga ityarthaḥ anādeśe ādeśādanyatra ādeśaḥ yathā
udyatyo viṣṭutayaḥ abhicaraṇīyā viṣṭutayaḥ nirmadhyā saptadaśasya viṣṭu-
tirūīṃ hetuḥ pṛṣṭhāt || 1 ||

tāḥ pathyāḥ sarvābhiprāyāś ca || 6.2.2 ||
yāstāḥ prathamā vidyutayaḥ tāḥ pathyā iti saṃjñā vacyati pathyā-
bhiṣāmāṇi vidadhyādapathyābhiritarāṇīti sarvābhiprāyāca tāḥ
sarvābhiprāyasampādikā ityetadukta bhavati || 2 ||

yathāśiṣaṃ vā vidadhyāt || 6.2.3 ||
athavā yathāśiṣaṃ yatkāmaḥ kratuḥ syāt tajjātīyāṃ viṣṭutiṃ
prayuñjīta || 3 ||

dvitīyāṃ trivṛtaḥ pathyām eke 'viṣṭāvā hy ādyāḥ || 6.2.4 ||
eke ācāryyāḥ trivṛtstomasya dvitīyāṃ viṣṭutiṃ pathyāṃ manyante
hiśabdaḥ kāraṇopadeśārthaḥ yasmādaviṭāvā śrādyā vacabhāgāvāpasthānaṃ
paricarā ḍhacabhāgāvāpasthānaṃ vāpasthānaṃ paricarāṭacabhāgetyete
viṭāvā asyāṃ na vidyante || 4 ||

saṃhāraśiraḥsandhisaṃtaniṣu cānupapannā || 6.2.5 ||
kiñcānyat saṃhārādiṣu anupapannā mā yadā stomakṛtī payyāyāḥ
maṃdriyante tadāsau nopapadyate 'viṣṭāvatvāt tathā śirasi nopapadyate
sakṛddhiṃkṛtena śirasā parācā stuvata dūti nahi tasyāḥ parākkamasti
tathā mantrī nopapadyate parācā hi mandhī somaḥ kriyate etasyāntu
kriyamāṇāyāṃ yathāsthānaṃ stomānāṃ prayoga: syāt tathā manta-
ninyevamevānupapannā uktañca citstomeca parivarttinī vidhāna-
miti || 5 ||

avarṣukas tv iti cāsyām || 6.2.6 ||
tathā apavādo 'pi śrūyate avarṣukastu parjyanyo bhavatīti || 6 ||

iṣor vyatyāsam iṣubhyām || 6.2.7 ||
dvaṣusaṃjñe dve viṣṭutyau paṭhite tābhyāṃ vyatyāsena dvaṣo yajñe prayogaḥ
karttavyaḥ || 7 ||

tayoḥ pūrvā śyenasyāgniṣṭomasāmni || 6.2.8 ||
tayorviṣṭutyoyī pūrva viṣṭutiḥ sā śyenasyāgniṣṭomasānni ka
rttavyaḥ || 8 ||

sattreṣu daśarātre sarvāḥ prayuñjīta || 6.2.9 ||
iha daśarātre trivṛdādayaḥ stomāñcaturviṃśāntāḥ sarve paṭhitāḥ
anenāta prathamābhirviṣṭutibhiḥ stomavidhānamanādeśa iti so 'ya-
mācāryo daśarātre sarvāsāṃ prayogamādiśati satreṣu daśarātre sarvāḥ
prayuñjīteti tatrāyaṃ saṃśayaḥ kiṃ satre daśarātre sarvāḥ prayuñjīta atha satreṣu,
daśarātreṣu sarveṣu sarvāḥ prayuñjīteti yadi tāvatsatre daśarātre sarvāḥ
prayuñjīta itareṣvahaḥsu na prayuñjītetyayamabhiprāyaḥ tena daśarātraprayojaka-
sarvaprayogasya na satratvam atha punaḥ matreṣu yo daśarātrastasmin sarvāḥ
prayuñjītetyayamabhiprāyaḥ tata āhaunike daśarāce prāpnoti sarva-
prayogaḥ atrocyate ubhayatra sarvaprayogaḥ satre cāhonikeṃ ca dūha
sarvāptiḥ prayojikā sarvaprayogasya sā ca sarvāptirubhayatra dṛśyate satre
cāhonike ca ubhayatra sarve stomāḥ sarvāṇi pṛṣṭhāni sarvāṇi chandāṃsi
prayuñjīteti tasmārdiṣṭutīnāmapi prayoga ubhayoriṣyate natu tatro-
cyate yadyevaṃ satragrahaṇaṃ na karttavya satreṣu daśarātra iti uccate, yadyayaṃ
satragrahaṇaṃ na kuryyāt trikakubdaśarātrādiṣvahīnikeṣu daśarātreṣu sarvaḥ

prayoga: syāt so 'yamācāryyaḥ satragrahaṇaṃ karoti yatraiva hetumamatvaṃ
tatraiva sarvatrayogaḥ itareṣu neti kiṃ taddhetusamatvaṃ sarvāptiḥ mā ca
sarvāptistulyā satre cāhone ca tasmādvādaśāhādike daśarāce sarve
cāhonike ca viṣṭutīnāṃ sarvaprayogaḥ syāt || 8 ||

abhicaraṇīyā uddhṛtyeti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 6.2.10 ||
śāṇḍilya śrāha astu sarvatra prayogaḥ kintu śrābhicaraṇīyā
uddhṛtya prayuñjīteti || 10 ||

śāṇḍilyāyanaḥ kulāyinīm || 6.2.11 ||
śāṇḍilyāyana etasminneva prayoge kulāyinyā apyuddhāraṃ ma
nyate || 11 ||

bhastrāṃ cobhā || 6.2.12 ||
bhakhāñca viṣṭhutīm uddharedityevamubhāvācāryo manyete śāṇḍilyaḥ
śāṇḍilyāyanaśca || 12 ||

dhānañjapyas tām eva || 6.2.13 ||
dhānañjaya ācāryastāmeva uddharediti spaṣṭārthaḥ || 13 ||


pathyābhir hotṛṣāmāṇi vidadhyād apathyābhir itarāṇi || 6.2.14 ||
evaṃ sati sarvaprayoge viṣṭutīnāṃ stotravinyāsaḥ kriyate sarvasmin
daśarātre sarveṣāṃ stomānāṃ yāḥ pathyā viṣṭutayastābhiautṛṣāmāṇi
vidadhyāt apathyābhiritarāṇi sarvāṇi stotrāṇi kuyāt hoṭaṣā-
mabhyo yānyanyāni || 14 ||

samāptāsu tā evāditaḥ punaḥ prayuñjīta || 6.2.15 ||
samāptāsu sarvāsu viṣṭutiṣu pratyahantā evādita ārabhya punaḥ
prayuñjīta | 15 ||

tathā kurvan prāgāthikāni parivartinyā prathamasyeti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 6.2.16 ||
tathā tena prakāreṇa kurvan yāni prāgāthikāni sāmāni
prathame 'hani bhavanti rathantaranaudhamakāleyayajñāyajñīyāni tāni
parivarttinyā viṣṭatyā kuryyāt itarāṇyanyābhiḥ || 16 ||

sakṛt sarvāḥ samāpya pathyayaiva tata ūrdhvam iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 6.2.17 ||
makṛtsarvāḥ kṛtvā yānyanyāni stotrāṇi śiṣṭāni tāni pathyacaiva
viṣṭutyā kuyāt ityevaṃ dhānañjapya ācāryo manyate kintu || 17 ||

asamāptāsu tv api hotṛṣāma pathyayaiva tathā kuryāt || 6.2.18 ||
yadyetena prakāreṇa kuyāt tato yadyasamāptāsu sarvāsu hoṭa-
ṣāmāgacchet pathyayaiva kuyāt tataḥ śeṣāḥ samāpayet || 18 ||

kulāyinīṃ cet prayuñjīta prathamasyāhno dvitīyaṃ pṛṣṭhaṃ tayā vidadhyāt || 6.2.19 ||
yadi kulāyinoṃ prayuñjīta tatastayā dvitīyaṃ pṛṣṭhaṃ vidadhyāt
prathamagrahaṇaṃ niyamārthaṃ dvitīyameva pṛṣṭhaṃ vidadhyāt kramāgatāṃ na
kuyāt || 18 ||

ṣaṣṭhasyāhna uttamam uktham uttamayā trayastriṃśasya viṣṭutyā vidadhyāt || 6.2.20 ||
ṣaṣṭhasyāhna uttamamevoktam uttamayā cayastriṃśasya viṣṭutyā vida
dhyāt atrāpi ṣaṣṭhagrahaṇaṃ niyamārtham || 20 ||

uktaḥ prayogo brāhmaṇena catuścatvāriṃśe || 6.2.21 ||
catuścatvāriṃśe stome brāhmaṇena prayoga uktaḥ diprakāraḥ śrājyānāṃ
prathamā pṛṣṭhānāṃ dvitīyokthānāṃ ḍhatīyeti pūrvaḥ śrājyānāṃ sā hetu-
rityata ārabhyottaraḥ || 21 ||

savanasamīṣantī pūrvaḥ prayogaḥ stotrasamīṣanty uttaraḥ || 6.2.22 ||
yaḥ pūrvaḥ prayogaḥ sa savanasamīṣanto prayoga ucyate ekaikaḥ san
savanamupagacchati dūṣatirgatyarthaḥ uttaraḥ prayogaḥ stotrasamoṣantī
prayogaḥ || 22 ||

tathā kuryād aharbhāji || 6.2.23 ||

anyatrāpi yadyaharbhajeta catuñcatvāriṃśaḥ anena prakāraṇāsya vidhānaṃ
kuyāt savanamamīṣantī prayogaṃ paththayā kuryyāt stotrasamoṣanto
prayogaṃ vā || 23 ||

pathyām anyatra || 6.2.24 ||
anyatra anahabhāji pathyāmeva kuyāt || 24 ||

aṣṭācatvāriṃśasyottarayā maitrāvaruṇasyaivājyaṃ vidadhyāt || 6.2.25 ||

aṣṭācatvāriṃśasyottarayā viṣṭutyā maitrāvaruṇasyaivājyaṃ kuryyāt
evaśabdo 'vadhāraṇārthaḥ anyāni sarvāṇi stotrāṇi pūrvayaiva || 25 ||
sarvatra vā hotṛkasāmāni || 6.2.26 ||

sarvasminvāini hāṭakasāmāni aṣṭācatvāriṃśasyottarayā viṭutyā

ekiprabhṛtaya ekādhiśayāḥ || 6.5.18 ||
ekiprabhṛtayaḥ stomāḥ vyuttarībhāvena varttamānā ekādhiśayā
bhavanti || 18 ||

dvikaprabhṛtayo hy adhiśayāḥ || 6.5.19 ||
dvikaprambhṛtayaḥ vyuttarobhāveṇa varttamānādyadhiśayā bhavanti ||18||

samās trikaprabhṛtayaḥ || 6.5.20 ||

tṛkaprabhṛtayaḥ vyuttarībhāveṇa varttamānāḥ samā bhavanti || 20 ||

viṣamāḥ samaparyāyair vartante || 6.5.21 ||
ya ete viṣamā ekādhiśayā dyadhiśayāḥ samaparyyāyairvarttante ete
samāḥ samānaparyyāyāstasmāt samānavṛttireteṣāṃ yāvabhito 'nantarau
samau tayoḥ payyāyān maṃharet pūrvasyaikamekādhiśayeṣu dvau dyādhaṇayeṣabha
yānyathājāti yasmin viṣame stome paryyāyakṛtiḥ karttavyā tasya yāva-
bhito 'nantarau samau stomā syātāṃ tayoḥ paryyāyasaṃhāraṃ kuryyāt eka-
mekādhiśayeṣa, stomeṣu pūrvasya stomasya evaṃ paryyāyamanusaṃharet uttarasya
samasya dvāvanusaṃharet | dyadhiśayeṣu, saṃharediti varttate pūrvasya dā
macāyamuttarasya paryyāyasyaikamekādhiśaye paryyāyavacanaṃ tadekamiti
ubhayānetān paryyāyān yathājātimatāmanusaṃharet ye tu ye tatra |
anumaṃharet samānajātīyān tānapasatra eva samebhyo yathā saptiniḻā
yatrānurūpaparyyāyakṛtiḥ uttamau trivṛtastathā | ekādaśasya trivṛtaḥ
prajānāṃ pūrvo triko paryāyau pañcadaśasyottamaḥ saptadaśaḥ | dvitīya-
tṛtīyayodiśāt | dvitīyaṣaṭkāt uttamau dvādaśāt viparyyayo
yugmāyujāmayukṣu yathāsthānaṃ paryāyān kuryyāt mo 'vidoṣaḥ bhāyyānta-
rametat || 21 ||

yāv abhito 'nantarau samau tayoḥ paryāyān saṃharet || 6.5.22 ||
yo stomo yasya stomaviṣayasthānantarau samastomo tayoḥ samba-
ndhinaḥ paryyāyānāharet viṣamaḥ klṛptyarthaṃ kathamityāha || 22 ||

pūrvasyaikam ekādhiśayeṣu || 6.5.23 ||
pūrvasya samasya sambandhinamekaṃ payyāyam śrāharet klṛptyarthaṃ sāmarthyāt
dvāvuttarāditi siddham || 23 ||

dvau dvyadhiśayeṣu || 6.5.24 ||
dyadhiśayā yeṣānde samebhyo 'dhike teṣu kalpapramāneṣu pūrvasya
dvau parthyāyāvāharet arthāduttarasya samasyaikam atra viśeṣamāha ||24||

ubhayān yathājāti || 6.5.25 ||
ubhayānekādhiśayān dyadhiśayān saṃhṛyamāṇān yathājāti
kalpapramāne jātyanatikrameṇa saṃharet tenāyucce kādhiśayadyadhiśayeṣu
karttavyeṣvayugbhya eva samebhyaḥ pārśvasthebhyo 'nantarebhyañca payyāyasaṃhāraḥ
karttavyaḥ tathā yujyekādhiśayadyadhiśayeṣu kartavyeṣu samānajātīyebhyo
paryāyasaṃhāraḥ kartavyaḥ
yugbhya eva samebhyaḥ pārśvasthebhyo 'nantarebhyaḥ
udāharaṇaṃ trayodaśī trasyaikādhiśayatva viṣamatvañcāsti ayañca
dvādaśāhavat samastotrīyakādekayādhikaste viṣamā iti ca viṣamaḥ
ekiprabhṛtitvāccāyuk tenānantarayorayujoḥ samayoḥ sakāśāt
paryyāyasaṃhāre viṣamāḥ samapayyāyairiti yukta pūrvasyaikamekādhi-
śayeṣviti ca tenāyujaḥ samastotrīyakasyānantarasya trivṛta ekaṃ
payāyamāharet trayodaśini prathamaṃ yathāsthānaṃ payāyāniti hi
vakṣyati pañcadaśasyottarau ca ho tadanantaratvādayuktātmamastotrī-
yakatvācca saptadaśo dyadhiśayo viṣamaḥ pañcadaśaḥ dābhyāmadhikatvāt
tasthākṛtiḥ prathamaiḥ paṭhitaiḥ pañcadaśādanantarādayujaḥ samādekaviṃśā-
duttamaḥ atra ca do pūrvasya dyadhiśayatvāt ṣoḍa़śa ekādhiśayo yuk
śrato 'syaikaḥ samādyujaśca dvādaśāt prathamaḥ aṣṭādaśāduttarau cau
atraikaḥ pūrvasyaikādhiśayatvāt ṣaḍviṃśo dyadhiśayaḥ samo yuktasya
yujaḥ samādanantarāccaturviṃśāt ho prathamau triṃśācottamo yuk sama-
tvāt tena yugviṣamān yuksamebhya ayugviṣamānayukmamebhya iti siddhaṃ
tadviṣamān viṣamairityeko jātisaṃhārakalpaḥ saprapañca uktaḥ adhunā
yugmāyuksaṃhārakalpaṃ vaktumāha || 25 ||

yugmāyugbhyo vā || 6.5.26 ||
anantarau samau tadyathājātyevaṃ kalpapramānasya yathā yugvi-
ṣamān yuksamebhya ayugviṣamānayuksamebhya iti apitvanantarebhyaḥ
samebhyo yugmāyugbhyaḥ ubhayatra prayuji yuji ca maṃhāraḥ kartavyaḥ kathami-
tyata śrāha || 26 ||

tatra yujām ekādhiśayānām ayujaḥ pūrvau paryāyau || 6.5.27 ||
tatra yugmāyuksaṃhārakalpe yujāmekādhiśayānāṃ kartavyānāmayujo '-
nantarasya ca hī pūrvī paryāyāvāharet yathā ṣoḍuśini samānayujo '-
nantarāt pañcadaśāt pūrvo hai| paryāyau arthādaṣṭādaśāt yuksamāco-
ttamamāharet || 27 ||

uttarau dvyadhiśayānām || 6.5.28 ||
yukyadhiśayānāmayujaḥ anantarasya samasyottarau yatra tatrasthasya
atevottarāvityāha athavottarāviti vacanāt uttarādayuja āneyau
yadhiśayānāṃ samānāṃ ekādhiśayānāṃ pūrvasmāt pūrvatra pūrvāvityoḥ
yathāsthānamiti vacanaṃ vakṣyati tena yujo dyadhiśayasya ṣaḍ ḍviṃśasthā-
nantarādayuja uttarāt saptaviṃśāt samāduttarī dāvadhikāt yujaḥ
ekaṃ pūrvāt caturviṃśāt samāditi || 28 ||

viparyayo yugmāyujām ayukṣu || 6.5.29 ||
ayujāmekādhiśayeṣu dyadhiśayeṣu kartavyeṣu yugmāyujoḥ pūrvotāt
nyāyāt viparīto nyāyo jñeyastenāyujādhiśayānāṃ kartavyānāṃ trayo-
daśādīnāṃ yujo dvādaśāt pūrvī paryāyau arthāt pañcadaśāduttara
yadhiśayānāntu prayujāṃ yujaḥ samādanantarāduttarī yathā saptadaśinyevo-
dāharaṇamātrantu saptadaśaṃ klṛptatvāt tenāṣṭādaśāduttarī pañcadaśāt pūrva-
iti saptadaśe yugmāyuksaṃhāraḥ || 28 ||

yathāsthānaṃ paryāyān kuryāt so 'vidoṣaḥ || 6.5.30 ||
śrayamāṇān paryāyān yathāsthānaṃ svasthānānatikrameṇa
kuyāt prathamādānītaṃ prathamamuttarāduttaramiti tena prathamaḥ prathame
sthāne dvitoye dvitīyastṛtīye tṛtīya iti evaṃ kriyamāṇe avidoṣo
bhavatoti saṃvyavahārārtham | kaṇḍikā samāptā | ṣaṣṭhasya pañcamī kaṇḍi-
kāyāṃ tyaktaśirān bhaṇitvā anyabhāyyāttyaktamekarmādhikābaliiyamuddhṛtya
triṃśadadhikāgre kṣepitaṃ catuṣke trikān ṣaṣṭhi yujāyugbhyo vā athavā
ayugbhyaścānuśayā yugmā | tatra yujāmekādhiśayānāṃ stomānāmekā-
dhiśayānāṃ trayujaḥ pūrvī payāyo syātāmuttarī dyadhiśayānāṃ tat
tasmin yugmā | stomānāmekādhiśayānāmayujāvitarau stomā pūrvī
paryāyau sthātāṃ arthādāpannaṃ yuja uttamamiti dyadhiśayānāṃ yujasteṣāṃ
uttamau payyāyau na ekasmāt uttarapūrvayoḥ syātām arthādāpannaṃ yujaḥ
pūrvā prathama iti yathā daśini trivṛtaḥ pūrvo dvādaśāt uttamaḥ tathā |
prathamayuja uttaraḥ samaḥ prathamadvitīyā ṣaṭkāt tathottamaḥ prathamaḥ
trivṛtaḥ dvitoyagṛtīyau prathamamajujaḥ | payāyo trānantayyāt payyāya-
saṃhāraḥ | kuyāt payāyāṇāṃ yathāsthānaṃ | vidoṣaṃ varjayediti ||30||
iti ṣaṣṭhasya pañcamī kaṇḍikā |

svādirbhavati tasyāpi yadavamānantadevotta masyārambhaḥ || 1 ||

kṛtau varjayet || 8 ||
dāśarātrikebhyaḥ paryāyakṛtau pathyāparyāyān eva saṃharet || 6.6.9 ||

parivartinyās tu trivṛto 'nādeśe || 6.6.10 ||
trivṛtastu yadā payāyakṛtā saṃhāraḥ karttavyaḥ syāt tadā pari-
parttinī paryāyānanusaṃharet anādeśe ādeśe tu yathādeśaṃ darśini
stome vakṣyati udyatyāveti || 10 ||

eṣa nyāyo 'nādiṣṭānām || 6.6.11 ||
yeṣāṃ stomānāṃ paryāyā nādiṣṭāḥ teṣāmeṣa paryāyakṛtau nyāyaḥ || 11 ||

ekinyāvartisthānagate triṣ purastāddhiṃkāraṃ śāṇḍilyaḥ trike ca prathamayā stomapūraṇam || 6.6.12 ||
ekini stome āvarttisthānagate sati śāṇḍilyo hiṃkārasya
purastāt triḥ kriyāṃ manyate eṣa payāyāṇāṃ dharma iti trike ca stome
śrāvarttisthānagate prathamayā stotrīyayā stomapūraṇaṃ manyate sarveṣu
pāyeṣu prathamā eva syāditi || 12 ||

tṛcena dhānañjapyaḥ sakṛc caikini hiṅkāram || 6.6.13 ||

trike stome tvacena dhānañjaya ācārya: stomapūraṇaṃ manyate
ekini cāvarttisthānagate maddhivāraṃ manyate payyāyanimitto
hiṃkāraḥ na ca tasmin sarve payāyāḥ santi || 13 ||

jātisaṃhāraḥ pañcini || 6.6.14 ||
pañcini stome jātisaṃhāraḥ syāt nāyaṃ pṛthaganupūrvaṃ saṃhāraḥ syāt || 14 ||

pañcadaśaparyāyaiḥ pṛthaganupūrvaṃ rātriparyāyān vidadhyāt saṃlaṅghitāyām iti bhāṇḍitāyanaḥ || 6.6.15 ||
saṃlaṅghitāyāṃ rātrau paṭhati pañcadaśabhirhetre stuyuḥ pañcabhiḥ pañcabhi-
ritarebhya iti saṃlaṅghanaṃ kālātikramaḥ pañcabhiḥ pañcabhiritarebhya
dūti kṛtalakṣaṇasya pañcini saṃhāraḥ prāpnoti evaṃ kṛtvā śrāha pañcadaśa-
payyāyairiti ye pañcadaśe stome payāyāḥ kṛtāḥ taiḥ payāyaiḥ pṛthagnā-
netyarthaḥ ānupūryeṇa rātripayāyeṣu vidhānaṃ kuyāt evamabhivyuṣṭāyāṃ
rātrau || 15 ||

tatra viṣṭāvāḥ paryāyasthāneṣu syuḥ pṛthak ca stotrīyāsu paryāyāḥ || 6.6.16 ||
tatra tasminvidhau pañcadaśapayāyā evaṃ syuḥ trayāṇāṃ payāyāṇāṃ sthāneṣu trayo 'pi viṣṭāvāḥ syuryathāsthānaṃ evaṃ paryāyā nānāstotrī-
yāsu bhavanti || 66 ||

dāśarātrikebhya ekayājyāyaḥsu tasmāt pūrvasyottame paryāya āvapet || 6.6.17 ||
ye dāśarātrikebhya ekayājyāyāṃsasteṣu payāyakṛtau tasmāt
kariṣyamāṇaparyyāyāt stomādyaḥ pūrvastasyottame payyāye ekāṃ
stotrīyāmāvapet || 17 ||

brahmāyatanīyā tu teṣu kṣatrāyatanīyā vā || 6.6.18 ||
tatra prathame viṣṭāve bhūyiṣṭhāṃ trāvapet madhyame vā uttamasya payyā-
yasya evaṃ sā brahmāyatanīyā vā bhavati kṣatrāyatanīyā vā || 18 ||
āskandaṃ tu gautamaś catvāriṃśe || 6.6.19 ||

catvāriṃśe stome gautama trācārya śrāskandaṃ manyate tuśabdo '-
vadhāraṇārthaḥ traskanda uktalakṣaṇe mahāvrataprakaraṇe caturviṃśena vā
stutvā madhyamāmahiṃkṛtāntṛcasya prastuyātma āskando nāmeti || 18 ||

dhānañjapya itareṣu || 6.6.20 ||
itareṣu dāśarātrikeṣu ekayājyāyaḥsu stomeṣu āskandaṃ dhānañjapyo manyate || 20 ||

iti ṣaṣṭhasya ṣaṣṭhī kaṇḍikā |

daśini catuṣko madhye parivartinī paryāyāvabhitaḥ || 6.7.1 ||

udyatyā vā || 6.7.2 ||

prathamāyāmeva catuṣkaṃ kuryyāt || 3 ||

stome paryāyeṣu yāni ṭacabhāgāsthānāni teṣu dvikāviṣṭāvāḥ
syuḥ || 4 ||

trayodaśatrayoviṃśayoḥ pañcaviṃśe ca madhyamaḥ paryāyo jyāyān ekajyāyaḥsu kanīyān ekakanīyaḥsu || 6.7.5 ||
trayodaśatrayoviṃśayoḥ stomayoḥ pañcaviṃśe ca stome viśeṣa
ārabhyate ekayā stotrīyayā jyāyaḥsu yo madhyamaḥ paryāyaḥ ma jyāyān
syāt ekayā kanīyaḥsu ekayā kanīyān madhyamaḥ paryāyaḥ svāt tat
kimidaṃ bahuvacanaṃ yadā ekaḥ kanīyān jyāyāṃ dau syātāṃ tatrai-
ke tāvadarṇayanti paryāyeṣu bahuvacanamiti ekojyāyān yeṣu paryā-
yeṣu time ekajyāyāṃsaḥ ekaḥ kanīyān yeṣu tadame ekakanīyāṃsaḥ
apare bruvate stomeṣveva bahuvacanaṃ yathā narāṇāṃ vā narāṇāñca katha-
māsot samāgamaḥ atriṣṭujagatokā asya stotrapravaḥ savanapravaḥ sya
athavā paryāyakṛtirevāsya prāthamyena vivacitā tatra cayodaśe tāvat
ekayājyāyami prathamottamā paryāyā dvādaśāt madhyamaḥ pañcadaśaśat
tathā pañcaviṃśe caturviṃśāt prathamottama paryāyo madhyamatriṇavāt
trayoviṃśa ekayā kanīyān tasmiṃścaturviṃśāt prathamottamā madhyama

strayastriṃśāt || 6 ||

rūḍhaparyāyā vā || 6.7.7 ||

paryāyau syātāṃ trayodaśī madhyamaḥ payāyo bhavati || 14 ||

tasmin prathamāyās trirvacanaṃ tathottamāyāḥ || 6.7.15 ||

pañcadaśa paryāyāv abhito navī madhye trikaviṣṭāv iti dhānajapyaḥ || 6.7.16 ||

vabhitaḥ syātāṃ navī paryyāyo madhye sthāt trikairviṣṭāvairiti || 16 ||

śāṇḍilyāyanas tu jātisaṃhāram || 6.7.17 ||

dvātriṃśe tisro viṣṭutīḥ kuryād iti gautamaḥ || 6.7.18 ||

manyate || 18 ||

tāsāṃ paryāyakṛtiprayogau catusraś catvāriṃśena vyākhyāto || 6.7.19 ||
tāsāṃ dvātriṃśaprayoge tisṛṇāṃ viṭutīnāṃ paryāyāṇāñca kṛtiḥ
prayogaśca catukhalāriṃśena stomena vyākhyāto payyāyakṛtistāvat
prathamāyāṃ viṣṭutyāṃ ekādaśī prathamaḥ payāyaḥ daśī dvitīyaḥ ekādaśī
tṛtīyaḥ dvitīyayā daśo tṛtīyayā ekādaśī daśī pūrva ekādaśināvu-
ttarau dvitīyāyām ekādaśino pūrvī daśī uttamāḥ tṛtīyāyāṃ
prayogaḥ yājyānāṃ prathamā pṛṣṭhānāṃ dvitoyokthānāṃ tṛtīyetyevaṃ
prathamaḥ yājyānāṃ mā hāturityata ārabhya dvitīyo yo 'cchāvākamiti
tṛtīyaḥ || 18 ||

apy aharbhāji prathamayaiveti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 6.7.20 ||
śāṇḍilya ācāryya āha aharbhājyapi dvātriṃśe prathamayaiva viṣṭutyā
sarvamahaḥ kuryyāt uttare viṣṭutī naiva prayuñjata anyeṣāṃ vācāyāṇaṇaṃ
yathā kuryyādaharbhājīti prakṛtameva || 20 ||

catuṣṭomayoś catuṣparyāyāḥ || 6.8.1 ||

catuṣṭomṛtuṣṭomacatuṣṭomo

kiṃ punarayaṃ stomabhedonigadyate iha tripayāyatā stomānāṃ nyāya
ucyate brāhmaṇam athaiṣa catuṣṭomaḥ paśukāmo yajetetyevamadhikṛtya
catuṣkādīn ṣaṭ caturuttarān stomān paṭhati tasmin ṣaṭstome
sati catuṣṭomaśabdo na sambhavati so 'yaṃ catuṣṭomaśabdo mukhyairathaira-
sambhavan gole kalpate catuṣpavyāyāḥ stomā iti uttaro yadyapi
catuṣṭomo bhavati tathāpi tatra catuṣpavyāyataiva sannihitatvāt stomā-
bhyāmityukta tat || 1 ||

tatra catuṣke dvau prathamāyāṃ paryāyau syātāṃ yadi brahmāyatanīyāṃ kuryāt || 6.8.2 ||
tatra tayocatuṣṭomayoścatuṣke stome do prathamāyāṃ stotrīyāyāṃ
payāyo syātāṃ yadi brahmāyatanīyāṃ viṣṭutiṃ kuyāt || 2 ||

madhyamāyāṃ kṣatrāyatanīyāṃ cet || 6.8.3 ||

yadi kṣattrāyatanīyāṃ viṣṭutiṃ kuyāt tato madhyamāyāṃ stotrī-
yāyāṃ ho payyāyo syātām || 3 ||


tathāṣṭini dvikās tu tasya paryāyāḥ || 6.8.4 ||
yathā catuṣko tathāṣṭini yadi brahmāyatanīyāṃ kuyāt hī
prathamāyāṃ payyāyā syātām śratha kṣattrāyatanīyāṃ madhyamāyāṃ traya-
mapi tathāṣṭino viśeṣa: hikāstu tasya paryāyāḥ eṣā brahmāyata-
nīyā eṣā kṣattrāyatanīyā || 4 ||

pūrvayoḥ pūrva uttarayor uttara evaṃ vihitāv aparāv iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 6.8.5 ||
pūrvayoḥ stotrīyayoḥ pūrvaḥ paryāyaḥ syāt uttarayoḥ stotrīya-
yoruttaraḥ payyāyaḥ syāt aparāvapi payāyo evaṃ vihitau pūrvayoḥ
pūrva uttarayoruttara iti evaṃ śāṇḍilya ācāryo manyate eṣa dvitīyaḥ
prakāro 'ṣṭinaḥ || 5 ||

pūrvayor dvitīyaḥ pṛthakstotrīyāsv itara iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 6.8.6 ||
pūrvayoḥ stotrīyayoḥ prathamadvitīyayodvitīyaḥ paryyāyaḥ myāt dūtare
paryāyāḥ prathamaṭatoyacaturthāḥ pṛthakstotrīyāsu syuḥ evaṃ dhānañjapya
ācāryo manyate eṣa tṛtīyaḥ prakāro 'ṣṭinaḥ || 6 ||

dvādaśe vyākhyātau prathamottamau madhyamau dvikau pūrvayoḥ pūrva uttarayor uttaraḥ || 6.8.7 ||

dvādaśe stome vyākhyātā prathamottama paryāya dvādaśaprakaraṇa
eva dvādaśe ṭacabhāgāsthāneṣu dvikā viṣṭāvā iti madhyamā paryāyā
ḍiko syātāṃ prathama dvitīyayoḥ stotrīyayoḥ madhyamayoḥ paryāyayoḥ
pūrvaḥ syāt dvitīyatvatīyayoruttaraḥ || 7 ||

ṣoḍaśe catvāro dvādaśaparyāyās teṣāṃ madhyamau sadṛśau || 6.8.8 ||
ṣoड़śe stome dvādaśastomavaccatvāraḥ paryyāyāḥ syuḥ teṣāṃ madhyamā
paryāyau madṛśau samānarūpī viṃśaḥ stomaḥ ārbhava eva tatra paryyāya-
kṛtirnoktā || 8 ||

caturviṃśe catvāraḥ ṣaṭkāḥ paryāyā dviviṣṭāvāḥ || 6.8.9 ||
caturviṃśe stome catvāraḥ ṣaṭkā: paryāyā hikaviṣṭāvāḥ syuḥ ||8||

pṛthaktṛceṣu paryāyāḥ syuḥ || 6.8.10 ||
caturṣu ṭaceṣu vāravantīyamagniṣṭomasāma paṭhitaṃ teṣāṃ prayogaḥ
paṭhito maśakenaiva vikalpārthaḥ punarārambhaḥ pṛthaṅnānetyarthaḥ tace ṭace
paryyāyaḥ syāt || 10 ||

dvayor dvayor vā vyatyāsam || 6.8.11 ||
iyoīyorvā ḍhacayoḥ paryāya: syāt prathamadvitīyayoḥ prathamaḥ
paryāyaḥ tṛtīyacaturthayoḥ dvitīyaḥ paryyāyaḥ punaḥ prathamadvitīyayostṛtīyaḥ
tṛtataॊyacaturthayoścaturthaḥ atra yathāsthānaṃ stotrīyāḥ eṣa vyatyāsaḥ ||11||

yajñāyajñīyarkṣu vā prathamaḥ prathamāsu ca yajñāyajñīyaprathamāyāṃ sākamaśvarkṣu paramadhyamayoś ca dvitīyaḥ pūrvayoś ca madhyamayor hārivarṇarkṣu tairaścyottamāyāṃ ca tṛtīyaḥ pūrveṣāṃ cottamāsu tairaścyarkṣu cāntyaḥ || 6.8.12 ||

ayaṃ tṛtīyaḥ prakāro yajñāyajñīyastha tisraḥ mākamaśvahāri-
varṇatairajhānāmādyāstisraḥ eṣa tāvat prathamaḥ paryāya: yajñāyajñī-
yastha prathamaḥ mākamaśvasya ca stisraḥ hārivarṇataira ścāyormadhyame dve eṣa
dvitīyaḥ paryāyaḥ yajñāyajñīyamākamaśvayormadhyame dve ṛcau hārivarṇa-
syarvastisraḥ tairaścātyottamā ṛk eṣa tṛtīyaḥ paryāya: yajñāyajñīya-
sākamavahārivarṇānāṃ samuttamāstisrastairaścārca: sarvā eṣa uttamaḥ
paryāyaḥ etāsu sarvāṃsu vāravantīyameva syāt sarveṣveteṣu ḍhikā viṣṭā-
vāḥ || 12 ||

upottamo vā dvikaviṣṭāvo 'śvamedhe 'ṣṭādaśād itare || 6.8.13 ||
lāghavika ācārya: prasaṅgena bravīti aśvamedhe 'pi prathame 'hani catu-
ṣṭomāḥ stomāḥ tatrāgniṣṭomasāntri caturviṃśe stome calāraḥ ṣaṭkā:
paryāyā vikaviṣṭāvāḥ syuḥ athavā upottama eva ddikaviṣṭāvaḥ syāt
prathamadvitīyacaturthā śraṣṭādaśātsarve bhavanti paryāyaklaptāt || 13 ||

pañcadaśaparyāyau pūrvāv ekaviṃśasyottarāv iti vottarāv iti vā || 6.8.14 ||
tatrāpi āhonike catuṣṭome agniṣṭomamāni caturviṃśe stome
catvāraḥ ṣaṭkāḥ paryāyā dvikaviṣṭāvāḥ syuḥ athavā pañcadaśasya prathama-
dvitīyā paryāyau syātām ekaviṃśasya tṛtīyacaturthī iti vikalpaḥ || 14 ||

chando daivatasāmāntayogān nyāyyān bruvate yathā ṣoḍaśimato 'tirātrasya jyotiṣṭomasya || 6.9.1 ||
aśvamedhe ukta stomavidhānaṃ sāmprataṃ stotrakalpānāṃ nyāya
uccate yena stotrāṇi kalpayitavyāni atrocyate kimidaṃ yāvatā
maśakena gavāmayanādīnāṃ sahasra saṃvatsarāntānāṃ jyotiṣṭomaṣoḍaha-
dādaśāhavicārāṇañca sarveṣāṃ niravaśeṣeṇa stotravidhiḥ kalpitaḥ
paraṃ naḥ pramāṇaṃ maśakaḥ tat kimidaṃ piṣṭapeṣaṇaṃ kriyate stotrakalpana-
vidhyarthaṃ ārambha iti ! ucyate, satyametadevaṃ kintu anubrāhmaṇikānāṃ
ahayogaṃ gṛhītvā stotravidhiḥ kalpito maśakena tatra ye tāṇḍake
pravacane āmnātāsteṣāṃ stotravidhiḥ kalpitaḥ kṣudrakṛtiśca asya vācā-
ryasya pratijñā sarvakratvadhikāra dūti tad ye śākhāntare dṛṣṭāḥ bhruḥ sarva-
medhacaturthamārasvatādayasteṣāṃ stotravidhiḥ kalpayitavyaḥ so 'yamācārya-
stavyāyārthaṃ śāstramārabhate chandodaivatamāmāntayogāni chandāṃsi ca
daivatāni ca sāmāntāśca chandodaivatasāmāntāsteṣāṃ chandodaivatamāmā-
ntānāṃ yogāḥ chandodaivatasāmāntayogāḥ ayaṃ yogaśabdaḥ pratyekaśaḥ
mambadhyate chandodaivatasāmāntayogānyāyyānācāryo brūvate nyāyādana-
petānyāvyān yathā ṣoड़ śimato 'tirātrasya jyotiṣṭomasya chandamāṃ
tāvadyogo gāyatraṃ prātaḥsavanaṃ chandaḥ mādhyandinaḥ pavamāno gāyatrī
bṛhatīviṣṭuviti bāītāni pṛṣṭhāni gāyatroṣa vāmadevyam ārbhave
pañca chandāṃsi gāyatrī kakubuṣṇiganuṣṭujagatīti agniṣṭomasānni
prathamā bṛhato kakubhāvuttare gāyatraṃ prathamamukthaṃ kakubhaṃ dvitīyaṃ
vicchandaḥsu tṛtīyaṃ svarānuṣṭubhi ṣoड़śī anuṣṭubhī rātriḥ bārhataḥ
sandhiḥ kakubuttaryeṇa śratha daivatāni saumyāḥ pavamānā: āgneya prathama-
mājyam maitrāvaruṇaṃ dvitīyam aindraṃ tṛtīyam aindrā caturtham
aindrāṇi sarvāṇi pṛṣṭhāni āgneyamagniṣṭomasāma āgneyaṃ prathamamuktham
aindra pare ukthe aindraḥ ṣoड़śī aindrī rātriḥ tridevatyaḥ sandhiḥ agni-
ruṣā aśvināviti atha sāmāntāḥ prātaḥ savane tāvatkhāraṃ gāyatra
mādhyandine svāraṃ gāyatra'
āmahoyavaroravayodhājayośanāni nidhana-
vanti bahirlidhana dūड़ā triṇidhanapadanidhanasvarāṇāṃ yathāsaṃkhyena ratha-
ntaraṃ nidhanavat vāmadevya hāikāraskhāraṃ nodhasampadanidhanaṃ kāleya-
miड़ānidhanaṃ śrārbhavaṃ gāyatraṃ khāraṃ saṃhitaṃ padanidhanaṃ sarpha hāikāra-
svāraṃ pauṣkalaṃ padanidhanaṃ yāvācaṃ hāikārasvāram śrāndhīgavaṃ madhye
nidhanaṃ aiड़ñca kāvaṃ padānukhāraṃ yajñāyajñīyaṃ vāṅ‌nidhanaṃ prathamamuktaṃ
sākama hāikārakhāraṃ saubharaṃ bahirṇidhanaṃ nārmedhaṃ hāikāraskhāraṃ
rātriravikṛtaiveti kalpanā śrāhīnikaṃ viśeṣamukkā eṣa nyāyaḥ sarva
eva jyotiṣṭomasya chandodaivatamāmāntayogaḥ || 1 ||

ukthottame 'nuṣṭubho nyāyyāḥ || 6.9.2 ||
ukthānāmuttame sāmni anuṣṭubha eva ṛcastā nyāyyāḥ evaṃ maśake-
nokthānteṣu kalpanā kṛtā || 2 ||

vichandasas tu vacanāt || 6.9.3 ||
vichandasa eva bhavantuprakthānte kasmāt vacanāt prakaraṇenāramya
vihitāḥ anuṣṭubho viprakaraṇe apare bruvate anuṣṭubhaḥ sarvatra bhavantura -
kthānta eva vichandamastu yatra vacanaṃ tatra bhavantītyarthaḥ || 3 ||

yaddevatyāsu stuvate sā stotradevatā || 6.9.4 ||
yavatyāsmṛnu stotreṇa stuvanti sā stotradevatā tat kimidamucyate evaṃ śrutiḥ atha khalvāśī: samṛddhirupasaraṇānītyadhikṛtya yāṃ
devatāmabhiṣṭoṣyaṃ syāt tāṃ devatāmupadhāvediti iha ca savanadevatā
ākuvādastotradevatā api śrūyate yathā vasūnāṃ prātaḥsavanaṃ rudrāṇaṃ
mādhyandinaṃ savanamādityānāñca viśveṣāñca devānāṃ tṛtīyasavanamiti
anyo jyotiṣe daivatayogaḥ tatrāyaṃ saṃśayaḥ kiṃ savanadevatā tenopa-
dikhate athavā stotradevatā śratha jyotiṣaṃ daivatayogaṃ gṛhītvopāsyā-
maha iti etasmin saṃśaya āha yaddevatyāsa stuvate sā stotradevateti
bhaktimātra savanadevatā || 4 ||

anvadhyāyam apavādaniśāmanam || 6.9.5 ||
ihe chandodaivatasāmāntayogān nyāyyān bruvate yathā
ghoड़śimato 'tirātrasya jyotiṣṭomasyeti anyatrāgneyoṣu sarvāṇi
stotrāṇi bruvate yathāgniṣṭutsu aindroṣu bhavantīti sarvāṇi stotrāṇi
aindrīṣu bhavanti tathā vaiśvadevīṣu bhavantīti atyantaraṃ tatkimeṣāṃ
stotrāṇamāgneyyādiṣu kriyamāṇānāṃ yā eva stotradevatā nyāyyā
nirdiṣṭā jyotiṣṭome tā eva bhavanti agnyādayo guṇabhṛtāḥ yathendro
gārhapatyamupatiṣṭhata iti agnireva bhavati tatra prādhānyena tat śruteḥ
indro guṇabhṛtaḥ evaṃ tat yathāgnyādidevatābhiḥ tanyāyastotradevatānāṃ
pratyāmnāya iti etasmin saṃśaya āī anvadhyāyamapavādaniśāmana-
miti adhyāyamanu anvadhyāyaṃ anvadhyāyamapavādo niśāmayitavyaḥ
anvadhyāyaṃ nyāyyānāṃ chandodaivatasāmāntānāmapavādaḥ yathā vandamāṃ
tāvat jagatyaḥ pratipado bhavanti jagatīnāṃ loke triṣṭubhaviṣṭubhāṃ
gāyatryaḥ triṣṭubhaḥ pratipadā bhavanti triṣṭuma lokeṃ jagatyo jaga-
tīnāṃ gāyavya iti tathā devatānāmapavādaḥ anvadhyāyamapavādaṃ
niśāmayitvā pramāṇīkarttavyaḥ goteca pratyanvadhyāyamapavādaniśāmanam
śratha yatkṛvitamiva niruditamiva sā vāk śavahaḥ kṣipraṃ mariṣyatīti
vidyādyastathādhote 'thaṃ yadā kranditamiva niṣkranditamiva mā mitrahaḥ
sarvajyāniñjasyatīti vidyādyasaṃthādhīta ityetadādinā cāṃpavādena
anvadhyāyamapavādaniśāmanam || 5 ||

svārāṇi hāikārasvārapadānusvārāṇi || 6.9.6 ||
iha sāmāntā uccante vakṣyati sarveṣāṃ tulya mantripāte jāmoti
kharo yeṣāṃ nidhanaṃ tāni khārāṇi tāni dvividhāni hāikāra-
khārāṇi padānukhārāṇi ca yathā vāmadevya hā dūkārasvāram ūśanaṃ
padānukhāram || 6||

tebhyo 'nyāny aiḍavāṅnidhanebhyaś ca nidhanavanti || 6.9.7 ||
tebhyaḥ svārebhya aiड़vāṅgidhanebhyaśca yānyanyāni sāmāni tāni
nidhanavantītyucyate yathā naudhamādīni aiड़āni rauravakāleyā-
doni || 7 ||

sarveṣāṃ tulyasaṃnipāte jāmi vāco 'nyatra || 6.9.8 ||
sarveṣāmeva teṣāṃ svārādīnāṃ tulyamantripāte samānanidhanamantri-
pāte jāmi bhavati jāmyatirekanāmavāliśasya vāsamānajātīya stha
vopajanaḥ vāṅgidhanayoranyatra jāmi bhavati vāṅdhinayostunyamantri
pātena jāmi bhavati yathānurūpāgniṣṭomasāmni dādaśāhe yajñāyajñī-
yamārbhavāntyakameva vṛhadagniṣṭomasāma || 8 ||

tan na kuryād anādeśe || 6.9.9 ||
tajjāmi na kuryyādanādeśe ādeśe yathādeśaṃ yathāsarvasvāre
pṛṣṭhe || 8 ||

daśarātrāt ṣāḍahikāny aṣṭamanavamayoś caitāny ahīnatantrāṇi || 6.9.10 ||

kalyanānyāyaḥ kṛtaḥ athā 'hīneṣu kalpayitavyeṣu daśarātrāt ṣāḍa़-
hikāni tantrāṇi aṣṭamanavamayoñcāhoḥ ahīnakalpanā pratyetavyā
||10||

jyotiṣaś ca goś caikāhike || 6.9.11 ||
ekāhikatantre jyotistantrañca gostantraṃ rathantarapṛṣṭhevvekāheṣu
kalpayitavyeṣu jyotistantra bṛhatpṛṣṭheṣu gostantram || 11 ||

kalpasaprāyāṇy ṛksāmāni yatra samavayanti tad āśīstantraṃ yathābhicaraṇīyeṣu || 6.9.12 ||

kalpasamāna prāyāṇi dravyāṇi yatra yasmin samavayanti tadā-
śīstantramityucyate yayābhicaraṇīyeṣu ṛtuṣu yatra yatra samānakalpa-
prāyaṃ dravyaṃ tadāśīstantramityevaṃ draṣṭavyaṃ yathā vaiśvajitaṃ tantram upa-
havyaṃ tanvaṃ mahāvrata tantram || 12 ||

bhūyiṣṭhaṃ tantralakṣaṇam || 6.9.13 ||
kiṃ punastantramityucyate bhūyiṣṭhaṃ tantralakṣaṇamiti yatra jyoti-
ṣṭome bhūyiṣṭhaṃ dravyaṃ prayujyate tat jyotistantraṃ evaṃ gostantramevaṃ ṣāड़hika-
ntantramityucyate || 13 ||

uttarayoḥ savanamukhīye gāyatryāv iti śaucivṛttiḥ || 6.9.14 ||
uttarayeāḥ pavamānayormadhyandinārbhavayārye savanamukhīyagāyatyā te
tantramiti evaṃ śaucivṛttirācāryo manyate || 14 ||

madhyandinīyānvācāryāṇām || 6.9.15 ||

yāṇāṃ tantramabhipretam || 15 ||

dvirātraprabhṛtiṣv ahīneṣu prāk ṣaḍahebhya uttamam uttamaikāhikam iti rāṇāyanīputraḥ || 6.9.16 ||
dvirātraprabhṛtayo ye ahīnāḥ prāk ṣaड़hebhyasteṣu sarveṣu uttamamutta
