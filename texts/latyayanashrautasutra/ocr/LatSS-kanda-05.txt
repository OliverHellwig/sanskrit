raśmir asīty etāv anuvākau stomabhāgāḥ || 5.11.1 ||
sāmprataṃ stotrānumantraṇamucyate, raśmirasi tanturamotyeto hāvanu
vāko ekasminnanuvāke dvādaśamantrāḥ paṭhitāḥ aparasminnekaviṃśatiḥ
veṣu mantreṣu bahuvacanaṃ stomabhāgā iti || 1 ||

taiḥ pṛthag anupūrvaṃ stotrāṇy anumantrayeta brahman stoṣyāmaḥ praśāstar ity ukte || 5.11.2 ||
kimeṣāṃ stomabhāgatvaṃ stomān bhajanta iti stomabhāgāḥ taikhaya-
sviṃgatā stomabhāgaiḥ mantraiḥ pṛthagekaikamanupūrvam ekaikaṃ stotramanumantra-
yeta brahmā brahman stoyyāmaḥ praśāstarityukte prastotrā ṣoḍubhimatyatirāce
ekonatriṃśastotrāṇi aptoryāni catvāryyatiriktastotrāṇi evamete
mantrāḥ eteṣāṃ stotrāṇāṃ pratimantraṇe eṣa samuditānāṃ trayastriṃśatāṃ
stotrāṇaṃ pratimantraṇavidhiruktaḥ | idānoṃ mantra vivekārthamāha || 2 ||

ūrdhvaṃ prathamād rātriparyāyād yatra syād amusmai tveti taṃ jitveti tatra brūyāt || 5.11.3 ||
viṃśatibhya ūīṃ stomabhāgebhyaḥ akṛtlā mantrāḥ paṭhitāḥ
dvitīyatṛtīyayoḥ paryāyayoḥ paṭhitvā tataḥ mantrī kṛtsaḥ paṭhitaḥ tataḥ

dvādaśasvapi || 3 ||

savitṛprasūtā bṛhaspataye srutety ekaikasyāntaḥ || 5.11.4 ||

pūrveṣāñcaiteṣāñca svāditi || 5 ||

ñcetyutam || 6 ||

’si vaspaṣṭirasi veṣaśrīrabhi maviprasūtā bṛhaspataye srutetyevam || 7||

traidhaṃ vibhajed iti dhānañjapyo vasuko 'si vaspaṣṭir asi veṣaśrīr asi vasukāya tvā vaspaṣṭaye tvā veṣaśriye tvā vasukaṃ jinva vaspaṣṭiṃ jinva veṣaśriyaṃ jinva savitṛprasūtā bṛhaspataye sruteti || 5.11.8 ||

ānantaryeṇetarāṇi || 5.11.10 ||

sūtrakāreṇa kalpitaṃ tadādhikaṃ syādikalpo vaitayormānamavājapeyamāmmroḥ

mānasavad vātiriktasāmānyāt || 5.11.13 ||

sarvānumantraṇena vānuktatvāt || 5.11.14 ||
