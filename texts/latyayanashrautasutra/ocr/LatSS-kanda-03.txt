atha prathamā kaṇḍikā |

ṣoḍaśisāmnā stoṣyamāṇo yathāsanam upaviśya havirdhānaṃ gatvā ṣoḍaḍaśigraham avekṣetodgātā yasmād anya iti || 3.1.1 ||
uktaṃ sārvakratukaṃ sāmānyavidhānaṃ sāmprataṃ ṣoड़śino guṇavidhāna-
mucyate sārvakratuko’yaṃ ṣoड़śī daśarātrike caturtho’hanyutpannaḥ atraiva
cāsya hiraṇyaṃ sampradānaṃ ṣoड़śinā stuvatityevamādayo dharmāḥ paṭhitāḥ
teṣāṃ vidhānaṃ vivaturāha ṣoड़śisāmrā stoyyamāṇa iti, nanu ukta
tvayā caturthe’hani ṣoड़śī utyanna iti tat prakaraṇe caiva dharmā vaktavyāḥ
syuḥ tat kimidaṃ viprakaraṇe guṇavidhānam ārabhyata iti yadā ca
tasminneva prakaraṇe yasmādanyo na paro asti jāta ityetadādi mantra
vidhāyoktam udgātā grahamavekṣeta iti dañcāveca vidhānaṃ kimartha-
miti cet atrocyate tadidam avekṣaṇaṃ prakaraṇe stotrānte uktaṃ tatrāyaṃ
saṃśayaḥ kiṃ kālaṃ prati tāvat kiṃ tasminneva kāle’vekṣaṇamathānyasmi
niti tathā kiṃ havirddhānasthasyeva athāvekṣaṇapravṛttiḥ sadasoti avekṣaṇaṃ
hi sadasi pravṛtta momānāṃ saumyasya ca tathā ete dharmā hiraṇyasampra-
dānādayaḥ caturthotpādāḥ ayañca ṣoड़śo prakaraṇāntareṣvapi śrūyate
sarvastomātirātrādiṣu tat kimete dharmāḥ prakaraṇasyasya bhavanti uta
prakaraṇāntarevvapi ṣoड़śanaṃ bhajatiti kutaḥ punarayaṃ saṃśayaḥ ucyate,
aprākṛtatvāt ṣoड़śinaḥ tena kiṃ prākṛto hi vidhirvvikṛtīrbhajata
aprākṛtastu na vacanādikkatīrbhajata iti ato’yamācārya etā-
nmohān parititīrṣuḥ sārvakratukānantaraṃ ghoड़śino vidhānamārabhate
katham etaṃ sārvakratukaṃ vidhānaṃ ṣoड़śinaḥ syāditi yadi hyetaṃ vidhāna-
mācāryaḥ prakaraṇa āmnāyasya tadviṣayamevābhaviṣyat tanmābhūdatā vipra-
karaṇa ārammaḥ ṣoḍa़śisānā stoṣyamāṇaḥ yathāsanamupaviśya havirddhānaṃ
gatvā ṣoड़śigrahamaveceta udgātā yasmādanya iti eṣa padānāṃ vicchedaḥ
artho vakṣyate ghoड़śimāmneti karttṛkaraṇalakṣaṇā ḍhatīyā stoṣyamāṇa
dūti bhaviṣyatkālopadeśaḥ yathāsanamupaviśya saṃsthāyāsane niḥsṛpya
samastopasthānaṃ kṛtvā kṛtlena vā akṛtvā vā svasminnāsana upaviśya
havirddhānaṃ gatvā ṣoड़śigrahamavekṣeta udgātā yasmādanya ityanena mantreṇa,
nanu ṣoड़śisāmneti sāmagrahaṇaṃ kimarthaṃ yadāyaṃ ṣoड़śiśabdaḥ kṛte’pi
sāmagrahaṇāt stotravācī bhaviṣyatyeva ayaṃ hi ṣoड़śiśabdaḥ triṣu
varttate stotre grahe saṃsthāyāmiti stotre tāvat yogāya trīṣu dipadāṣu
bṛhatā ghoड़śinā stuta iti graḍa़e prātaḥ savane ṣoड़śinaṃ gṛhītaṃ
tṛtīyamavane prajanayatīti svatrāyatane ṣoड़śī gṛhyatiti ca saṃsthāyāṃ
so’bravīt ṣoड़śī te’yaṃ yajñakratura sviti so’yaṃ ṣoड़śiśabdaḥ prayu-
jyamāna stotragrahasaṃsthāsu ityarthāt pratīyate tadiha ghoड़śinā
stoṣyamāṇa ityukte ṛte’pi sāmagrahaṇāt stavanasāmarthyāt sāmneti
gamyateva tat kimidaṃ sāmagrahaṇamiti atroccate asya ghoड़śina
ukthānantaravidhānaṃ
tasminneva krame dharmāḥ prāpnuvanti idañca ṣoड़śī-
sāmasaṃsthāntare’pi prayujyate tadasyānyasmin sthāne prayujyamānasya dharmā
bhavanti uta netyeṣa saṃśayaḥ so’yamācāryaḥ sāmagrahaṇaṃ karoti kathaṃ
sthānāntare’pi prayujyamānasya dharmā bhaveyuriti kva punarasya sthānāntare
prayoga: atyagniṣṭome ṣoड़śanaṃ gṛhīyāditi ||1||

evam eva prātaḥsavane || 3.1.2 ||
yo’yaṃ ṣoḍuśigrahasyāvetaṇe vidhirutaḥ ghoड़śisānā stoṣyamāṇa
ityetadādi tenaiva prātaḥ savane’pyavekṣeta yadi prātaḥ savane’pi gṛhyeta
prātaḥ savane ṣoड़śinaṃ gṛhītantaṃ tṛtīyasavane prajanayati || 2 ||

yadi cottarayor api gṛhṇīyuḥ || 3.1.3 ||

yadyuttarayorapi savanayoṭahīyuḥ tayorapya’nenaiva prakāreṇā-
vekṣeta || 3 ||

aśvaḥ kṛṣṇaḥ pūrvasyāṃ sadasodvāri pratyaṅmukhas tiṣṭhed iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 3.1.4 ||
uktaḥ aśvaḥ kṛṣṇa upatiṣṭhati sāmcyāyai tasya sthāna nirdeśa:
kriyate aśva iti pratītapadārthakaḥ śabdaḥ kṛṣṇa iti varṇanirdeśaḥ pūrvasyāṃ
sadamā dvāri pratyaṅmukhastiṣṭhediti dhānañjayaḥ ācārya āha || 4 ||

aparasyāṃ prāṅmukha iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 3.1.5 ||
aparasyāṃ sadaso dvāri prāṅmukho’śvastiṣṭhedevaṃ śāṇḍilya trācārya
āha || 5 ||

kṛṣṇābhāve śyāvaḥ || 3.1.6 ||
kṛṣṇasyāśvasyābhāve śyāvaḥ syāt śyāva ityanirukto varṣaḥ na kṛṣṇā
na śvetaḥ tayorantareṇa varttate || 6 ||

tadabhāve yaḥ kaścāśvaḥ || 3.1.7 ||
tasyābhāvaḥ tadabhāvaḥ taditi pūrvaniśaḥ tasya śyāvāśvasyā -
bhāve yaḥ kaścāśvaḥ syāt śratha yaḥ kaśca iti siddhe kiṃ punaraśvagrahaṇa-
madhikaṃ kriyate’śva iti uccate, anyatrāpyabhāve yaḥ kaśāśvaḥ syāt
yathā tasmā amumādityamaśvaṃ śvetaṃ kṛtvā dakṣiṇamānayanniti tathā
aśvagyāvo dakṣiṇa sahyanirutaḥ sabrahmaṇe deya dūti tatrāpi śvetaśyā-
vayorabhāve yaḥ kaśāśvaḥ syāt || 7 ||

gaur vājīvāśvābhāve || 3.1.8 ||

yadyaśvo na syāt gaurvājo vā bhavet athehāśvagrahaṇaṃ punaḥ kima-
rtham ucyate, tadabhāve yaḥ kañcāśva ityuktaṃ śvetaṇyāvayoraṣyabhāve madya-

topahavyayoḥ yaḥ kañcāśva iti tayorapi maurvājo vāśvābhāva iti
prāpnoti tannivṛtyarthamaśvagrahaṇaṃ karoti, kathaṃ, ṣoड़śinyeva gaurvājo
vāśvābhāve bhavet na tathātayorapoti || 8 ||
yo yaḥ sāmāṅgaṃ brūyāt sa hiraṇyaṃ dhārayet || 3.1.9 ||
uta hiraṇyasampradānaṃ ṣoḍa़śinā stuvata iti tadvivekārtha-
māha, yo yaḥ ghoड़bhimānaḥ aṅga brūyāt sa sa hiraṇyaṃ dhārayet
svasthāṃ svasyāṃ bhakto ||8||

udgātā nidhanam upayatsu || 3.1.10 ||
udgātā nidhanaṃ bruvatsu taṃ hiraṇyaṃ dhārayet || 10 ||

sarve vābhimṛśeyuḥ || 3.1.11 ||
sarve vā hiraṇyamabhimhaśeyurnidhanamupayantaḥ || 11 ||

avasarpati prathamaṃ paryāyaṃ gāyecchannam || 3.1.12 ||
avamarpatyāditya ṣoड़śamānaḥ prathamaṃ paryāyaṃ gāyet channam
śraprakāśam ana adhobhāve sṛgatā nanu kathametadgamyate yathā śrava-
marpatyādityadati ucyate upariṣṭādakṣyatyastamite tṛtīyamāvi-
riti || 12 ||

viṣite dvitīyaṃ madhyena || 3.1.13 ||
vigate site visite vigate śakke varṣe dvitīyaṃ paryāyaṃ gāyenmadhya-
mena svareṇa || 13 ||

astamite tṛtīyam āviḥ || 3.1.14 ||
astaṅgate āditye tṛtīyaṃ paryāyaṃ gāyet prakāśam || 14 ||

yadopākuryuḥ stuvīrann eva || 3.1.15 ||
dṛha prathamaṃ kālaniyamaḥ kṛtaḥ avasarpati prathamaṃ paryāya mitye-
tadādi so’yaṃ vikalpayati yadā yasmin kāle upākuryuradhvaryavaḥ
tasminneva kāle stuvīran na svaṃ kālamādriyeran apare viniveśaṃ
varṣayanti sandhikāle ghoḍa़śini atyagniṣṭome’hani atirāce rājā-
viti, apare bruvate vicāritaṃ, kimarthamidam ghoḍaśina udgāturiti
ucyate atrācāryyāṇasubhayathā pratipattiḥ yeṣāmaharante teṣāmaha-
nyupākaraṇaṃ yeṣāṃ rātrī teṣāṃ rājāviti ato’yamācārya āra-
bhate yadīpākuryuḥ tadā traprakāśaṃ stuvīraneveti atha taca kaḥ svara
ityucyate || 15 ||

ahani channaṃ rātrāv āviḥ || 3.1.16 ||

yadāinyupākuryuḥ tadā śraprakāśaṃ suvīran rātrī prakāśam || 16 ||

udgātre dadyād aśvahiraṇye dakṣiṇāvatsu || 3.1.17 ||
udgāce dadyād yajamānaḥ aśvahiraṇye aśvaśca hiraṇyañcāśva-
hiraṇye dakṣiṇavatma yajñeṣu atha dakṣiṇavatviti kimartham adati-
aiṣu dānaprasaṅga eva nāsti uccate, paśyatyayaṃ satreṣvaṅgadakṣiṇaprasaṅga
tena satyapyaṅgadakṣiṇāprasaṅge satreṣu ṣoḍaśinyaśvahiraṇye nivarttayati || 17 ||

satra eva grahasya bhakṣayeyur aindraṃ saha ity udasya harivadaharikāsu cet stuvīran || 3.1.18 ||
ṣoḍaśigrahasya satra eva bhakṣayeyuḥ nekāhāhoneṣu aindra saha itya-
nena yajuṣā udasya harivadaharikāsu stuvīran yo’smin yajuṣi hari-
vachandaḥ tam uttipya utsṛjya yadyaharikāsu ṛtu ṣoḍaśinaṃ stuvoran
atha punaḥ harivatīṣu sṭuvīran naiva harivachandamudastheyuḥ harivat kṛta-
meva mantraṃ prayuñjīran atraivaśabdaḥ kimarthaṃ satra eveti uccate, na
kevalaṃ ṣoḍaśigrahasya anye ye grahāsteṣāmapi ca matra eva bhakṣayeyuḥ
naikāhāhī neṣu etasthānyatarasmin vyahe nigrāhyāṇaṃ dravyānāṃ bhakṣa-
yeyurityetadādinā || 18 ||

anuṣṭup chandasā camasasya || 3.1.19 ||
ṣoḍaśini stotre yaścamamaḥ tasyānuṣṭup cchandamā bhakṣayeyuḥ ||18||

pūrveṇa vā || 3.1.20 ||
mantreṇa camamasya bhakṣayeyuḥ aindra maha ityanena udasya harivada -
harikāsu stuvīrannityanuṣajyateva || 20 ||

tathā kurvann indraś ca samrāḍ varuṇasya rājā tau te bhakṣyaṃ cakratur agra evaṃ tayor aham anubhakṣyaṃ bhakṣyāmi vāg juṣāṇā somasya tṛpyatv iti grahasya || 3.1.21 ||
tathā kurvan pūrveṇa mantreṇa camamasya bhakṣaṇam indraśca samrāruṇasya
rājetyetadādinā yathāsvatritena mantreṇa grahasya bhattayeyuḥ || 21 ||

indram iti vā vāg devī somasya pibatv ity etad adhikayā || 3.1.22 ||
indramiḍurivadityetayarthā vāgdevī somasya pibatvityetadadhikayā bhayediti vikalpaḥ || 22 ||

aindraṃ saha iti camasasya sattre triṣṭupchandasā vājapeyasāmni bhakṣayed iti gautamaḥ || 3.1.23 ||

vājapeyasānni triṣṭup cchandamā ityeva chanda ādeśaṃ kṛtvā bhakṣaye-
diti gautama trācārya śrāha tacchandasvāt stotrasva || 23 ||

anuṣṭupchandaseti dhānañjapyaśāṇḍilyau || 3.1.24 ||
anuṣṭupcchandamā vājapeyamāni bhattayediti dhānṛṣyaśāṇḍi-
khyāvācāryāvāhatuḥ yadūrddhamukthebhyaḥ anuṣṭubhaḥ sabhāga iti anu-
ṣṭup cchandamā rātrau || 24 ||

anuṣṭupchandasā rātrau || 3.1.25 ||
na bhavayeyuḥ anuṣṭubhatvāt rātreḥ || 25 ||

pṛthak tṛcebhyaḥ saṃdhau hiṃkuryuḥ || 3.1.26 ||
pṛthaṅnānetyarthaḥ nānātvacebhyaḥ mandhī hiṃkuryuḥ nānādevayavā
cānām || 26 ||
iṣṭayajuṣa iti bhakṣyaḥ || 3.1.27 ||

iṣṭayajuṣa ityanena yajuṣā mandho bhakṣyaḥ || 27 ||

gāyatrachandasātiriktastotreṣv iti gautamaḥ || 3.1.28 ||
atiriktastotreṣu gāyatracchandamā bhataṇamiti evaṃ gotama
ācāryo manyate samāpteṣu bhakṣakandaḥsu punarupakrame ko’nyo gāyatryāt
khāditi || 28 ||
pṛthagbhakṣachandobhir anupūrvam iti dhānañjapyaśāṇḍilyau || 3.1.29 ||

yāni catvāri bhakṣachandāṃsi taiḥ pṛthak pṛthak ānupūrvyeṇa caturṣvana-
vecca tadānupūryeṇa bhakṣayeyuriti dhānañjaṣyaśāṇḍilyāvācāryāvā-
hatuḥ sambhavāca sarveṣāṃ bhakṣakandamām arthāca, ko’rthaḥ agnyādīnāṃ
devatānāñca chandobhāvāt || 28 ||

anuṣṭupchandasā vā || 3.1.30 ||
mantreṇa bhattyeyuḥ anuṣṭupbhāganimittatvāt yadūrddhamukthebhyaḥ anu-
ṭubhaḥ sabhāga iti || 30 ||

achandasā vā || 3.1.31 ||
achandamā vā mantreṇa bhattayeyuḥ upayuktatvāt bhakṣyachandasām ||31||

saṃdhivad vā || 3.1.32 ||
yathā vā mantrī tathā vā bhakṣayeyuḥ pravṛttatvāt mantrasya anupadeśādvānyeṣām || 32 ||
iti tṛtīyasya prathamā kaṇḍikā |

atha dvitīyā kaṇḍikā |

āhitāgnir asomayājī paraṃ somena yājayitvā paribhakṣaṃ kurvīta || 3.2.1 ||

uktāḥ ṣoḍaśino dharmāḥ somasya ca bhatavidhānamuktaṃ yāvadati-
rikastotrāṇoti sāmpratamupari bhatavidhānamucyate āhitāgniḥ ā-
hitā agnayo yena sa āhitāgniḥ somayājo yena somena iṣṭāṃ
sa momayājī na somayājī aseomayājī param anyaṃ somena yāja-
yitvā aparibhakṣaṃ kurvīta aparibhakṣa iti karmaṇa: saṃjñā parasyāgniṣu
yena somo bhakṣitaḥ tena svāgmayaḥ paribhakṣitāḥ pitaraca tadapari-
bhakṣārthamidaṃ karmārabhyate nanu śrāhitāgnigrahaṇaṃ kimarthamiti yadā
śravacyadasomayājoti nayanāhitāgneḥ somayāga: syāt anyārthe
na vāhitāgnitvaṃ vijñāpyate iha gārhapatyadakṣiṇaṇagyāivanīyeṣu śrā-
jyādhiśrayaṇṭramānidhānājyagrahaṇanyādecyante jatāyāṃ prātarāja-
tāvājyaṃ gārhapatye’dhiśrityodagudāsayet kaṃse camase vā dramānavadhāya
dakṣiṇāmābupamādayet ruphyadarbhamuṭī ca paścimena gārhapatya gatvā pañcā-
dāhavanīyasyopaviśya suveṇa sucyājyaṃ gṛhīyādyāvadgṛhītī syāditi
śraya ca karmaṇo nāntareṇāhitāgneḥ prasiddhiḥ tadevamarthādāhitāgnive
middhe yaducyate āhitāgnirityetadanarthakaṃ tasmādāhitāgnigrahaṇaṃ na
karttavya kriyamāṇe kāraṇaṃ vaktavyam uccate, yadyāhitāgnigrahaṇaṃ na
kuryyāt anāhitāgneryājanaprasaṅgo na syāt so’yamāhitāgnigrahaṇaṃ
karoti āhitāgnirasomayājī paraṃ somena yājayitvā itthaṃ karma
kuryāt anāhitāgniyajayitvāpi para na vedaṃ kuryyāt yāvatā
tasyāgnayo naiva manti yeṣāmaparibhakṣārthannaivedaṃ karma kriyate prathā-
somayājīti kimarthaṃ yāvatā vakṣyati somayājyapīti śāṇḍilya
iti somayājigrahaṇaṃ kathamucyate aniṣṭaprathamasyedaṃ karma syāt
iti somayājī hi anāhitāgnirapi bhavati satreṣu tathāpyamā-
vasomayājyeva śratha paragrahaṇaṃ kimarthaṃ yadā yājayitva siddhametat
paramiti uccate, paragrahaṇaṃ parameva yājayitvā na pitaraṃ vā putra
vā bhrātaraṃ veti nirguṇaḥ svajana: ebhyo yo’nyaḥ paraḥ para eva saḥ ||1||

somayājy apīti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 3.2.2 ||

momayājyapyetat karma kuryāditi śāṇḍilyaḥ trācārya śrāha, sāmpra-
tamasyāparibhacasya karmaṇaḥ karttavyatocyate || 2 ||

hutāyāṃ prātarāhutāv ājyaṃ gārhapatye 'dhiśrityodag udvāsayet || 3.2.3 ||
hutāyāṃ prātarāhutau ṛ̱te’gnihotre ājyaṃ ghṛtaṃ gārhapatyāgnā-
vadhiśritya udak udāsayet || 3 ||

kaṃse camase vā drapsān avadhāya dakṣiṇāgnāv apasādayet || 3.2.4 ||
kaṃse vā bhājane dārave vā camase dadhidramānavadhāya dadhyavaya-
vān daciṇāgneḥ samope sthāpayet || 4 ||

sphyadarbhamuṣṭī ca || 3.2.5 ||
sphyaśca darbhamuṣṭiśca sphādarbhamuṣṭī dakṣiṇāgne: samīpe sthāpayet ||5||

paścimena gārhapatyaṃ gatvā paścād āhavanīyasyopaviśya sruveṇa srucy ājyaṃ gṛhṇīyād yāvad gṛhītī syāt || 3.2.6 ||

sān svadarbhamuṣṭo ca sthāpayitvā anantaraṃ paścimena pradeśena
gārhapatyasya gatvā śrāhavanīyasya paścime pradeśa upaviṣya yattadājyaṃ
gārhapatye’dhiśrityodagudāsitaṃ tasmāt subeṇa suci gṛhīyāt yāvad-
gṛhītī sthāt yadi caturgrahotī yadi pañcagṭahītī || 6 ||

taj juhuyād imām agne śaraṇiṃ mīmṛṣo na imam adhvānaṃ yam agāma dūram āpiḥ pitā pramatiḥ somyānāṃ bhrimir aspṛṣi kṛnmatyānāṃ svāheti || 3.2.7 ||
yadi caturgṛhītaṃ yadi pañcagṭahītaṃ tahuyādimāmane itye
tadādinā yathāsūcitena mantreṇa || 7 ||

evam eva gṛhītvāpāṃ puṣpam asty oṣadhīnāṃ raso 'gneḥ priyatamā tanūr indrasya priyatamaṃ chaviḥ svāheti || 3.2.8 ||
evameva gṭaholā yadi caturgrahītaṃ yadi pañcagṛhītaṃ apāṃ puṣpam ity etadādinā yathāsvatritena mantreṇa juhuyāt || 8 ||

ājyasya pūrvān grahān gṛhītvā dadhna uttamaṃ viśvebhyo devebhyaḥ svāheti || 3.2.9 ||
śrājyasya pūrvān grahān gṛhīla yadi caturgṛhīta cīnājyasya

saprabhṛtitvāt uktaṃ saprabhṛtayo bhavantīti tāḥ śvasanādayaḥ || 27 ||

tasya bṛhatyām araṇye geyāni tṛceṣu pṛṣṭhyaṃ ca || 3.6.28 ||
tasya grahaṇaṃ yadi jyotiṣṭomaḥ payāsaḥ yadi sāptamikaḥ tasya
bṛhatyāmekādaśasāmāni paṭhitāni teṣāṃ ḍhacaikarcatvaklṛptiḥ kalpayati
yānyaranye geyāni tāni ḍhaceṣu sya : pṛṣṭhyañca anyāynyekaceṣu || 28 ||

vāsiṣṭham adhyāsyāyām || 3.6.29 ||

tat kimidamārabhyate anuprāptameva vāsiṣṭhamadhyāsyāyām śrācā-
vyairapi prakaraṇe’dhyāsyāyāmeva gītam ucyate niyamārthamārabhyate
vakṣyate saptaiva ḍhaceṣu yairajāmītyaparamiti na tat kalpe vāsiṣṭhaṃ syāt
śradhyāsyāviṣayameva vāsiṣṭhamiti || 28 ||

vaiyaśvaṃ prāgbhargadābhīśavaṃ yaśasaḥ || 3.6.30 ||
pāṭhakramaṃ vikalpayati śrajāmyarthena vaiyaśvaṃ prāgabhargādbhavet abhī-
śavaṃ yaśasaḥ prāgiti varttate || 30 ||

saptaiva tṛceṣu yair ajāmīty aparam || 3.6.31 ||
aparaḥ kalpaḥ sṛhatyāṃ saptaiva ṭaceṣa syuḥ caiḥ kriyamāṇairjāmi na
bhavati etadaparaṃ śrācāryamataṃ prathamaḥ kalpo yathāmnātānāṃ kriyā-
śeṣe vikalpaḥ || 32 ||

dīrghatamaso 'rko 'ntyaḥ sāmarājaṃ vā || 3.6.32 ||
ante dīrghatamaso’ka: sāmarājañca tayosa dvayorekasmin sambhavo
nāsti tasmād vikalpaḥ syāt ekārthavāt || 32 ||

pṛṣṭhasyottarayor dīrghatamaso 'rko māṇḍavaṃ caiḍaṃ sāmarāje 'ntye || 3.6.33 ||
yadā tāvat dīrghatamaso’’ntyaḥ syāt tadā sāmarājasyābhāva
eva yadā punaḥ sāmarājamanyaṃ syāt tadā pṛṣṭhasyottarayoḥ stotrīyayoḥ
dīrghatamaso’ka: svāt māṇḍavaṃ caiḍa' dīrghatamaso’rkaḥ arko’nugrahārthaḥ
māṇḍavaṃ stomapūrṇārtham || 33 ||
iti tṛtīyasya ṣaṣṭhī kaṇḍikā |

atha saptamī kaṇḍikā |

daśamasya bahiṣpavamānaṃ dvitīyaprabhṛtīnāṃ pañcānām ahnām anurūpāḥ prathamāc ca bahiṣpavamānaṃ tan navarcam || 3.7.1 ||

daśānāṃ pūraṇaḥ daśamaḥ daśamasyeti sambandhalakṣaṇā ṣaṣṭho bahi-
vyavamānamiti stotropadeśaḥ dvitīyaprabhṛmīnāmiti nidadhyarupadeśaḥ
prabhṛtiśabdaḥ ādyābhidhāyī pañcānāmiti saṃkhyā hāmiti aha-
nirdeśa: anurūpā iti bahiṣpavamānaṃ tannavacaṃ navarcamiti maṃjñā
bahiṣpavamānasya ādyadvitīyāntyāstṛcaḥ pratipadanurūpaparyāmaḥ prathamā-
ccāhnaḥ bahiṣpavamānantannavacaṃ navarcamiti saṃjñā etaddaśamasyāho bahi-
ṣpavamānam atrocyate yadā tāvadeva bahiṣpatramānaṃ dāśamikaṃ klṛpta-
meva sarvaṃ brāhmaṇe pañcānāmanāmanurūpaiḥ pratyavayanti navarce bhavanti
yā evāmū: prayacchan yā śrāvadadhātīti ca sambandho’sya darśitaḥ
prāthamikasya navarcastha vāruṇyekā bhavati ādityekā bhavatīti ca
liṅgacodanā tat kimidaṃ siddhe sati punarārabhyate daśamasya bahiṣpava-
mānamiti ucyate, trācāryyasyeyaṃ pravṛttiḥ yatra hi kālo vā
viśeṣo vā kañcidvivakṣito bhavati tatra siddhasyāpi sato vidhiḥ puna-
rupādānaṃ karoti yathā ekatrike madyaḥ sarvaṃ kuryurekadīkṣastyupasatka
iti gautamastasya gāyatrapārśvamārbhavo yā ruceti purojityamiti
śāṇḍilya iti etad vivatuḥ siddhamidaṃ gāyatrapārśva punarāmanati
evamiha vidhiśeṣa vivakṣuḥ siddhopādānaṃ karoti kaḥ punarvidhiḥ
śiṣyate yo vaktavyaḥ uccate, brāhmaṇaṃ pañcānāmahāmanurūpaiḥ pratyavaya-
nīti tathā vaktavyam etadarthaṃ siddhamapi santaṃ punarādadate ucyate
yadyevaṃ siddhe’pi sati punarādadate daśamagrahaṇaṃ tāvanna karttavyaṃ kasmāt
nāvamikaṃ vidhānamukta śeṣo’pi vidhirucyamānaḥ saṃkhyānāt daśa-
mādanyatra ca bhavet tasmāddaśamagrahaṇaṃ śakyamavatu bahiṣpavamānagrahaṇaṃ
śakyameva vaktu bahiṣpavamānasya grahaṇasya kiṃ kāraṇaṃ tacchakyatvāt tat
vivaktavyam atra heāturājyādārabhya susamiddho na śrāva hetyetasmāddāśa-
mikāni stotrāṇi pratyakṣaśiṣṭāni tasmāt kāraṇaṃ na vaktavyaṃ dvitīya-
prabhṛtīnāmityetadapyanārabdhavyameva yasmādvitīyaprabhṛtiṣvahaḥsu vahi-
ṣyavamānadvitīyāstṛcasteṣāmanurūpā iti saṃjñā pavaskhendovṛṣāsuta
ityetadādīnāṃ ahāmanurūpā ityetacca na vaktavyaṃ kasmāt ukta pañcā-
nāmahāmanurūpaiḥ pratyavayantīti prathamācca bahiṣpavamānantanavarca-
mityetadapi na vaktavyaṃ kasmāt uktaṃ navarce bhavatīti yā evāmū:
prayacchan yā śravadadhāti tā etā udasyatīti prāthamikenaiva
bahiṣpavamānena sambandhaṃ darśayati tasmāt sarvameva sūtraṃ nārabdhavyam
ārambha vā prayojanaṃ vaktavyam atreocyate, yaduktaṃ nārabhavyamityārabhavya-
miti brūmaḥ śeṣavidhyarthaṃ yattāvaducyate daśamagrahaṇaṃ na kartavyamiti
atra brūmaḥ karttavyameva chandomā iti paṭhati abhyasaṅgyaḥ pañcāsa-
tvāraḥ chandomā viśvajidatirātra iti tatredaṃ saṃkhyānato navamaṃ tatrāyaṃ
maṃśayaḥ kimatra dāśamikaṃ bahiṣpavamānaṃ bhavatyata utpattikatvāt
nāvamikaṃ mābhūditi yāvatā nidānakāreṇāpi vicāritaṃ kathaṃ
daśamadharmā mānasamiti karttavyā ityeke taccāharjāyata iti aka-
rtavya ityaparamadaśamañca luptaśca daśarātra iti so’yamācārtho’niya
mārthaṃ daśamagrahaṇaṃ karoti kathaṃ adaśame’pyetasmin bahiṣpavamānaṃ
syāditi, atha yaduktaṃ bahiṣpavamānagrahaṇaṃ na karttavyamityatra brūmaḥ
bahiṣpavamānagrahaṇaṃ kriyate niyamārthaṃ kathaṃ etasminnahani na kadā-
cidanyaṃ bahiṣpavamānaṃ syāditi kutaḥ punarāśaṅkā yathānya bahi-
vyavamānaṃ syāditi ucyate, maśakaḥ paṭhati dvādaśāhavicāreṣu api
vā pratipadāvevānyonyasyāḥ sthānaṃ vyatiharediti na kadācidapi
bahiṣpavamānasya vikāraḥ syāt daśame’hanīti, atha yadukta dvitīya-
prabhṛtonāmiti na vaktavyamityatra brūmaḥ anyonyasya sthānaṃ dvitīya-
prabhṛtīnāmeva syuḥ, śratha yadukta pañcānāmityatra brūmaḥ śravidyamāne-
vvapi pañcānāmeva kuryuḥ yathā chando bhavati daśarātre, śratha yadukta-
mahāmiti na vaktavyamityatra brūmaḥ aharvyatyāso’pyānupūrvyeṇa syāt,
atha yaduktamanurūpā iti na vaktavyamityatra brūmaḥ anurūpagrahaṇaṃ
sāptamikapratiṣedhārthaṃ kathaṃ samūhite dvādaśāhe etā anurūpā na syu-
riti yeṣāmevānurūpā saṃjñā tā eveti, atha yaduktaṃ prathamācca bahi-
ṣpavamānaṃ tannavarcamityetadapi na vaktavyamityatra brūmaḥ avachinnabahi-
ṣpavamānaṃ klṛptaṃ chandomā mati daśarātre tanmābhūditi tasmāt sūtra-
mārabdhavyam || 1 ||

anurūpeṣu tṛcā pratilomā ṛcā navarca iti gautamaḥ || 3.7.2 ||

uktaṃ pañcānāmahāmanurūpaiḥ pratyavayanti yathābhyāruhya pratyavaro-
hettathā taditi tatpratyavarāhaṇaṃ kiṃ ṛkprātilomyena athavā dvacaḥ
prātilombeneti ucyate anurūpeṣu tvacā pratilomā ṛcā navarce ṣāṣṭi-
kādanurūpādārabhya prātilomyena tāvadyāvadvitīyaka iti taiḥ pratya-
varohaḥ na ṛcāmapi trasṛkṣata pravājinaḥ pavamānasya viśvavitpava-
māno ajījattadete asṛgramindavaḥ pavakhendohaṣāsuta ityeṣa karma-
kramaḥ tata ṛcaḥ pratilomāḥ syuḥ upāsmai gāyatānara ityata ārabhya
yāvadasya pratnāmanudyutamiti evaṃ pratyavarāhaṇaṃ gautama ācāryo
manyate || 2 ||

sarvatra tṛcā iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 3.7.3 ||

sarvatra sarvasmin bahiṣpavamāne ḍhacā prātilombena dhānañjaya śrā-
cāryo manyate anurūpāṇāṃ yathoktaṃ navarce tu upaśikṣāya tasthuṣa eṣa
pratnena janmanāsya pratnāmanudyutamityevantṛcaḥ prātilomyam || 3||

yathādhītaṃ navarcam iti śāṇḍilyāyanaḥ || 3.7.4 ||
anurūpāṇāṃ yathoktameva prātilomya navarce tu yathādhītameveti
evaṃ śāṇḍilyāyana ācāryo manyate || 4 ||

sarvatrarcaḥ pratilomā iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 3.7.5 ||

sarvatrānurūpeṣu navarcevyṛcaḥ pratilomāḥ syurityevaṃ śāṇḍilya śrā
cāryo manyate, dvitīyakādanurūpādārabhya yāvat ṣāṣṭika iti ete
ṛkpratilomāḥ syuḥ anyathā hi yadi ṣāṣṭikādārabhdheran evamubhaya-
prātilomyaṃ syāt ṛk prātilomyantṛcaprātilomyañca navarce tu sarva-
hakapratilomameva syāt upāsmai gāyatānara ityata ārabhya yāva-
dasya praneti || 5 ||

apratibhām etasminn ahany anuṣṭummātrāṃ kṛtvācakṣīran || 3.7.6 ||
apratibhāṃ na pratibhām anavasthānamityarthaḥ yadetasminnahani
pratibhāyāḥ stotrāṅga kiñcittadanuṣṭummācāṃ kṛtvācakṣīran uktaṃ yāva
tyanuṣṭuptāvatoṃ vācaṃ sampādya vibrūyuriti śratha mātrāgrahaṇaṃ kimartha-
miti ucyate ṛṛknyāyannādriyeran dvātriṃśanmātrāṃ kṛtvācakṣīran ||6||

jyāyasi chandasi lumpeyur akṣarāṇi kanīyasy abhyasyeyuḥ || 3.7.7 ||
jyāyastvaṃ nāma vṛddhiḥ kanīyastvaṃ hāmaḥ jyāyami indasyākhyā-
tavye anuṣṭumānāṃ kṛtvā adhikānyakṣarāṇi lumpeyuḥ kanīyasī abhya-
syeyuḥ ke yeṣāmugātṛṇāmidaṃ karmma upadiṣṭam || 7 ||
agniṣṭomasāmnā stutvā prāk patnīsaṃyājebhyoyadebhiḥ pra-
stute parārdhya brajita syāttadgatvā pratyāvrajya manasāmutsāha

huteṣu patnīsaṃyājeṣu gārhapatya udgātā juhuyād upasṛjan dharuṇaṃ mātre mātaraṃ dharuṇo dhayan rāyaṣpoṣam iṣam ūrjam asmāsu dīdharat svāheti pūrvam || 3.7.8 ||

agniṣṭomasānā stutvā prāk patnosaṃyājebhyaḥ yadebhirudgādabhiḥ
prastute some prasavādārabhya sarvairdū raṅgataṃ syāt tadgatvā pratyāvrajya mana-
sānutsāhe yadyutmāheo na syāt gamanāgamane manasā gamanāgamane
kṛtvā huteṣu patnīmaṃyājeṣu gāīpatya udgātā juhuyāt upasṛjandharuṇaṃ
mātra etadādinā yathāsatritena mantreṇa udgādagrahaṇaṃ na sarve eva ||8||

svāhākāreṇottarām || 3.7.9 ||
uttarāmājatiṃ svāhākāreṇa juhuyāt || 8 ||

āhavanīyaṃ gatvā paścāt tiṣṭhanto 'yaṃ sahohā iti trir udgātā gāyeta nidhanam itarāv anūpeyātām || 3.7.10 ||

gārhapatye udgātā jatlā adhvaryuṇa sampreṣite āhavanīye gatvā tasya
paścime pradeśe tiṣṭhanto yadacyate tat kuryuḥ kiṃ punastat tryaṃ sahāhā
ityetatmāma trirudgātā gāyet nidhanamitarau prastotapratihattarī


udgātāramupayantaṃ anupeyātāṃ gānagrahaṇaṃ uktamatichandamāhavanīyamupa-
tiṣṭhanta iti ṛcā mābhūditi || 10 ||

yajamānasaṃcareṇa gatvā pūrvayā dvārā sadaḥ praviśeyuḥ || 3.7.11 ||
yajamānasya yaḥ sañcarastena gatvā nodgātṛsañcareṇa pūrvayā dvārā sadaḥ praviśeyuḥ || 11 ||
iti tṛtīyasya saptamī kaṇḍikā |

atha aṣṭamī kaṇḍikā |

yadā adhvaryur upākuryād atha mānasena stuvīran nāyaṃ gāḥ pṛśnir akramīd iti gāyatreṇa pṛthak stotrīyābhyo hiṃkāraṃ trikena stomenāstomayogā yajamānavācanena || 3.8.1 ||
yasmin kāle śradhvaryurupākaraṇaṃ karoti athānantaraṃ mānasena
stotrena sṭuvīran manasā kriyata iti mānasam uktaṃ manamopāvartta-
yatyetadādi śrayāḥ pṛśnirakramodityetasmiṃstace gāyatreṇa sāmnā
ukta sārparājñā ṛgmiḥ stuvantīti etā sārparājyaḥ pṛthak stotrī-
yābhyo hiṅkāraṃ pṛthak nānetyarthaḥ stotrīyābhyo hiṃkāraṃ kuryu: trike
stomena ukta parācībhiḥ stuvantīti evaṃ parākkam trastomayogā yaja-
mānavācanena nātra stomayojanaṃ sthāt na ca yajamānavācanam || 1 ||

pratihāravelāyāṃ pratihartāpānyāt || 3.8.2 ||

pratihāradeśakāle pratihata apānyāt ukta tasmādvayaṃ prāṇena
karoti prāṇiti cāpāniti ca tadapānasaṃjñakavāyurantaḥ praveśayet ||2||

uttamāṃ prastutyaiṣeti hotāram īkṣeta || 3.8.3 ||
uttamāṃ stotrīyāṃ prastutyaiṣetyanena hotāramocet || 3 ||

sarvam etasmin stotre manasā kuryuḥ || 3.8.4 ||

samīkṣaṇena vijñāpayantaḥ || 3.8.5 ||
yatkiñcidasmin stotre purastājjapādi tatsarvaṃ mānasaṃ kuyuḥ samī-
kṣaṇena vijñāpayantaḥ brahmaṃstoṣyāmaḥ praśāstarityataḥ ārabhya samīkṣaṇa-
mātreṇa vijñāpanaṃ kurvantaḥ sarvapreṣeṣu prajñāpanaṃ manasaiva syāditi ||4||5||

yam adhvaryur bhakṣaṃ prayacchan manyeta tasya manasopahūya kas tvā kaṃ bhakṣayāmīti bhakṣayeyuḥ || 3.8.6 ||

yamadhvaryurbhakṣaṃ prayacchanmanyeta yadi grahaṃ yadi camamaṃ tasya manasopahaya
kastvā kaṃ bhattayāmīti bhakṣayeyuḥ manamaiva ukta graha vā gṛhītvā camamaṃ
vonnīya stotramupāvarttayatīti na bhakṣaṃ stotreṇa sambadhyate’taḥ puna-
rmanasetyāca || 6 ||

brahmodyaṃ vadantīti gāyatrīṃ nirbrūyur iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 3.8.7 ||
uktaṃ brahmādyaṃ vadanti brahmavarcasa eva pratitiṣṭhantīti kiṃ punarbrahmādya-
miti ucyate gāyatrīṃ tribrūyuriti dhānañjaya dūti gāyalyāstrirvacanaṃ
brahmodyaṃ mā ca gāyatrī paribhāṣitā taṃ sa trāpyāyayedabhimṛśyāpyāya-
khetyuktāha sarve vaitayā gāyatryeti || 7 ||

caturhotāram abhipretyaitad iti gautamaḥ || 3.8.8 ||
caturhotāraṃ brahmodyamabhipretya etadbrāhmaṇam iti gautama ācāryo
manyate || 8 ||

yathāśvatrirātra iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 3.8.9 ||
svāhākṛtibhiśca ṛte pūrveṇa sada upaviśya brahmodya vadeyuriti
etadbrahmādyamihāpi syāditi śāṇḍilya śrāha || 8 ||

etasyāṃ velāyāṃ varān vṛṇīran manasā yān iccheyuḥ || 3.8.10 ||

etasyāṃ velāyāṃ varān vṛṇoranmanasā yo yasyeṣmitaḥ sa taṃ varaṃ vṛṇīte || 10 ||


uttarata udgātaudumbarīṃ gṛhṇīyāt paścāt pratihartā dakṣiṇato brahmā purastād itare sarve || 3.8.11 ||
uttare pradeśe’vasthita udgātā audumbarī gṛhoyāt anvārabhet
paścātpratihata paścime pradeśe cāvasthitaḥ pratihartā śranvārabhet dakṣi
to brahmā anvārabhet pūrvapradeśe’vasthitā dūtare sarve anvārabheran ||11

atha japeyur iha dhṛtir iha svadhṛtir iha rantir iha ramadhvaṃ mayi dhṛtir mayi svadhṛtir mayi ramo mayi ramadhvam iti vā || 3.8.12 ||
athaśabda ānantarye anvārabhyāthānantaraṃ japeyuḥ dūha dhṛtiritye-
tadādi yathāsūcitaṃ mantra mayi dhṛtirityetadādi vā || 12 ||

ubhau vā || 3.8.13 ||
ubhau vā mantrī japeyuḥ || 13 ||

vāgyatā apidhāya dvāre āsīrann ā nakṣatrapravacanāt || 3.8.14 ||
evaṃ japitvānantaraṃ vāgyatā vācaṃ yamayitvā apidhāya dvāre

āsīran ākutaḥ kālāt śrā nakṣatrapravacanāt yāvannacatra prokta-
miti uktaṃ vācaṃ yacchanti dugdhānīva vai tarhi chandāṃsi yātayāmā-
nyantagatāni tānyeva tadrasenāpyāyayantyayo’śvastanāmevābhisantanva-
ntoti || 14 ||

prokte nakṣatre pūrvayā dvārā niṣkramya subrahmaṇyāpraṇavair vācaṃ visṛjerann iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 3.8.15 ||
uktam āsīran śrānacatrapravacanāditi tato’nyena prokte nakṣatre
pūrvayā dvārā sadamo niṣkramya subrahmaṇyāpraṇavaiḥ subrahmaṇyomityebhiḥ
vāgvisarga kuryuriti dhānañjayaḥ śrācāryo manyate || 15 ||

śrutvaivāhūyamānāyā iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 3.8.16 ||
vamatīvarīpariharaṇasyānte subrahmaṇyenāhūyamānāyāḥ subrahmaṇyāyāḥ
praṇavān śrutvā vācaṃ visṛjeyurityavaṃ śāṇḍilya śrācāryo manyate
na svayaṃ praṇavaṃ brūyuriti || 16 ||

varavaraṇavāgyamane yajamāna eva kuryād ahīneṣu || 3.8.17 ||
varavaraṇañca vāgyamanañca te varavaraṇavāgyamane yajamāna eva
kuryādahīneṣu evaśabdo’vadhāraṇārthaḥ sarvakarttakamidaṃ karmopariṣṭhaṃ tad
yajamānaḥ kuryādahīneṣu iti sarvartvijo na kuryuḥ nanu yadā dāvevā-
honau tāṇḍake yayodaśamamahaḥ tayośca śrahīnadādaśāhachandomavāṃśca
daśarātraḥ tat kimidaṃ bahuvacanaṃ śrahīnedhviti ucyate śākhāntarābhi
prāyeṇa athavā pauṇḍarike catuṣṭome ca vicāritaṃ kathaṃ daśamadhama
mānasamityakarttavyā ityeke tatvena nāmadheyenaikāha ādiśyate catu
ṣṭomo’gniṣṭome ityathāpi na dārśamikaṃ liṅga dṛśyate yathaitad bahi-

aparayā dvāraudumbarīm āsandīmauñjavivānām atihareyuḥ || 3.12.1 ||
uktam upasthāya sadaḥ praviśeyuriti sāmprataṃ pṛṣṭhena stavanamuktam
āsandīmāruhyodgāyatīti tasyā āmandyāḥ praveśanamucyate aparayā
dvārā sadamaḥ paścimayetyarthaḥ audumbarīmāmandīmūdumbarasya vikāraḥ
audumbarī āsandī saṃjñā trāsyate asyāmityāmandoṃ maujavivānā-
matihareyuḥ māñja vivānaṃ muñjebhyo nirvṛttāṃ moñjavivānāmatihareyuḥ
praviśeyuḥ || 1 ||

yajñiyasya vṛkṣasyaudumbarābhāve || 3.12.2 ||
yadyudumbaro na syāt yasya kasyacit yajñiyasya vṛkṣasyāvayava-
niṣpannā syāt śrāmandī yajñamarhati yajñiyaḥ || 2 ||

muñjābhāve dārbham || 3.12.3 ||
yadi muñjo na syāt darbhamayaṃ vivānaṃ syāt prabhāvo’nupapattiḥ ||3||

prādeśamātrāḥ pādā aranimātrāṇītarāṇy aṅgāni || 3.12.4 ||
ukta prādeśamātrī bhavatīti tasyā āsandhāḥ prādeśamātrāḥ pādā
dūtararāṣyaṅgāni aratnipramāṇani syuḥ aṅgulaṃ prādeśo’ratnirityetadā-
dīni parimāṇāni siddhāni granthāntare || 4||

dakṣiṇenaudumbarīṃ hṛtvā tasyā uttarato nidadhyuḥ || 3.12.5 ||
tāmāmandīmaudumbayā dakṣiṇena pradeśena hatvā tasyā uttarārddhe
sannikarṣe sthāpayeyuḥ tasyā iti kimartham ucyate, sadohyadhikṛtam
atastasyā iti bravīti tasyā audumbayā uttaratā na sadasaḥ || 5 ||

tām uttareṇodgātā gatvā paścād upaviśya bhūmispṛśo 'syāḥ pādān kṛtvā kūrcāv adhastād upohyābhimṛśed bṛhadrathantare te pūrvau pādau śyaitanaudhase aparau vairūpavairāje anūcī śākvararaivate tiraścī ity etaiḥ pṛthag aṅgāny anvartham || 3.12.6 ||

tasthā śrāmandyā uttareṇa pradeśenodgātā gatvā paścime pradeśe upa-
vigya bhūmispṛśaḥ asyā āsandhāḥ pādān kṛtvā kūca tasyā adhastā-
dupohya tānabhimṛśet bṛhadrathantare te pūrvī pādāvityetadādibhi
ryayātritairmantraiḥ pṛthagāsandhaṅgānyanvarthaṃ prayaṅgānetyarthaḥ arthamanu anvarthaṃ
yathāliṅgamityarthaḥ || 6||


vivayanam ālabhyarcaḥ prāñca ātānā yajūṃṣi tiryañcaḥ sāmāny āstāraṇaṃ śrīr upabarhaṇaṃ vākovākyam atīrekā vāravantīyaṃ saṃdhayor ājanam ātmā pratiṣṭhā yajñāyajñīyam iti || 3.12.7 ||
yadi mauja yadi dārbha vivānamālabhya smṛṣṭvā ṛcaḥ prāñca ityetadādinā yathāsūcitaṃ mantra japet || 7 ||

tāṃ mukhenorasā bāhubhyām iti spṛṣṭvārohed vasavas tvā gāyatreṇa chandasā rohantu tān anvadhirohāmi rājyāya rudrās tvā traiṣṭubhena chandasā rohantu tān anvadhirohāmi vairājyāyādityās tvā jāgatena chandasā rohantu tān anvadhirohāmi svārājyāya viśve tvā devā anuṣṭubhena chandasā rohantu tān anvadhirohāmi sāmrājyāyeti || 3.12.8 ||

tāmāsandoṃ mukhenoramā bāḍabhyāñca ssṛṣṭodgātā śrārohet vasava-
svetyetadādibhiryathāsūcitairmantraḥ tāmiti kimartham ucyate, vivā-
namālabhyeti prakṛtam atastāmityāha || 8 ||

athaitair eva devatā udasya rājaśabdaṃ cāmunā tvā chandasā rohāmīty ārohet || 3.12.9 ||

athaśabdo vikalpārthaḥ athāgamapara: vikalpa etaireva mantrairdevatā
utkṣipya padānāmanviṣya rājaśabdañca śramunātvā chandamā rohāmītyā-
rohet prakāramupadiśyate gāyatreṇa vā chandamā rohāmi traiṣṭubhena tvā
chandasā rohāmi jāgatena lā chandamā rohāmi ānuṣṭubhena tvā
chandamā rohāmi iti || 8 ||

vairājapañcamair iti gautamaḥ || 3.12.10 ||

etaiḥ udasya devatārājāśabdaiḥ vairājapañcamairārohet ityevaṃ gautama
ācāryo manyate vairājena vā indamā rohāmītyeva pañcame pra-
yogaḥ || 10 ||

caturbhir iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 3.12.11 ||

eteṣāmevādasya devatārājaśabdānāṃ caturbhiḥ pūrvaiḥ stomairārohe-
dityevaṃ dhānañjapya trācāryo manyate || 11 ||

tribhir iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 3.12.12 ||

eteṣāmeva pūrvaistribhiriti śāṇḍilya trācāryo manyate uktā-
rthānāmeva, nanu śrānantaryārtho trathaśabdaḥ kasmānna bhavati athānantara-
metairudasya devatārājaśabda: ārohediti ucyate, ukta chandobhi-
rāroitīti tadekenaivārohaṇasthārthavattvaṃ kṛtaṃ bhavati atha punaḥ samuccayaḥ
kalpyate tadā pūrveṇa kalpenāruhya punarārohaṇārthamavarohaṇaṃ svāt
tanmābhūdityato vikalpārtho’thaśabdaḥ kalpyate || 12 ||

āruhya japet syonām āsadaṃ sukhadāmāsadaṃ namas te 'stu mā mā hiṃsīr iti || 3.12.13 ||
śrāruhya śrāmandoṃ yonāmāsadamityetaṃ mantraṃ japet || 13 ||

tasminn ārohati kūrcān āroheyuḥ praṣṭī brahmā gṛhapatiḥ || 3.12.14 ||
tasminnudgātari ārohati āmanārthān pūrvakalpitān kūrcāmāroheyuḥ ke praṣṭī pārśvavarttino praṣṭo prastotapratihattarī brahmā gṛhapatitra || 14 ||

āsandyā vyākhyātaṃ dravyaṃ vāṇasya dravyaṃ vāṇasya || 3.12.15 ||
āmandyā vyākhyātam uktaṃ nirdiṣṭaṃ dravyaṃ vāṇasya uktaṃ vāṇaṃ vitantrantīti tat kiṃmayo vāṇo bhavati taducyate aāsanyā vyākhyātaṃ dravyaṃ
vāṇasyeti audumbaro vāṇaḥ syāt udumbarābhāve yajñiyavṛkṣasya ||15||

iti tṛtīyasya dvādaśī kāṇḍakā |
iti tīyaḥ prapāṭhakaḥ samāptaḥ |
