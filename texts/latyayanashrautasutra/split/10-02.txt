śarkarīṣu ṣoḍaśinā stuvīteti gaurīvitaṃ mahānāmnīṣu syād ṛkcodanā hi tatprakaraṇe || 10.2.1 ||
mahānāmnyas tv eva ṣoḍaśisāma syur na hyetāsv anyat sāmāpadyate || 10.2.2 ||
api ca pṛṣṭhastotrīyeṣu parokṣāṇi kurvan naitāsu nityavatsāḥ karoti || 10.2.3 ||
kṛtāś caitasmiṃ sthāne vajrasya || 10.2.4 ||
virāṭsv anuṣṭupsu ca gaurīvitam eva syāt svāraprāyāṇi hi ṣoḍaśisāmāni || 10.2.5 ||
śarkvarīṇāṃ kāmyāni nidhanānīḍāsthāneṣu syur ity ācāryās taddhi bhūyiṣṭham || 10.2.6 ||
ūdhaḥsv eveti gautamadhānañjapyāv atra hi nidhanavādaṃ vadati || 10.2.7 ||
athakārasthāna iti śaucivṛkṣir evam alpīyān vikāraḥ || 10.2.8 ||
aniyamāt sarveṣu nidhanasthāneṣv iti kautsaḥ || 10.2.9 ||
uttamayā stotrīyayā saṃyuktaṃ purīṣam ānantaryād iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 10.2.10 ||
śarkvarībhiḥ stutvety apavarjya purīṣeṇetyāha tasmād asaṃyuktaṃ syāt || 10.2.11 ||
paryāyānte syāt stotrānte vā || 10.2.12 ||
pañcame 'hani rāyovājīyaṃ brahmasāma kurvaṃs tasya sthāne kuryāt yat kalpavṛttenopapannam || 10.2.13 ||
bārhadgiraṃ kuryād araṇye geyaṃ hi sachandasyaṃ ca samāne cāhani dṛṣṭam || 10.2.14 ||
pārthuraśmaṃ kurvan bārhadgirasya kuryād ṛcām asaṃcārāya || 10.2.15 ||
