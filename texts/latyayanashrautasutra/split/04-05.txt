athāto gavāmayanavikalpāḥ || 4.5.1 ||
sarvaṃ jyotiṣṭomenaike || 4.5.2 ||
atirātracaturviṃśanavāhavratātirātrā vā yathāsthānaṃ syuḥ śeṣo jyotiṣṭomena || 4.5.3 ||
atra vā goāyuṣī pṛṣṭhyābhiplavau daśarātram ity upāharet sa saṃvatsarapravarhaḥ || 4.5.4 ||
śaṅkhāhataṃ ca || 4.5.5 ||
nānāhobhir vayaṃ kalpayāmo yathaitad brāhmaṇam || 4.5.6 ||
atirātram ubhayasāmānaṃ kurvann āndhīgavād uttare udalabṛhatī ṣoḍaśimāṃś ced audalasthāne nānadam || 4.5.7 ||
pūrvo bṛhataḥ sāmatṛcaḥ || 4.5.8 ||
caturviṃśam arathantaram eke || 4.5.9 ||
rathantarapṛṣṭhaṃ vā bṛhadanuṣṭupkam || 4.5.10 ||
agniṣṭomasaṃsthaṃ cet triṃśāny ājyāni ṣaṭtriṃśāni pṛṣṭhāni || 4.5.11 ||
ṣaṭtriṃśāni vā hotṛkasāmāni || 4.5.12 ||
pṛṣṭhānāṃ vāṣṭācatvāriṃśāni || 4.5.13 ||
sarvāṇi vā triṃśāni stotrāṇi || 4.5.14 ||
vyatyāsaṃ vā triṇavatrayastriṃśau || 4.5.15 ||
etad antarukthyam || 4.5.16 ||
abhiplavaṃ svarasāmnaś ca jyotiṣṭomatantra eke kalpayanti || 4.5.17 ||
tathā satāṃ śāṭyāyaninaḥ ṣaḍahavibhaktīr anukalpayanti || 4.5.18 ||
dvividham abhiplavam eka upavatyagriyavatī ca vyatyāsaṃ pratipadau rāthantarabārhatāni cājyāni || 4.5.19 ||
ājyapratipadāvauśanakāve antye pārthayāme vā bārhatānāṃ svayonyabhīvartto rathantarapṛṣṭheṣu śyaitarkṣu bṛhatpṛṣṭheṣu || 4.5.20 ||
vyūḍhaṃ pṛṣṭhyam eke || 4.5.21 ||
tasya samūḍhāt prathamasyāhno bahiṣpavamānam || 4.5.22 ||
samūḍham eke rathantarabṛhatpṛṣṭham || 4.5.23 ||
nānāpṛṣṭhe cottare caturahe rathantarabṛhatī vyatyāsaṃ pavamāneṣu bṛhatīṣu rathantaram anuṣṭupsu bṛhate dvitīye || 4.5.24 ||
bṛhaduttamaṃ samūḍhe || 4.5.25 ||
āṣkāraṇidhanāt pūrvaṃ kaulmalabarhiṣam || 4.5.26 ||
āndhīgavācchyāvāśvam || 4.5.27 ||
upottamaṃ samūḍhe || 4.5.28 ||
