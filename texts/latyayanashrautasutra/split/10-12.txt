agnihotropasthānaṃ viṣṇukramāḥ samiṣṭayajūṃṣīti na kriyerann avabhṛthanyaṅgaś ceti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 10.12.1 ||
samidhas tv ādadhyur yās tatra purastād uktāḥ || 10.12.2 ||
sarvaṃ kriyeteti dhānañjapyaḥ || 10.12.3 ||
māsaṃ darśapūrṇamāsābhyām ity āmāvāsyena pūrvapakṣam itaraṃ paurṇamāsena || 10.12.4 ||
āmāvāsye pitṛyajñaḥ sakṛd bhūtvā na syād iti gautamaḥ || 10.12.5 ||
na sakṛc ca na syāditi śāṇḍilyaḥ || 10.12.6 ||
ahar ahaḥ syād iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 10.12.7 ||
māsaṃ sākamedhair iti gṛhamedhīyeneṣṭvā tadaiva śaṣaṃ saṃsthāpayeyur evaṃ triṃśatkṛtva upetā bhavantīti || 10.12.8 ||
dvyahāpavargas tv eṣāṃ prakṛtau pañcadaśakṛtva evābhyasyeyuḥ sa māsaḥ || 10.12.9 ||
agnihotraṃ daśahoteti daśahotāraṃ nāmādhvaryavo 'gnihotropasthānam adhīyate || 10.12.10 ||
ṛtvija uttarair abhipretāḥ || 10.12.11 ||
vaṣaṭkāriṇo 'dhvare || 10.12.12 ||
atsarukaiś camasair bhakṣayeyuḥ kuṇḍapratirūpaiḥ || 10.12.13 ||
hotrā samāsa ukto brāhmaṇena || 10.12.14 ||
svād āsanād upasaṃcāraṃ brahmetare hotre kuryāt || 10.12.15 ||
tathodgātṛprastotārāv āgnīdhraś ca || 10.12.16 ||
gṛhapatir gṛhapatiḥ subrahmaṇyaḥ subrahmaṇya ity asamāsaṃ tayor darśayati || 10.12.17 ||
