saṃbhavati stome 'ntyaṃ sarvatra tṛce || 6.4.2 ||
dvitīyaṃ ced araṇye geyam || 6.4.3 ||
tadabhāve yadantyāt pūrvam || 6.4.4 ||
ekarcasthāneṣv anāpadyamānāni tṛceṣu kuryāt gāyatraṃ triṇidhanaṃ madhyandine madhye nidhanam aiḍam ārbhave || 6.4.5 ||
anādiṣṭatṛcaikarcāny etenoddeśena kalpayet || 6.4.6 ||
kakuby atra madhyandina ekarcas tasyām anyatrottamād vrātyastomāt || 6.4.7 ||
pūrvayor vrātyastomayor ārbhave gāyatram ekarce dhānañjapyas tṛce marutāṃ saṃstobham || 6.4.8 ||
viparotaṃ śāṇḍilyaḥ || 6.4.9 ||
caturṛcānām āvṛttiṣv antyām uddharet || 6.4.10 ||
tṛtīyāṃ sarvasvārasvarasāmnoḥ || 6.4.11 ||
pravām arcantīti dvitīyām || 6.4.12 ||

nārāśaṃsīm anyeṣām || 6.4.14 ||
samavāye tu bhūyasāṃ kalpaḥ || 6.4.15 ||
gṛhapatiṃ vānusattriṇo 'nubhavantīti bhavantīti || 6.4.16 ||
