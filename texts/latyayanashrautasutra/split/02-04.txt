ṛtasya dvārau stha iti pūrveṇa dvārabāhū saṃmṛjya dakṣiṇasya purastād udaṅmukha upaviśed iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 2.4.1 ||
paścāt pratyaṅmukha iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 2.4.2 ||
ukte praveśanopaveśane || 2.4.3 ||
ṛtvikpathena gatvā samastopasthānaprabhṛti samānam || 2.4.4 ||
dakṣiṇārdhe tu sadasaḥ pūrvam upaveśanaṃ prastotuḥ pratyupaveśanaṃ brahmopaveśanena nirastaḥ parāvasur iti dakṣiṇātṛṇṇaṃ nirasyed ā vasoḥ sadane sīdāmīty upaviśet || 2.4.5 ||
bhūr bhuvaḥ svar bṛhaspatir brahmāhaṃ mānuṣa ity uktvaudumbarīṃ gatvopaviśet || 2.4.6 ||
anūpaviśetām itarau || 2.4.7 ||
etenānupūrveṇa sarvaṃ yathāsanaṃ cikīrṣet || 2.4.8 ||
glāyec ced adhyaudambaryeva || 2.4.9 ||
sadasya evam eva dhiṣṇyān upatiṣṭhan brahmāṇam anupraviśed brāhmaṇācchaṃsinaṃ vā || 2.4.10 ||
tenāsya samupahavaḥ syād bhakṣaś ca samānaḥ || 2.4.11 ||
bībhatseyātāṃ cen nānācamasau syātām || 2.4.12 ||
sarpatsu na śūnyaṃ sadaḥ kuryāt || 2.4.13 ||
tasya dadhibhakṣāntaṃ karma || 2.4.14 ||
sa nivarteta satreṣu || 2.4.15 ||
visaṃsthite savane pūrvayā dvārāntareṇa hotur dhiṣṇyaṃ maitrāvaruṇasya ca saṃcaraḥ || 2.4.16 ||
tad yathetaṃ tad yathetaṃ || 2.4.17 ||
