saṃvatsarād ūrdhvaṃ parīṇaṃ nāma sthalī kurukṣetre tasyām agnīn ādhāya yathākālam anvārambhaṇīyayeṣṭvā prasṛjyeteti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 10.19.1 ||
darśapūrṇamāsābhyāṃ yajeteti dhānañjapyaḥ || 10.19.2 ||
sāyaṃ prātarāhutī eva hutvā purastāc ced āhitāgniḥ || 10.19.3 ||
sarasvatīdṛṣadvatyoḥ saṃbhedaṃ prāpyāgneyenāṣṭākapālena yaṣṭum upakrameta || 10.19.4 ||
tatra śamyāprāsanaṃ yathā sārasvateṣu || 10.19.5 ||
na tu prakramāt prakrāmet || 10.19.6 ||
kāmam anekām iṣṭim ekaikenāhnā saṃsthāpayet || 10.19.7 ||
dṛṣadvatyā dakṣiṇena tīreṇeyāt || 10.19.8 ||
tasyāḥ prabhavyamarmaṃ prāpyaitayeṣṭyeṣṭvā triplakṣāvaharaṇaṃ prati yamunām avabhṛtham abhyaveyāt || 10.19.9 ||
yatra kvaca tato dūre yamunā syāt tatraitayeṣṭyeṣṭvā svayaṃ sāma gāyann avabhṛtham abhyaveyād agāyan vā || 10.19.10 ||
pravrajiṣyato 'yanam idaṃ manya iti dhānañjapyas tad eva manuṣyebhyas tirobhavatīti || 10.19.11 ||
na grāmaṃ punar eyād itīva || 10.19.12 ||
svargaṃ lokam ākramate || 10.19.13 ||
vyāvartate śreyān bhavatīti vā || 10.19.14 ||
udakāntardhānād vā yathā sarajasa iti || 10.19.15 ||
