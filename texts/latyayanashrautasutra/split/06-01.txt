saṃtani codyamānam ekaikasyā sarvaṃ stomasaṃbhavāt || 6.1.1 ||
tathā cānyāni svavicchandaḥsu || 6.1.2 ||
saṃtanīti yad vāhiṣṭhoyavat || 6.1.3 ||
triṣu vā tṛceṣv ekarcavat tṛce darśanāt || 6.1.4 ||
sāmnā ca stomasaṃkhyānāt || 6.1.5 ||
tisṛṣv ācāryaḥ smṛteḥ || 6.1.6 ||
yathānyāni sāmāni || 6.1.7 ||
saṃtānāc ca saṃtani || 6.1.8 ||
saṃtataṃ gāyatīti cāha || 6.1.9 ||
ekarceṣu coddhṛtaṃ kalpe || 6.1.10 ||
pramāṇārthaṃ tṛce darśanaṃ stotrīyāyāṃ sāmasamāptiḥ || 6.1.11 ||
tāsu sarvāsu prastāvāḥ syur nyāyapragāthadharmābhyām || 6.1.12 ||
nottarayor ācāryāḥ smṛteḥ || 6.1.13 ||
saṃtataṃ gāyatīti ca bhaktilopadarśanāt || 6.1.14 ||
tasya stotrādāv āvartiṣu prastāvaḥ paryāyādiṣu vā || 6.1.15 ||
sarvatra prathamāyām iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 6.1.16 ||
nidhanabhūtāḥ pratihāravatyo 'prastāvyāḥ || 6.1.17 ||
dhānañjapyena prathamāyāṃ śāṇḍilya uttare tu stotrīye viṣṭāvamukheṣv evāprastāvye viṣṭāveṣu caikiṣu trivṛtstome ca parivartino vidhānam || 6.1.18 ||
uttarayoḥ stotrīyayoś chandasto vipratipattiḥ || 6.1.19 ||
dvipade syātām aprastāvye kakubhau prastāvye || 6.1.20 ||
bṛhatyau śāṇḍilyaḥ kakubhāv aprastāvye || 6.1.21 ||
tathā saty ubhe kakubhau sthaviro gautamaḥ || 6.1.22 ||
bṛhatyau dhānañjapyaḥ || 6.1.23 ||
