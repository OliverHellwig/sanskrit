samidhaṃ prasthānīyām anumantrayeta deva savitar etat te yajñaṃ prāhur bṛhaspataye brahmaṇe tena yajñam ava tena yajñapatiṃ tena mām ava mano jūtir juṣatām ājyasya bṛhaspatir yajñam imaṃ tanotv ariṣṭaṃ yajñaṃ sam imaṃ dadhātu viśve devā sa iha mādayantām ity upāṃśu pratiṣṭhety uccaiḥ || 4.12.1 ||
sarvānumantraṇena vā || 4.12.2 ||
vimuktāsu praṇītāsu samidha ādadhyād yathāvabhṛthād udetya || 4.12.3 ||
etat sarveṣṭīnāṃ brahmatvam || 4.12.4 ||
samānāhany ekāsane na punar yajuṣopaviśed iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 4.12.5 ||
agnyādheyeṣv iṣṭiṣu prathamāyāṃ yajuṣopaviśeta uttamāyā samidha ādadhyād iti gautamaḥ || 4.12.6 ||
pṛthagiṣṭiṣv iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 4.12.7 ||
cātuṣprāśyaṃ ca prāśnatsu dhenuṃ dadyād brahmaṇe 'dhvaryave cāgnipadaṃ dhenūr hotre vatsatarī brahmaṇe 'naḍuho 'dhvaryave tathodgātre sarve trivargāḥ paṣṭhauhīm agnīdhrāya sarvebhyo 'śvarathaṃ kuṇḍale ceti || 4.12.8 ||
evam eva dhenum agnipadaṃ ceti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 4.12.9 ||
dhenum anaḍvāhaṃ vatsatarīṃ vatsataram ajaṃ pūrṇapātraṃ tatprathamāyām iṣṭau dadyāt || 4.12.10 ||
tathottarayor anyatrājapūrṇapātrābhyām iti || 4.12.11 ||
varaḥ saptamo dakṣiṇānāṃ trayodaśo vā pañcaviṃśo vaitad vājasaneyakam || 4.12.12 ||
agnyādheyāntān kurvate pūrṇāhutim akṣābhihomam iṣṭīr iti || 4.12.13 ||
mithunau dakṣiṇānvārambhaṇīyāyām iṣṭau || 4.12.14 ||
vājināśvinagharmāṇām ṛtvikṣapahavam iṣṭvā prāṇabhakṣaṃ bhakṣayet pratyakṣabhakṣaṃ sve yajñe || 4.12.15 ||
yan me retaḥ prasicyate yadā me 'pi gacchati yad vā jāyate punas tena mā śivam āviśa tena mā vājinaṃ kuru tasya te vājapītasyopahata upahūtasya bhakṣayāmīti vājinasya || 4.12.16 ||
ṛtūnāṃ tvā vākṣināṃ vājinaṃ bhakṣyāmīti vā || 4.12.17 ||
ubhābhyāṃ vobhābhyāṃ vā || 4.12.18 ||
