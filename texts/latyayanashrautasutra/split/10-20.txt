saṃvatsaram ahar ahas taureṇa yajeta || 10.20.1 ||
ṣaṣṭhyāṃ caitrīpakṣasya pañcamyāmiti gautamo vratopāyanīyam odanam aśitvā keśaśmaśrūṇi vāpayitvāhataṃ vasanaṃ paridhāya parisamuhyāgnīn paristīryya praṇītāḥ praṇīya mekhalām ābadhya daṇḍaṃ pāṇau kṛtvā paścād gārhapatyasyopaviśya svayaṃ kṛṣṇājinaṃ pratiṣajeta tūṣṇīm || 10.20.2 ||
paścimenāhavanīyaṃ dakṣiṇātikramyaitat kuryāt || 10.20.3 ||
tasya tisra iṣṭayaḥ pūrvāhna ekā pṛthak ca madhyandināparāhṇayoḥ || 10.20.4 ||
satāsu pṛthag anupūrvaṃ havīṃṣi yāny uktāni brāhmaṇena || 10.20.5 ||
ekā vaiva syāt tasyāṃ tāni sarvāṇi syuḥ || 10.20.6 ||
saṃvatsarād ūrdhvam avabhṛtham abhyaveyāt tatra sāmagānaṃ yathā pūrvasmin || 10.20.7 ||
ubhayos tv avabhṛthād udetya yat purastād iṣṭyayanaṃ teneṣṭvā somena yajeta || 10.20.8 ||
yāvanti vā havīṃṣi purastāt tāvadbhir vā paśubhis taddevatyaiḥ || 10.20.9 ||
ṣaṇḍakuṣaṇḍāv abhigarāpagarāv iti hyāheti || 10.20.10 ||
pratyakṣavihitaṃ tv ārṣeyakalpena tatrānumānaṃ na vidyate || 10.20.11 ||
abhigarāpagarāv evopottabhe 'hani syātām || 10.20.12 ||
paurṇamāsīprasavaṃ trisaṃvatsaram || 10.20.13 ||
tasya yāthākāmī dīkṣāṇām || 10.20.14 ||
ṣaṣṭir iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 10.20.15 ||
prathamottamau saṃvatsarau vyatihared ity eke 'bhiplavastomapṛṣṭhyadeśarohārtha iti || 10.20.16 ||
yathāmnāyam evopeyuḥ || 10.20.17 ||
