ādiṣṭā dakṣiṇā brāhmaṇena dūṇāśasya || 8.10.1 ||
etāvanty aṣṭāviṃśatiśatamānānīti yatra syāc caturviṃśatimānānāntāvanty aṣṭāviṃśatiśatāni dadyāt || 8.10.2 ||
rukmo hotur iti niṣkonāmālaṅkāras taṃ dadyāt || 8.10.3 ||
srag udgātur iti hiraṇmayo syāt || 8.10.4 ||
vaiśyastome sadyo dugdhena payasā somaṃ miśraṃ kuryuḥ prātaḥsavane śṛtena madhyandine dadhnā tṛtīyasavane || 8.10.5 ||
tasya dvādaśaśataṃ dakṣiṇāḥ sahasraṃ vā yathārūpāḥ pañcaśāradīye paśava ālabhyante || 8.10.6 ||
tīvrasuti somātipavitaṃ yājayed iti || 8.10.7 ||
ya iṣṭvā pāpīyān syāt sa somātipavita iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 8.10.8 ||
yaḥ somaṃ pītvā chardayeta viricyeta veti dhānañjapyaḥ || 8.10.9 ||
tatra bhakṣāvṛtāś camasān avajighreyur acchāvākastotreṇa stutvā bhakṣayeyur abhakṣayanta ṛtvijaś camasān avajighranti tān acchāvākasya stotre bhakṣayantīti hy āha || 8.10.10 ||
eva sarveṣu savaneṣu || 8.10.11 ||
abhyabhisomān unnayantīti yān avajighreyus teṣv evābhyunnayeyuḥ || 8.10.12 ||
śatam āśiraṃ duhantīti dhenuśataṃ dohayitvā traidhaṃ vibhajya somaṃ miśraṃ kuryur yathā vaiśyastome || 8.10.13 ||
tasya tad eva dhenuśataṃ dakṣiṇās tā u eva dakṣiṇā iti hy āha || 8.10.14 ||
