tvaṃ vāg asi ye naḥ satre anindiṣu dīkṣāyāṃ śrānta āsi te rāddhiṃ tebhyo dundubhe rāddhim asmabhyam āvadeti parāvada dviṣantaṃ ghorāṃ vācaṃ parāvadāthāsmabhyaṃ sumitriyāṃ vācaṃ dundubhe kalyāṇīṃ kīrtim āvadeti parāvada dviṣato vādyaṃ durhārdo ye viṣūkuhothāsmabhyaṃ puṣṭiṃ rāddhiṃ śriyam āvadundubha ity enam etaiḥ pṛthag āhatya bāladhānenānyaṃ vāghnantam anumantryāpa upaspṛśya paścāt tiṣṭhanto 'gnim upatiṣṭheran namas te gāyatrāya yat te puro yat te śiro namas te rathantarāya yat te dakṣiṇato yat te dakṣiṇaḥ pakṣo namas te bṛhate yat te uttarato yat te uttaraḥ pakṣo namas te yajñāyajñīyāya yat te paścād yat te pucchaṃ namas te vāmadevyāya yat te ātmā yat te madhyam ity etaiḥ pṛthag aṅgāny anvartham || 3.11.3 ||
atha samastena tasmai te subhoḥ subhavo bhūyāsma nāmāsi nāma bhūyāsam āvir asyāvir mā kuru savitaḥ pramāsuvayāṃ manuṣyāṇāṃ bhūtau saṃpaśyasi teṣv abhibhūyāsaṃ śriyaṃ mayi dhehīti || 3.11.4 ||
upasthāya sadaḥ praviśeyuḥ || 3.11.5 ||
