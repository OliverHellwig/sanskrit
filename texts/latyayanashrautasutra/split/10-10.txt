chandomadaśāhayor daśamasya trayastriṃśam agniṣṭomasāma syād ity eke trikaṃ sārparājñaṃ daśamapradeśāt || 10.10.1 ||
yathā bhūyasovādaś caturviṃśam iti || 10.10.2 ||
ṛksāmāni tu pradiśyante daśamāt sarvaṃ hy ahar adhikṛtya codyate caturviṃśaḥ || 10.10.3 ||
pratyakṣavikāraś ca yathā bhūyaso vādāt || 10.10.4 ||
brāhmaṇa vihite ca stome kalpapradeśaḥ ṛksāmnāṃ bhavati || 10.10.5 ||
triṃśinaḥ sutyā māsān sarvatra saṃcakṣīran kauṇḍapāyine ca hāviryajñikāṃs tāpaścite ca daikṣopasadān || 10.10.6 ||
dṛtivātavator ayane dvāv ekonau māsau sunuyus trivṛtāv iti gautamaḥ || 10.10.7 ||
trayastriṃśāv iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 10.10.8 ||
ubhau vā triṃśinau syātāṃ yathaitad brāhmaṇam || 10.10.9 ||
tathā saty aṣṭamyāṃ dīkṣitvā pañcadaśadīkṣāḥ kurvīran || 10.10.10 ||
pañcadaśānāṃ daśamasyāṣṭādaṃṣṭrāntāny ukthāni syur ity ācāryaḥ ṣoḍaśyapāyāddhi nivṛttaṃ nārmedham iti || 10.10.11 ||
avacanāt tu nivṛttir na syād yathānyeṣāṃ sāmnām || 10.10.12 ||
vacanaṃ ca kāraṇaṃ nārmedhasya na ṣoḍaśī || 10.10.13 ||
dṛśyate cābhīkṣṇam aṣoḍaśikeṣu || 10.10.14 ||
tasyāyanavicārāḥ pṛṣṭhyenaikaiko māso vartetety eke || 10.10.15 ||
pṛṣṭhyastomeneti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 10.10.16 ||
abhiplavapṛṣṭhyābhyām iti śaucivṛttiḥ || 10.10.17 ||
pṛṣṭhyāhnaikaikeneti śāṇḍilyāyanadhānañjapyau || 10.10.18 ||
jyotiṣṭomeneti lāmakāyanas tasya rathantaraṃ pṛṣṭhaṃ bṛhac ca vyatyāsam || 10.10.19 ||
yathaivārṣeyakalpenoktam iti kṣairakalambhiḥ || 10.10.20 ||
