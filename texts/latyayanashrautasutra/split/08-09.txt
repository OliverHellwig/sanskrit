upahavye devatānāmadheyāni parokṣaṃ brūyuḥ svasthānāsu || 8.9.1 ||
pratyakṣam asvasthānāsu || 8.9.2 ||
devaśabdaṃ sarvatra varjayeyuḥ || 8.9.3 ||
hotā devo mahīmitrasyaty ete hotā yajñe mahīyajñasyeti brūyur indur iti somam || 8.9.4 ||
ādiṣṭā dakṣiṇā brāhmaṇena || 8.9.5 ||
itarebhyaś ca kiṃcid dadyāt || 8.9.6 ||
ṛtapeye tasya dvādaśadokṣopasada iti tisra upasadaḥ || 8.9.7 ||
ghṛtavrato bhavatīti yathotsāhaṃ dīkṣāsu prāśnīyāt || 8.9.8 ||
uttareṇottareṇa kāṇḍenopaitīti pṛthag upasatsv aṅguliparvabhiḥ pratilomaiḥ prāśnīyād iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 8.9.9 ||
ekāṅgulyā pṛthak parvabhiḥ sarvān deśāṃś camasā mitāḥ syus tair anulomair iti śaucivṛkṣiḥ || 8.9.10 ||
pratilomair iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 8.9.11 ||
ṛtam uktvā prasarpantīti prasrapsyataḥ sado bhakṣayiṣyantaś ca deva satyāni brūyur iyaṃ bhūmir asāv āditya iti || 8.9.12 ||
yā devatāś camasair ijyeraṃs tat pītavad bhakṣamantrān ūheyur iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 8.9.13 ||
avikāreṇa tu nārāśasānām || 8.9.14 ||
somacamaso dakṣiṇety abhiṣutasya syāt tasmin hi camasaṃ pramāṇam || 8.9.15 ||
taṃ madhyandine savanamukhīyaiḥ sahonnayeyur juhuyuś cāsya huto deya iti hi brāhmaṇam || 8.9.16 ||
taṃ dakṣiṇāvelāyāṃ pūrveṇāhavanīyam anirharanto veder dakṣiṇena sadohavirdhānāni cārayitvā pūrvayā dvārā sadaḥ praveśayeyuḥ || 8.9.17 ||
saṃsthite 'hany ṛtvigbhyaḥ kiṃcid dadyāt || 8.9.18 ||
