catuṣṭomayoś catuṣparyāyāḥ || 6.8.1 ||
tatra catuṣke dvau prathamāyāṃ paryāyau syātāṃ yadi brahmāyatanīyāṃ kuryāt || 6.8.2 ||
madhyamāyāṃ kṣatrāyatanīyāṃ cet || 6.8.3 ||
tathāṣṭini dvikās tu tasya paryāyāḥ || 6.8.4 ||
pūrvayoḥ pūrva uttarayor uttara evaṃ vihitāv aparāv iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 6.8.5 ||
pūrvayor dvitīyaḥ pṛthakstotrīyāsv itara iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 6.8.6 ||
dvādaśe vyākhyātau prathamottamau madhyamau dvikau pūrvayoḥ pūrva uttarayor uttaraḥ || 6.8.7 ||
ṣoḍaśe catvāro dvādaśaparyāyās teṣāṃ madhyamau sadṛśau || 6.8.8 ||
caturviṃśe catvāraḥ ṣaṭkāḥ paryāyā dviviṣṭāvāḥ || 6.8.9 ||
pṛthaktṛceṣu paryāyāḥ syuḥ || 6.8.10 ||
dvayor dvayor vā vyatyāsam || 6.8.11 ||
yajñāyajñīyarkṣu vā prathamaḥ prathamāsu ca yajñāyajñīyaprathamāyāṃ sākamaśvarkṣu paramadhyamayoś ca dvitīyaḥ pūrvayoś ca madhyamayor hārivarṇarkṣu tairaścyottamāyāṃ ca tṛtīyaḥ pūrveṣāṃ cottamāsu tairaścyarkṣu cāntyaḥ || 6.8.12 ||
upottamo vā dvikaviṣṭāvo 'śvamedhe 'ṣṭādaśād itare || 6.8.13 ||
pañcadaśaparyāyau pūrvāv ekaviṃśasyottarāv iti vottarāv iti vā || 6.8.14 ||
