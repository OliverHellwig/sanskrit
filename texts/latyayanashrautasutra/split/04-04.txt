pañcaviṃśe caturviṃśasyottame paryāye paricarāyām āvaped iti gautamaḥ || 4.4.1 ||
āvāpasthāna iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 4.4.2 ||
prathamasyāvāpasthāna iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 4.4.3 ||
caturviṃśena vā stutvā madhyamām ahiṃkṛtāṃ tṛcasya prastuyāt || 4.4.4 ||
sa āskando nāma || 4.4.5 ||
tan na kuryād iti śāṇḍilyāyanaḥ || 4.4.6 ||
uttamāyāṃ stotrīyāyām astutāyāṃ pādāv upāvaharet sahanidhanena bhūmau pratiṣṭhāpayet || 4.4.7 ||
āhriyamāṇe bhakṣe pratilomair ārohaṇīyair avaruhya japen maho dyauḥ pṛthivī ca na imaṃ yajñaṃ mimikṣatāṃ pipṛtān no bharīmabhir iti || 4.4.8 ||
adha āsīnāḥ śeṣeṇa stuvīran || 4.4.9 ||
udayanīye 'tirātre saṃsthite 'vabhṛtham abhyaveyur anupamajjantaḥ || 4.4.10 ||
pāṇibhis tu saṃdhāveran || 4.4.11 ||
yasyātra patnī syāt pṛṣṭhaṃ tayā vyuddhāvayet || 4.4.12 ||
tān unnetodānayet || 4.4.13 ||
udetya gṛhapatiḥ somopanahanaṃ paridadhīta || 4.4.14 ||
vastrāṇītare || 4.4.15 ||
abhyukṣaṇaprabhṛti samāpayeyuḥ || 4.4.16 ||
yeneto gaccheyur anyena pratyānavreyuḥ || 4.4.17 ||
anūbandhyavapāyāṃ hatāyāṃ dakṣiṇe vedyante keśaśmaśrūṇi vāpayeran || 4.4.18 ||
tasmin saṃsthite prāñca udañco vā gatvā pṛthagudavasānīyābhir iṣṭibhir āhitāgnayo yajñeran || 4.4.19 ||
gṛhapatiṃ yajamānam anāhitāgnaya upāsīran || 4.4.20 ||
taddevatyaiḥ sthālīpākair yajerann iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 4.4.21 ||
saṃvatsaraṃ saṃvaseyuḥ || 4.4.22 ||
dvādaśarātramahorātrau vā || 4.4.23 ||
yāvad vā sunuyur yavad vā sunuyuḥ || 4.4.24 ||
