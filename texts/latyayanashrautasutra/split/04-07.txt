kakubuṣṇihāv uddharati pipīlikamadhyāṁ śyāvāśvavikarṇagaurīvitāndhīgavaudalabhāsāny anuṣṭubhy audalasthāne vā vāṅnidhanaṃ krauñcam araṇye geye tṛcayor vratāt pare || 4.7.1 ||
viśvajiti yajñāyajñīyabṛhatī vyatiharanty eke || 4.7.2 ||
bṛhatpṛṣṭhaś cet tṛtīyāt sāhasrāt prātaḥsavanaṃ kāleyasthāne samantam ābhijitāni pṛṣṭhānīndrakratau śyaitāḥ kakubuṣṇihāv uddharati pipīlikamadhyā anuṣṭupprabhṛti vratāt || 4.7.3 ||
uttare pakṣasyayanavikalpāḥ || 4.7.4 ||
abhiplavapṛṣṭhyān pratilomān upayanty aharāvartakāriṇaḥ || 4.7.5 ||
pṛṣṭhyābhiplavān anulomān māsāvartakāriṇaḥ || 4.7.6 ||
goāyuṣī ca vipariharanti || 4.7.7 ||
uttamaṃ saṃbhāryaṃ kurvan saptamān māsān pṛṣṭhyābhilavān uddharet pañcāyanamāsān kṛtvā daśarātramiśrād abhiplavam uddharret || 4.7.8 ||
tathā sati pūrve pakṣasi vikalpyaḥ || 4.7.9 ||
ṣaṣṭhād abhiplavapṛṣṭhān uddhṛtya caturviṃśād uttarān kuryāt || 4.7.10 ||
samau pakṣau cikīrṣan pañcāyanamāsān kṛtvā daśarātramiśraṃ māsam uddharet pañcadaśau pakṣau vratasyāparimātkaṃ bṛhatpṛṣṭhaṃ viśvajitam || 4.7.11 ||
viśvajita upariṣṭād āyuṣam eka upayanti || 4.7.12 ||
tathā sati goāyuṣor aikāhike bṛhatyau || 4.7.13 ||
daśarātram eka āvartayanti || 4.7.14 ||
tasmād vrataṃ yadā paraṃ vratānte mānasaṃ sāṃvargyajitā gotamāḥ || 4.7.15 ||
