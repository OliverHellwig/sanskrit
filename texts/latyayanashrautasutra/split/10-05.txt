saṃvatsarasaṃmitāsu prathamaṃ dāśarātrikaṃ prāyaṇīyaṃ syād ity eke trivṛd eva kāryam iti hyāheti || 10.5.1 ||
prāyaṇīyaṃ tu codayitvā tasya stomavikāraṃ gṛhṇāti || 10.5.2 ||
dāśarātrikāṇāṃ cāvayavaprayogo na vidyate || 10.5.3 ||
āvṛtte 'bhiplave śārkvaravarṇaṃ syād ity ācāryaḥ sāṃvatsarikasya dravyasyānugrahāyeti || 10.5.4 ||
caturthas tv abhiplavo luptas tena sahāpeyād yathā pṛṣṭhena janitram || 10.5.5 ||
saṃtani pūrvaprayuktam āvṛttisādhutvāt || 10.5.6 ||
yaṇvaṃ tu syād upariṣṭādhyabhiplavānāṃ lopasthānaṃ pūrvasmin pakṣasi tat purastād āvṛttānām || 10.5.7 ||
etenoktam ekaṣaṣṭirātre || 10.5.8 ||
śatarātre 'bhiprayāyam abhiṣuṇvantv abhikrāntyai samānatrābhiṣuṇvanti pratiṣṭhityā iti sa vikalpaḥ syāt || 10.5.9 ||
yāntaḥ sunuyur yathā sārasvateṣu || 10.5.10 ||
yatra tu kvaca || 10.5.11 ||
samānadeśe vā yathānyeṣām ayānta eva || 10.5.12 ||
cakrīvanti sadohavirdhānāni kārayitvāhaḥsaṃsthāsu cakrāṇi viparivartayeyur ity ācāryas tadabhiprayāttadrūpam aprayāṇāṃ ca samānatrābhiṣavaḥ || 10.5.13 ||
ahar ahar etat kuryur iti dhānañjapyaśāṇḍilyau || 10.5.14 ||
abhiplaveṣv eveti gautamaḥ || 10.5.15 ||
trikadrukeṣv ity eke || 10.5.16 ||
uktakāladīkṣā brāhmaṇena gavāmayanasya || 10.5.17 ||
vaiśākhajyaiṣṭhau tu vikalpete caitrīpakṣeṇāṣāḍhaś ca || 10.5.18 ||
ṣaṣṭhyāṃ vā dīkṣitvā saptadaśadīkṣāḥ kurvīran || 10.5.19 ||
etenoktam anādeśe sāṃvatsarikānāṃ sāṃvatsarikāṇām || 10.5.20 ||
