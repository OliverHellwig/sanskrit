rājā rājasūyena yajeta || 9.1.1 ||
phālgunīpakṣasya prathamāyāṃ dīkṣetābhyārohaṇīyāya jyotiṣṭomāya || 9.1.2 ||
tasyaikā dīkṣā dvādaśaśataṃ dakṣiṇāḥ || 9.1.3 ||
saṃvatsarād ūrdhvam abhiṣecanīyena yajeta tasminn eva kāle || 9.1.4 ||
tasya tathaiva dīkṣā || 9.1.5 ||
dve iti gautamaḥ || 9.1.6 ||
tathā saty abhyārohaṇaṇīyasyāpi dve || 9.1.7 ||
ekābhyārohaṇīyasya tisro'bhiṣecanīyasyeti śāṇḍilyas trayodaśyāṃ tv aparapakṣasya dīkṣeta || 9.1.8 ||
tasya dakṣiṇā dvātriśataṃ madhyataḥ kāribhyaḥ sahasrāṇi dadyād ekaikasmai || 9.1.9 ||
ṣoḍaśa prastotṛmaitrāvaruṇapratiprasthātṛbrāhmaṇācchaṃsibhyaḥ || 9.1.10 ||
tā ardhinyaḥ || 9.1.11 ||
aṣṭau pratihartṛpotṛneṣṭrāchāvākebhyas tās tṛtīyinyaḥ || 9.1.12 ||
catvāri subrahmaṇyāgnīdhronnetṛgrāvastudbhyas tāḥ pādinyaḥ || 9.1.13 ||
abalān asya jñātīn savittān āvaheyuḥ || 9.1.14 ||
tāñjinīyād dakṣiṇāvelāyām || 9.1.15 ||
iṣūṃś caibhyo 'syet || 9.1.16 ||
tāṃs ta eva pratyāhareyur vijayasva rājann iti || 9.1.17 ||
teṣāṃ vittasya tṛtīyam ṛtvigbhyo dadyāt || 9.1.18 ||
daśapeye 'nuprasṛptibhyas tṛtīyam || 9.1.19 ||
śeṣaṃ tebhya eva pratyutsṛjet || 9.1.20 ||
grāmān ebhyo 'pi dadyāt || 9.1.21 ||
rājanyās te syus tata ūrdhvam anabhiṣekyāḥ || 9.1.22 ||
teneṣṭvā na sākṣād avabhṛtham abhyaveyāt kṛṣṇaviṣāṇaṃ kṛṣṇājinapādaṃ vāvadadhyāt || 9.1.23 ||
vārāhībhyām ita upānadbhyāṃ vrajet kārṣṇībhyāṃ pratyāvrajet || 9.1.24 ||
