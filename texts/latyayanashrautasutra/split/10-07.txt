bṛhatyā caiva triṣṭubhā caitavyam iti triṣṭubhaṃ caturviṃśe kuryd bṛhatīṃ prathama ābhiplavike || 10.7.1 ||
eṣa viparyāsaḥ sarvasminnayane || 10.7.2 ||
etāni mahānānātvāni || 10.7.3 ||
caturuttaraprayogo bṛhatītriṣṭubhāṃ ca viparyāsaḥ ṣaṣṭha eva māsi syād iti śaucivṛttiḥ saṃśara iva vā eṣa chandasām iti hi tasminn upodya vidadhāti || 10.7.4 ||
sarvatraivety ācāryāś caturuttarāṇi hy anupūrvaṃ prayuñjāno bṛhatīṃ madhya āmanati triṣṭubhā ṣaṣṭhaṃ māsam iti cāha || 10.7.5 ||
bṛhatīkṣayāc ca bṛhatyā vyatyāso nopapadyate || 10.7.6 ||
sā prāyād bṛhatīgrahaṇam iti śaucivṛkṣis triṣṭubheti yathā bhūyaso vādo 'kṣīṇāś ca bṛhatyaḥ || 10.7.7 ||
sātobārhataśeṣo hy aṣṭau bṛhadrathantarakāleyastotrīyāś ca tais triṣṭubho vyatyasyet || 10.7.8 ||
taraṇir it siṣāsatīti kāleyaṃ syād yad ahastasya stotrīye 'bhīvartaḥ || 10.7.9 ||
svarasāmnor vānyatarasmin rathantarastotrīyaṃ kuryāt kṛtā hi tatrānyā rathantarasyaivaṃ vyācakṣata ācāryāḥ  || 10.7.10 ||
viṃśatiśataṃ tv api pragāthā na dhīyate tebhya eva prayuñjīta saṃrodho hi catuḥśate || 10.7.11 ||
tatra tathaiva satobṛhatīḥ kuryāt || 10.7.12 ||
uddharet tu pañcamād ahnaḥ || 10.7.13 ||
śaucivṛkṣeḥ punarviparyāso 'nyāyenākṛtakriyā hi vidhānaṃ kṛtāḥ punar bṛhadrathantarakāleyastotrīyāḥ || 10.7.14 ||
