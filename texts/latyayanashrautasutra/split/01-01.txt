atha vidhyavyapadeśe sarvakratvadhikāraḥ || 1.1.1 ||
mantravidhiś cādigrahaṇena || 1.1.2 ||
uttarādiḥ pūrvāntalakṣaṇam || 1.1.3 ||
ekaśrutividhānān mantrān karmāṇi codgātaiva kuryād anādeśe || 1.1.4 ||
yāvadyajur anādiṣṭāntān mantrān || 1.1.5 ||
ṛcaś cādigrahaṇena || 1.1.6 ||
ṛtvig ārṣeyo 'nūcānaḥ sādhucaraṇo vāgmy anyūnāṅgo 'natiriktāṅgo dvayasataścānatikṛṣṇo 'natiśvetaḥ || 1.1.7 ||
yājyaś ca prathamais tribhir guṇair vyākhyātaḥ || 1.1.8 ||
somapravākam akariṣyan namaḥ somāya rājña ity uktvā pratyācakṣīta || 1.1.9 ||
mahan me voca iti kariṣyan pratimantrayeta || 1.1.10 ||
tan na jahyād aprokte 'pi kariṣyāmīti viditvā || 1.1.11 ||
āvathaḥ sasomapravākāya lavaṇam annaṃ dadhnāhārayet || 1.1.12 ||
prahiṇuyāt prastotṛsubrahmaṇyau purastāt karmabhyaḥ || 1.1.13 ||
prāgudakpravaṇaṃ devayajanaṃ lomaśam avṛkṣaṃ samaṃ || 1.1.14 ||
purastāc cāsyāpaḥ syuḥ || 1.1.15 ||
tadabhāve mahāvṛkṣa udapāno mahāpatho vā || 1.1.16 ||
na cāsya sthalataram adūre syāt || 1.1.17 ||
devayajanamātraṃ ca purastāt || 1.1.18 ||
viparyasyābhicaraṇīyeṣu sthalād anyad devayajanamātrāc ca || 1.1.19 ||
svayaṃ vrajet kraya upavasathye vā || 1.1.20 ||
udaṅṅeva tu gṛhebhyaḥ prathamaṃ devo devam etv iti || 1.1.21 ||
dūraṃ vrajitvā vihāya dauṣkṛtyam iti || 1.1.22 ||
vadvā nāmāsīti panthānam āpadya || 1.1.23 ||
uttaraṃ vedyantam ākramya dakṣiṇam īkṣamāṇaḥ pitaro bhūr iti || 1.1.24 ||
yajurantaraye 'nvāhāraṃ dhānañjapyaḥ || 1.1.25 ||
upekṣaṇaṃ śāṇḍilyaḥ || 1.1.26 ||
