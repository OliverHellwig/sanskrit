tasya vratāni kṣatravṛttiṃ vartayet || 8.12.1 ||
pratyavarohaṇābhivādane jaghanyavrajyāṃ samānaśayyatām iti varjayed avājapeyayājibhiḥ || 8.12.2 ||
yathāvayasaṃ vājapeyayājibhiḥ || 8.12.3 ||
pratyavarohaṇīyeneṣṭvā naitāni vratāni cared iti lāmakāyanaḥ || 8.12.4 ||
tasya vikalpāḥ || 8.12.5 ||
prākṛta eva vājāpeya ekiprabhṛtīn dvyuttarān saptadaśatrayastriṃśāntān stomān kalpayet samayūronīkarṣīty ācakṣate || 8.12.6 ||
tasyopāsmai gāyateti bahiṣpavamānam || 8.12.7 ||
brahmasāmaiva pratyeteneṣṭvā svargaṃ lokamākrameteti dhānañjapyaḥ || 8.12.8 ||
jyotiṣṭomo 'tirātro 'ṣoḍaśiko 'natirātro vā ṣoḍaśimān || 8.12.9 ||
tasya bṛhatsaptadaśaṃ stotraṃ syāt pañcadaśastomaṃ svāsu || 8.12.10 ||
agniṣṭomo vā || 8.12.11 ||
tasyābhīvartī brahmasāmānuṣṭubhi yajñāyajñīyam anukalpayed bṛhadagniṣṭomasāma || 8.12.12 ||
pañcaviṃśaṃ pṛṣṭhānāṃ brahmasāmācchāvākasāma vā caturviṃśam itarat || 8.12.13 ||
etasya vaiva catustriṃśāni pṛṣṭhānyagniṣṭomasāma ca saptadaśamitarat || 8.12.14 ||
naiteṣāṃ kaścana vājapeya iti gautamo yajamānaś caibhir na vājapeyadakṣiṇā dadyād iti dadyād iti || 8.12.15 ||
