alābuvīṇāṃ vakrākapiśīrṣṭyau ca pūrvasyāṃ dvāri bahiḥsadasam || 4.2.1 ||
vakrākapiśīrṣṭyau dundubhiṃ ca pratimantrayeta yā vakrāyāṃ kapiśīrṣyāṃ dundubhau yac ca vādyaṃ ghoṣo yo mahato mahāraṃs tena no rāddhi māvadeti || 4.2.2 ||
mahāvīṇāpi śīlavīṇe cāparasyām antar iti gautamaśāṇḍilyau bahir iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 4.2.3 ||
alābuvīṇāpi śīlavīṇe ca pratimantrayetālābuvīṇe 'pi śīlī ca yaṃ mantram adhijagmatus tenedam upagāyatāṃ te sāmamaheṣyata iti || 4.2.4 ||
paścimenopagātṝn dve dve ekaikā patnī kāṇḍavīṇāṃ pichorāṃ ca vyatyāsaṃ vādayet || 4.2.5 ||
upamukhaṃ pichorāṃ vādanena kāṇḍamayīm || 4.2.6 ||
tā apaghāṭilā ity ācakṣate || 4.2.7 ||
tāḥ pratimantrayeta yāṃ patny apaghāṭilāṃ mṛdukaṃ vādayiṣyati sārātimapavādhatāṃ dviṣantaṃ taijanitvagiti || 4.2.8 ||
sarvam anuvīkṣamāṇo japed ākrandā ulūlayaḥ prakośā yavācaṣṭati sarvā sattrasya sā rāddhis tathedaṃ sāma gīyata iti || 4.2.9 ||
japed eva suparṇo 'smi garutmān premāṃ vācaṃ vadiṣyāmi bahu kariṣyantīṃ bahu kariṣyan svargam ayiṣyantīṃ svargam ayiṣyan mām imān yajamānān iti || 4.2.10 ||
