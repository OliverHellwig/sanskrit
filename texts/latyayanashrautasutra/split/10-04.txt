āvṛttānāṃ pañcāhānāṃ prathamasya pañcāhasya prathamasyāhno mānavādir bṛhatī tajjāmi syāt saṃtaninā || 10.4.1 ||
vaikṛtatvāt tu nivṛttiḥ || 10.4.2 ||
pañcāheṣv abhiplavan na prasajet || 10.4.3 ||
tān pṛṣṭhyastomapañcāhān eke 'nādeśe prakṛtipratyayād viśvajidanvavāyān nānā brahmasāmatvāt pṛṣṭhyataḥ ṣaṣṭhasyālopavacanāt jyotirgaurāyur ity anādeśāt trivṛtā prayanti trivṛtodyantīti cāha || 10.4.4 ||
samās tv ete || 10.4.5 ||
abhiplavapañcāhā eveti gautame 'dhikṛto hi sa satreṣu bhavatītaraś cādṛṣṭaḥ || 10.4.6 ||
abhyāsaś cāsya prākṛtasyāpi kutaḥ pañcāhasya || 10.4.7 ||
jyotirgaurāyurgaurāyuḥ pañcāhaḥ sa dvitīyaḥ sa tṛtīya iti ca parabrāhmaṇam || 10.4.8 ||
eṣa pratnāvārkajambhaṃ vā pratipañcāhaṃ yathā lokabṛhadrathantarāś cet || 10.4.9 ||
āñjanābhyañjanāsu gārhapatya upasatsu sarpīṃṣi phāṇeyuḥ pṛthagdevasurabhibhir yāny uktāni brāhmaṇena || 10.4.10 ||
taiḥ sutyāsūpākaraṇavelāyāṃ savanānāṃ pūrveṇa havirdhāne āsīnā āñjīrann abhyañjīraṃ ca || 10.4.11 ||
bhuktvāgnīdhrīya eveti gautamaḥ || 10.4.12 ||
uktam ānupūrvyaṃ brāhmaṇena || 10.4.13 ||
aharaharetat kuryur iti dhānañjapyaśāṇḍilyau || 10.4.14 ||
sarvastoma eveti gautamaḥ || 10.4.15 ||
pṛṣṭhya ity eke || 10.4.16 ||
