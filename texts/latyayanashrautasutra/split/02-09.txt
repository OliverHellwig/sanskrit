pūrvaṃ nārāśaṃsaṃ bhakṣayitvāhavanīye prastotā pṛṣṭhahomaṃ juhuyād yat pṛṣṭhaṃ syāt tad ādiśyādaḥ pibatu somyaṃ madhv āyur dadhad yajñapatāv avihrutam imaṃ yajñam abhisaṃvasānāhotrās tṛpyantu sumanasyamānāḥ svāheti pūrvam || 2.9.1 ||
svāhākāreṇottarām || 2.9.2 ||
dvaidhaṃ juhuyād iti dhānañjapyo 'daḥ pibatu somyaṃ madhv āyur dadhad yajñapatāv avihrutaṃ svāheti pūrvām imaṃ yajñam ity uttarām || 2.9.3 ||
hotṛkapṛṣṭhebhya iti || 2.9.4 ||
revatīṣu vāravantīyam iti ṣaṣṭhe pṛṣṭhādeśaṃ gautamo mahāvratam iti vrate || 2.9.5 ||
revatyaṃ ca vāravantīyaṃ ceti dhānañjapyo yāvanti pṛṣṭhasthāni syuḥ sarvāṇy eva prasaṃcakṣīta pibatu dadhad iti ca yathārtham ūhet || 2.9.6 ||
bṛhatā rathantareṇa vā stoṣyamāṇaḥ prāk stomayogād daśa vyāhṛtīr manasā dhyāyed airam iti || 2.9.7 ||
rathantara evarcaṃ yas te goṣv iti || 2.9.8 ||
yuktvā stomam etad brūyād iti śāṇḍilya ṛcaṃ pūrvyaṃ vyāhṛtibhya iti || 2.9.9 ||
itaraṃ dhānañjapyam || 2.9.10 ||
rathantare prastūyamāne sarvatra saṃmīlet || 2.9.11 ||
ekaikaṃ manasākṣaraṃ stobhair vācā saṃyuñjyāt prathamasvarair akārair bhakārādibhir akrāntair dvitīyasvarottamaiḥ || 2.9.12 ||
etad yathādhītam ity ācakṣate || 2.9.13 ||
prāk pratihārāc catukṣaraṃ śiṣṭvā tat sarvatra prativīkṣeta || 2.9.14 ||
stutvā varaṃ codayet || 2.9.15 ||
tasyāpṛṣṭhasya sato nivarteran dharmāḥ || 2.9.16 ||
sahastomair iti gautamaḥ || 2.9.17 ||
stobhā evānviyur iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 2.9.18 ||
agniṣṭomasāmasandhisāmnor evāpṛṣṭaṃ stobhed iti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 2.9.19 ||
sarvatrāsvastotrīyaṃ na stobhed iti sthaviro gautamaḥ || 2.9.20 ||
