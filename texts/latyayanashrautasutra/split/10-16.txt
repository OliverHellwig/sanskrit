teṣāṃ paurṇamāsyāṃ goṣṭomaḥ stomo bhavatīti yajanīye 'hani syād ity ācāryaḥ saṃsthite goṣṭome paurṇamāsam iti hi saṃsthānāntād vidadhāti || 10.16.1 ||
te tam āpūryamāṇaṃ te tam apakṣīyamāṇam iti ca kṛtsnavatpakṣābhidhānam || 10.16.2 ||
parvaṇi tv eva syāt teṣāṃ paurṇamāsyāṃ teṣām amāvāsyāyām iti hi pratyakṣeṇa vidadhāti || 10.16.3 ||
karmānantaryamātraṃ tu saṃsthā darśapūrṇamāsayoḥ || 10.16.4 ||
te tam āpūryamāṇaṃ te tam apakṣīyamāṇam iti ca yathā bhūyaso vādaḥ || 10.16.5 ||
saṃsthite goṣṭome tad ahar eva paurṇamāsena haviṣeṣṭvodyuñjīrann iti || 10.16.6 ||
ayajamānās tatraiva tāṃ rātriṃ vaseyur iti gautamaḥ || 10.16.7 ||
udyuñjīrann eveti śāṇḍilyaḥ śvobhūte tu yajeran || 10.16.8 ||
etenāyur vyākhyātaḥ || 10.16.9 ||
tatra dhānañjapyenāmāvāsyāyāṃ sāyandohaṃ dohayeran purā prātaranuvākāt prātardoham || 10.16.10 ||
aticiram evaṃ dugdhaṃ sānnāyyaṃ tiṣṭhed iti śāṇḍilyāyana itarayor evānyatarat kuryur iti || 10.16.11 ||
kauṇḍapāyine bhojanamuktamiṣṭyayaneṣu darśapūrṇamāsadharmāś ca || 10.16.12 ||
candrapramāṇās tv eva parvasu syuḥ || 10.16.13 ||
