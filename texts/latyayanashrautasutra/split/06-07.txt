daśini catuṣko madhye parivartinī paryāyāvabhitaḥ || 6.7.1 ||
udyatyā vā || 6.7.2 ||
udyatīparyāyayor brahmāyatanīyāṃ kurvan prathamāyāṃ catuṣkaṃ kuryāt || 6.7.3 ||
dvādaśe tṛcabhāgāsthāneṣu dvikāviṣṭāvāḥ || 6.7.4 ||
trayodaśatrayoviṃśayoḥ pañcaviṃśe ca madhyamaḥ paryāyo jyāyān ekajyāyaḥsu kanīyān ekakanīyaḥsu || 6.7.5 ||
tathaikānnatriṃśaikatriṃśayoḥ || 6.7.6 ||
rūḍhaparyāyā vā || 6.7.7 ||
teṣāṃ prathame yugmāyuksaṃhāraṃ śāṇḍilyāyanaḥ || 6.7.8 ||
dvitīye śāṇḍilyaḥ || 6.7.9 ||
ubhayor jātisaṃhāraṃ dhānañjapyaḥ || 6.7.10 ||
śāṇḍilyaś ca pañcaviṃśe || 6.7.11 ||
aṣṭādaśaṃ saptadaśa evāvaped iti gautamaḥ || 6.7.12 ||
nyāyavihitaḥ syād iti dhānañjapyaḥ || 6.7.13 ||
ekānnaviṃśe parivartinī paryāyāv abhitas trayodaśī madhye || 6.7.14 ||
tasmin prathamāyās trirvacanaṃ tathottamāyāḥ || 6.7.15 ||
pañcadaśa paryāyāv abhito navī madhye trikaviṣṭāv iti dhānajapyaḥ || 6.7.16 ||
śāṇḍilyāyanas tu jātisaṃhāram || 6.7.17 ||
dvātriṃśe tisro viṣṭutīḥ kuryād iti gautamaḥ || 6.7.18 ||
tāsāṃ paryāyakṛtiprayogau catusraś catvāriṃśena vyākhyāto || 6.7.19 ||
apy aharbhāji prathamayaiveti śāṇḍilyaḥ || 6.7.20 ||
